West Power Transmission

China's western region has great potential for hydropower generation. China's hydropower resources rank first in the world, but the installed installed capacity is only 378 million kilowatts, and the development level is far below the world average of 22%. Among them, the Yunnan, Guizhou and Sichuan provinces account for 68% of the country's water resources, but the current development is less than 8%. The state launched the "West-East Power Transmission" strategic project, which not only effectively utilized these water resources, but also provided an opportunity for the development of the western region.

New chapter of West Power Transmission

On April 4, 2005, the No. 1 unit of the 4×3 million kW unit of the Nayong Second Power Plant, which was built by the Guizhou Electric Power Construction No. 1 Engineering Company, was connected to the grid to generate electricity, effectively alleviating the pressure on the power supply of Guizhou Province. This is another great news since June 18, 2003, after the Sichuan-Chongqing power grid and the Central China Power Grid, the main channel of the “West-East Power Transmission”, were docked, the “West-East Power Transmission” was kicked off. In the past two years, the power grids in the west have been continuously improved and connected to the grid. The Yangtze Three Gorges Power Grid has been transported to the East China Power Grid by the Central China Power Grid, effectively alleviating the shortage of voltage in East China and Central China. Guizhou Power Grid played an important role in this.

It is reported that the Nayong Second Power Plant officially started construction on July 30, 2003. It is a key project of the “West-East Power Transmission” and “Yudian Transmission to Guangdong” projects. According to the relevant personnel of Guizhou Power Grid Corporation, No. 1 of Nayong Second Power Plant The unit has been put into operation as planned, which is of great significance for alleviating the voltage use in Guizhou Province, especially in the dry season.

In addition, it is estimated that the investment is about 10 billion yuan. It is located in the ear-ear power plant in Faer Township, Shuicheng County, Guizhou Province. It is one of the second batch of “four fires and four waters” projects in the “West-East Power Transmission” project in Guizhou Province. It is a 4×6 million kilowatt domestic subcritical parameter coal-fired thermal power generating unit, and the project is put into operation in the middle and late 11th Five-Year Plan. This is undoubtedly another good news. The completion of the power plant will effectively increase the power supply capacity of Guizhou Power Grid, which is of great significance for the implementation of the national “West-East Power Transmission” strategic goal.

West-East Power Transmission

With the installation of the 250,000-kilowatt Unit 2 of the expansion project of the Wujiangdu Power Plant on the grid, the planned task of the planned operation of 2.5 million kilowatts in Guizhou Province was completed ahead of schedule. At the same time, the No. 3 generating unit with an installed capacity of 120,000 kilowatts was introduced in advance. At this point, the total installed capacity of Guizhou Power Grid reached 8.68 million kilowatts, and it is expected to exceed 10 million kilowatts this year. The centralized installation of new installed capacity and the large-scale construction and commissioning of the “West-East Power Transmission Project” have made Guizhou’s dream of becoming a strong energy-intensive province in the South China a step by step. The power transmission has not only benefited the surrounding power-receiving provinces, but also effectively promoted the economic development of the electricity-producing areas. It is understood that Guizhou West Power East's "four waters and four fires" power point construction will invest more than 30 billion yuan, with this supporting will drive large-scale coal mine construction investment of more than 10 billion yuan, pulling the construction of transmission channel investment of 17 billion yuan.

According to reports, large-scale power construction projects have achieved three pulls in the development of Guizhou's economy. In the upstream, large-scale power grid construction has driven the development of machinery, transportation, chemicals, building materials, coal and other industries, nurturing and expanding new pillar industries, and driving related industries. In the middle reaches, electricity directly accounts for double-digit annual growth in fiscal revenue and has become an important source of taxation. Downstream, high-quality and high-quality electricity supports the development of the province's industry and agriculture, especially high-energy industries.

Technology investment is effective

Driven by the “West-East Power Transmission”, Guizhou not only earned economic benefits, but also gained a lot of scientific and technological innovations. The concept of environmental protection is deeply rooted in the hearts of the people. The reporter learned that in 2003, Guizhou Electric Power Construction Company's construction of the Nayong Power Plant No. 1, No. 2 Plant, Guiyang Power Plant Technical Transformation Project, Pannan Power Plant and other "West-East Power Transmission" projects, "digital" The construction model is fully implemented in various engineering projects. They adopt intensive control mode to realize the integration of construction management, simulation of design and construction schemes, automatic control of dynamic costs in important links such as budget, material picking, leasing, construction and settlement, and management from intensive to modernization.
