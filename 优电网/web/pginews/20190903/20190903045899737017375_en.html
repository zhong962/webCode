State Grid Heilongjiang Power is fully developed - "Multi-Site Integration" Deep Cooperation Program

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

On August 27th, State Grid Heilongjiang Electric Power Co., Ltd. and China Mobile Communications Group Heilongjiang Province Co., Ltd. negotiated on the "multi-station integration". The two sides adhere to the concept of resource sharing, complementary advantages and mutual benefit, and reach cooperation intentions in 5G base station construction, edge computing, network services, mobile cloud, etc., promote the development of ubiquitous power IoT construction and mobile company 5G business, and jointly create " Digital Dragon River."

As the first batch of “multi-station integration” construction and pilot units of State Grid Electric Power Co., Ltd., State Grid Heilongjiang Electric Power Co., Ltd. is committed to building a sub-power plant + data center station + communication base station's ubiquitous power IoT important infrastructure, on the basis of meeting the needs of the power industry, Committed to providing data products and industry solutions to government departments and enterprises.

　　Beginning in May this year, the State Grid Heilongjiang Electric Power has changed its two 220 kV substations in Haxi, Hadong, and urban and rural areas, using the basic resources such as idle space, channel towers and communication networks in the station to build a data center station, 4G/5G. Base station, Beidou satellite base station.

Data center stations, at this stage only carry out Internet data center hosting, cabinet leasing, bandwidth leasing, etc. Through professional management and operation, we provide customers with product services such as site, computer room environment, equipment resources, and network bandwidth lease. In the future, comprehensive services such as cloud and edge computing will be launched. At present, Dr. Peng has rented 3 data center stations, 77 information equipments, including 70 servers; 3 network equipments; 3 antennas for communication base stations; and 1 Beidou satellite equipment.

The communication base station provides basic resources for operators to build 4G/5G. The 4G/5G macro station and 4G/5G micro station can be built in the substation site resources, and the 5G mobile edge computing server deployment area can also be provided in combination with the data center station products. At present, Unicom has completed the deployment of 4G macro base stations in the Haxi substation and has been put into operation.

　　The Industrial Internet of Things can also benefit from the fact that edge computing nodes will meet fast response events and changing business conditions and requirements, and can solve the complexity problems caused by a large number of heterogeneous devices and networks in the industrial field, and realize multi-dimensional heterogeneous data in the field. Real-time access and reliable transmission, avoiding unnecessary network, storage and computing resource overhead for operators.

Next, “multi-station convergence” will also rapidly expand in the ubiquitous power Internet of Things, 5G industry and digital industry in Heilongjiang Province, forming a typical business model, thereby upgrading the city's information and communication infrastructure faster and better, providing more A digital service for quality discounts.
