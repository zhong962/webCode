State Grid Hubei Power "Wanwu Internet" feels the new experience of smart energy

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

　　“The success rate of electricity information collection is 99.3%, and the line loss of No. 1 section of Wangjing Village is abnormally fluctuating...” On August 23, Mao Yong, director of the Leigong Power Supply Company of Xiaogan Anlu City, Hubei Province, logged in the “Group Perspective Window” The management platform, in just 10 minutes, got a detailed analysis report. The platform conducted automatic query and intelligent analysis on more than 30 sets of business systems and more than 140 assessment indicators, and monitored 11 transaction problems. According to this analysis report, the director of Leigong Power Supply Institute quickly drafted the work orders for the key tasks of the day and carried out special inspections.

　　This year, State Grid Hubei Electric Power Co., Ltd. integrated various professional information systems, opened up information barriers between more than 30 sets of key business systems, and realized inter-disciplinary data integration such as operation, maintenance, and marketing to form a unified data asset. On this basis, the company provided an efficient and fast digital management platform for the power supply, “the team perspective window”, which reduced the power supply service pressure of the grassroots team and improved the response speed of the power supply service. At present, the staff of 1049 grass-roots power supply stations in Hubei can collect real-time data of line-down, current and voltage of the concentrator in the squad through the “group visor window” to actively monitor and discover the power abnormalities and equipment faults on the customer side.

　　In addition to the digital platform, the State Grid Hubei Power Company has made the work more efficient, and it has continued to promote distribution automation to make the power grid more intelligent. At present, State Grid Hubei Power has built a distribution automation main station in 13 cities and cities, and began to apply it in Wuhan, Ezhou, Yichang, Xianning, Xiaogan, Huangshi and other places. In the first half of this year, the company carried out 627 remote control operations, with a remote control success rate of 99.75%.

　　In April, in order to deepen the construction of the ubiquitous power Internet of Things, State Grid Hubei Power set up a distribution Internet of Things demonstration area in Ezhou, from distribution automation full coverage construction, intelligent distribution terminal application, distribution cable intelligent management and power supply service command system. Piloting in areas such as deepening applications.

　　At present, Ezhou Power Supply Company has achieved full coverage of distribution automation in the main city. The company's distribution network fault finding and processing time dropped significantly, the switching operation time was shortened from 33 minutes to 1.5 minutes, the fault isolation time was shortened from 57 minutes to less than 3 minutes, and automatic failure analysis, isolation and creation within 13 seconds were created. The record of the whole process of load transfer. The company's operation and maintenance complaints work orders decreased by 40% year-on-year, and the reliability of distribution network power supply increased to 99.96%.

　　According to the three-year goal of Hubei Electric Power Distribution Automation Construction and Application, by 2020, the distribution of automatic distribution of medium voltage distribution lines in urban power network of Hubei Province will reach 95%, achieving considerable, measurable and fault location of the distribution network backbone. Isolation, part of the area to achieve self-healing. By 2025, State Grid Hubei Power will build a distribution network with a certain self-healing capability, which will automatically isolate the overhead network and underground cable network and automatically restore power.

　　In October, the 7th World Military Games will be held in Wuhan. State Grid Hubei Power will use this power-saving opportunity to integrate PMS2.0 system, power grid geographic information system platform, power supply service command and other business systems, integrate various online monitoring systems, and build coordinated command, comprehensive display, early warning and pre-control. Intelligent interaction, task management and other functions are the core intelligent power supply command platform.
