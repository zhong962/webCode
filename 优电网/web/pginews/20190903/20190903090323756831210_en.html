State Grid won two gold medals in the theme of the National Speech Contest of the 70th Anniversary of the Founding of New China, "The New Generation of the Times - My Growth with the Motherland"

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

　　Recently, in the National Speech Contest of "Times Newcomers - I and the Motherland Grow Together", the company selected the State Grid Hunan Electric Power Luo Li, the State Grid Zhejiang Electric Power Li Jiaqi in the "Great Powers" and "Homeland Feelings" theme competitions Won the gold medal, State Grid Shandong Power Wang Jin, State Grid Zhejiang Power Shi Wenbin, State Grid Zhejiang Power Zou Zhifeng won the silver medal in the theme of “Great Power Heavy Equipment”, “Laboring Dreams” and “Building the Party's Heart”. The company won two gold and three silver, and the results were the best for the participating central enterprises.

　　To celebrate the 70th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, extensively carry out mass publicity and education activities, and deploy according to relevant arrangements of the central government, the Central Propaganda Department, the State Council SASAC, the Central Military Commission Political Work Department, the National Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League Central Committee, the All-China Women's Federation, and the People. The Daily News jointly organized a speech contest entitled "Times for Newcomers - My Growth with the Motherland". The contest covers the theme of green rivers and green hills, the founding of the party, the dreams of labor, the great power of the country, and the feelings of the homeland. It focuses on cultivating new people in the era of national rejuvenation and telling the glory of the party and the country in the 70 years since the founding of the People's Republic of China. Achievements, great changes in the working and life of the ordinary people, and touching stories of the posts that have been struggling for the goals and tasks set by the party's 19th National Congress.

　　At the final of the "Great Powers" theme event hosted by the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission, the two players of the company closely followed the theme of "Great Powers", and combined with their actual work, talked about the greatness of the great power of the country for the development of the national economy and the progress of the times. Achievements, real feelings, and won the praise of the expert judges. Wang Jin, a "big country craftsman", described the struggle of a "big country craftsman" who grew up from a first-line power grid worker to a world record with the title "Guarding the Great Powers to Light up the Lights of Everything", fully demonstrating the new era. The good manners of the employees of state-owned enterprises are advancing, demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system with Chinese characteristics and demonstrating the responsibility of the State Grid to guard the light and create the mission with sweat. With the title of "Mount Everest of the World Power", Luo Li tells the story of a "90s" value of ±800 kV Lushan Converter Station, witnessing the rapid development of UHV technology and leading the world, showing the company as a glorious course. “Great country pillars and pillars”, climbing the peak of science and technology, and inspiring valuable experience and vivid practice with the new era.

　　At the final of the "Homeland Feelings" theme event, the company's contestant Li Jiaqi gave a story about a group of girls under the age of 26 who overcome difficulties and walk at high altitudes with the title "Stunning back, Mulan is three feet from the sky". "We are the daughter who lied to be engaged in clerical work; the bride who put on the tooling on the third day of marriage; the child who gave the child to the lover and took the initiative to take the high altitude", showing the vivid growth of the employees and the family and the Republic. The picture, the love and patriotism of the love of the family, the audience listened to it, causing resonance.

　　At the final of the "Working Dreams" theme event, the company's contestant Shi Wenbin used the theme of "Running, Master A Bin" to show the company's employees staying up late, wind and rain by showing the old objects such as the alarm bells and fuses. Unrestrained, using the actual action to practice the "people's electricity industry for the people" service purpose of a touching story, simple words, sincere emotions, "A Bin master" story touched every audience on the scene.

　　At the final of the "Founding the Party's First Heart" theme event, the company's contestant Zou Zhifeng described the moving story of the Red Boat Service Team from the perspective of a power journalist with the title "When the New Era Red Boat Power Man" It is a simple expression of the firm belief of the Red Boat Power Man’s “lighting the heart with practical actions” and “keeping the mission with one life”.

　　As one of the series of publicity activities to celebrate the 70th anniversary of the founding of New China, the company attaches great importance to the competition, carefully organized, carefully selected, and carefully prepared for the competition. With a good image and wonderful speech, it tells the story of the national network and shows the personal growth of the company's employees. The new era of the same frequency resonance with the development of the country has promoted the spirit of patriotic struggle. The company's gold medalists, Luo Li and Li Jiaqi, will be recommended to participate in the national finals of the "Times of Newcomers - My Growth with the Motherland" speech contest held in Beijing in November.
