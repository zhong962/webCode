The ubiquitous power Internet of Things has received wide attention at the 2019 World Artificial Intelligence Conference

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

From August 29th to 31st, 2019, the 2019 World Artificial Intelligence Conference was held at the Shanghai World Expo Center and the World Expo Exhibition Hall. As one of the first 15 strategic partners of the conference, State Grid Corporation actively organized an innovative application exhibition, hosted a sub-forum of “Pan-in-the-life in the future”, and sponsored the Global Industrial Internet Summit to participate in the conference and show it to the world. The company's investment and application achievements in artificial intelligence technology and ubiquitous power Internet of things integration, as well as new technologies and new ideas for green grid innovation and development.

The National Grid Exhibition Area is located in Hall A117 of Hall H2 and is located on the main road of Hall H2 of Shanghai World Expo Exhibition Hall, covering a total area of ​​180 square meters. There are 14 participating projects in the exhibition area, which are divided into three categories: customer service, smart power protection and energy service. The project is displayed in a touch-and-interactive manner to enhance the sense of form and technology and the audience experience.

Among them, the construction and operation of urban virtual power plants, 3I infrared visual intelligence inspection system and the world's living room - the city's green power and strongest brain and other projects, especially the application of the national grid company in artificial intelligence technology and ubiquitous power Internet of Things integration Results.

The virtual power plant is the first commercial building virtual power plant operation platform at home and abroad. Through the 5G communication technology, neural network computing, big data analysis, edge computing and other artificial intelligence technologies, the distributed resources of urban commercial buildings are multi-dimensional modeling and aggregation. The classification and aggregation of user idle resources and optimal allocation achieve similar effects to conventional power plants. On the basis of ensuring urban energy security, the virtual power plant will build a new format of energy value sharing, build a new engine for green power generation, and serve the city's green and low-carbon development. At present, based on the results of the commercial building virtual power plant in Huangpu District, Shanghai, the company is carrying out the next phase of pilot research and development.

The 3I Infrared Vision Intelligent Inspection System was jointly developed by the company and Fudan University. It is the first time to adopt the AI+ ubiquitous power IoT edge computing and big data technology solutions at home and abroad for the difficulties and pain points of infrared detection of substation equipment. Non-professionals have become the target of infrared experts. The system combines the deep learning algorithm with the power device connection logic knowledge mapping technology to realize the functions of automatic identification of power equipment and automatic diagnosis of infrared defects, and solves the pain points of high power infrared detection technology with high threshold, high workload and low manual detection efficiency. To improve the overall efficiency of infrared defect diagnosis by more than 3 times, the first prototype has been launched.

The world's living room - the greenest power of the city's green power is the company's full experience of "World Expo" and "AsiaInfo Summit" to protect electricity, breakthrough exploration of the use of advanced technologies such as power Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, etc., built the power of the "brain center" —— Panorama Smart Power Supply Support System and Green Power Index Evaluation System. The system is mainly used to improve the reliability of power supply, and it can realize the power supply guarantee from the entire network in Shanghai to the core area of ​​the Expo. The smart power-saving system solves the problem of the human-sea tactics of time and effort in the past power-saving process for a large number of personnel, avoiding the problem that equipment failures cannot be handled in time during the manual inspection interval, and can be processed before the failure occurs through trend analysis. . At present, the smart power protection system has been applied in the power conservation work of the first China International Import Expo, effectively protecting the power supply safety of the Expo.

In addition, the company also focuses on infrared perception, virtual reality, robotics, voice image recognition, big data, cloud computing, 5G communication, etc. through business hall service robots, power transmission and inspection robots, transmission line AI visualization devices and other projects. The application level of power IoT technology in the comprehensive awareness of grid state, efficient processing of multi-source information, and convenient and flexible customer service.

At the beginning of this year, the State Grid Corporation proposed the strategic goal of “three-type, two-network, world-class”, in order to comprehensively promote the construction of “three-type and two-network” and accelerate the creation of a world-class energy Internet enterprise with global competitiveness. The company further strengthened the development of artificial intelligence. New technologies and their applications have achieved remarkable results. State Grid Corporation said that it will continue to make persistent efforts to accelerate the construction of ubiquitous power Internet of Things, closely link upstream and downstream resources and needs, build an energy Internet ecosystem, accelerate adaptation to social forms, build industry ecology, and foster emerging businesses.
