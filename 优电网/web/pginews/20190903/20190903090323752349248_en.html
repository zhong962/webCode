The country's first urban energy Internet built 100% access and consumption of renewable energy in Jiaxing

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

On August 29th, Zhejiang Jiaxing Urban Energy Internet Comprehensive Pilot Demonstration Project passed the acceptance of Zhejiang Energy Bureau, which marked the completion of the country's first urban-level energy Internet demonstration project in Zhejiang. The core demonstration zone realizes 100% access and consumption of renewable energy, and realizes wide open interconnection of clean energy, high-efficiency power grid, low-carbon buildings, smart energy, and green transportation, and realizes green sharing between grid side and consumption side. 

Realize grid-side and consumer-side green sharing 

The Energy Internet, as its name implies, provides a large platform for the sharing of various energy sources. The demonstration project was jointly declared by the State Grid Zhejiang Electric Power Co., Ltd. and the Haining Municipal People's Government in March 2017, and was successfully selected as the first “Internet +” smart energy project of the National Energy Administration. 

According to the relevant person in charge of the State Grid Jiaxing Power Supply Company, the current proportion of new energy supply is growing, testing the capacity of the grid to absorb. In particular, a large number of distributed photovoltaic power sources are connected to the power grid on a large scale, and its intermittent and volatility characteristics bring many challenges to the safe and stable operation of the power grid. Compared with large-scale power plants, distributed photovoltaic power plants are like small workshops, and the active distribution network built by the project is effectively “driving” these unstable distributed power sources to achieve high-density new energy in situ.

 In addition to achieving high-permeability distributed power access, the active distribution network also achieves high power quality and high reliability through active planning, active management, active control, active service, active response on the load side, and active participation on the power side. The goal. 

On the consumption side, the current development of green and low-carbon cities has become a major trend. The city is the main economic activity and energy consumption place for human beings. At present, the city's energy consumption has reached 80% of the global total, generating 70% of energy-related carbon emissions. The construction of urban energy Internet, through the deep integration of Internet and energy technologies, improve the efficiency of terminal energy utilization, promote the rational allocation of urban energy, is providing a green solution for future urban development. Haining has become such a sample of the city.

 During the peak energy demand period, the energy storage equipment is switched from the energy storage state to the energy supply state, the intelligent building adjusts the flexible load, and the cold and heat electricity triple connection facility increases the power supply and cooling capacity of the building... The flow and distribution of energy determine the city's Vitality, this is the area in which the city's integrated energy service platform has become a reality. In the era of the Internet of Everything, the energy demand data of all walks of life and thousands of households, such as rivers and rivers, enter the sea and gather in the city's comprehensive energy platform. 

It is more convenient to use electricity behavior guidance to be more "smart"

The platform uses the smart meter of load sensing function to analyze the electricity usage behavior of various users, provide customized electricity cost diagnosis and energy efficiency assessment services, and guide users' electricity consumption behavior; coordinate various energy-using facilities through intelligent energy efficiency management system. Improve the energy efficiency of factories and buildings, and make urban energy distribution reasonably; in the platform, users can know the location of smart charging piles all over the city with easy operation on the fingertips, which brings full convenience to travel. 

The Jiaxing Urban Energy Internet Integrated Pilot Demonstration Project has broken the “information island” by creating a comprehensive urban energy service platform, realizing full-service data sharing and integration, integrating new energy planning, construction, operation and other one-stop clean energy services. +Building energy-saving service, "one-time non-running" smart energy service, car-pile-network interactive green transportation service, opened up the comprehensive energy industry chain, providing unprecedented green convenience services for all kinds of users.

 It is through the provision of a complete set of systematic solutions such as energy Internet planning and design, integrated energy service management, active distribution network and source network load storage operation control and business model application. The demonstration project has realized the transformation from theoretical research to engineering practice. It provides demonstration significance and popularization and application value for the construction of urban energy Internet in China.
