70 years of growth 1028 times! How strong is China's power?

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

The world's largest grid scale

-291 times +5 times -

In 1949, the power lines in China totaled only 6474 kilometers;

In 2009, China's power grid ranked first in the world.

In 2018, the national power grid 35 kV and above transmission line circuit length of 1.89 million kilometers, not counting the lower voltage level, has been 291 times in 1949 , equivalent to 47 laps around the equator .

According to the CEC data, by the end of 2018, the national trans-regional transmission capacity reached 136.1 million kilowatts, of which the cross-regional transmission capacity of the AC-DC network was 122.81 million kilowatts; the cross-regional point-to-network transmission capacity was 13.34 million kilowatts.

In 1949, the highest voltage rating of China's power grid was 220 kV.

In 2018, China's power grid has a maximum voltage rating of 1,100 kV, ranking first in the world.
