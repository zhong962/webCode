Xinhua News Agency: "Power Tian Road" two-way cumulative transmission of electricity exceeded 9 billion kWh

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xining, August 23 (Reporter Luo Xiaofei) The reporter learned from the State Grid Qinghai Electric Power Company that as of August 22, the two-way cumulative transmission of the Qinghai-Tibet network project, known as the “Power Tianlu”, exceeded 9 billion kWh. It reached 9.14 billion kWh.

　　On October 21, 2018, the professional staff of the State Grid Qinghai Electric Power Maintenance Company conducted a patrol of the Chala DC system equipment at the Qaidam Converter Station. (Image courtesy of the respondent)

　　Known as “Power Tianlu”, the Qinghai-Tibet network project starts from Xining in Qinghai and west to Lhasa in Tibet, with a total length of 2,530 kilometers. The average elevation of the DC section of the Qaidam Converter Station to Lhasa Converter Station is over 4,000 meters. It is the world's highest direct current transmission project and the longest transmission line through permafrost.

　　Li Xiaoming, director of the Qaidam Converter Station of Qinghai Electric Power Maintenance Company of the State Grid, introduced that the “Power Tianlu” Golmud-Lhasa DC project has been continuously upgraded in two-way transmission capacity since the operation in 2011. The transmission power is now available. It was upgraded from 100 MW at the beginning of the project to a maximum of 600 MW.

A series of compensation platforms for the Qaidam converter substation, which was filmed on October 21, 2018. (Image courtesy of the respondent)

　　"Since June 1 this year, Tibet once again began to send surplus hydropower to Qinghai, with a maximum daily delivery capacity of more than 7.8 million kWh. This is the first time that the Chala DC system has been in normalized high-power operation since the project was put into operation. Sending surplus electricity to Tibet," Li Xiaoming said.

　　The theoretical reserves of hydropower resources in Tibet amount to 210 million kWh, which is an important energy connection base for “West-to-East Power Transmission”. However, due to the seasonal load characteristics of Xiafeng’s winter dryness in Tibet Power Grid, severe power shortage in winter and power consumption during summer flood season The contradiction of the narrative is outstanding.

　　On May 26, 2018, the professional staff of State Grid Qinghai Electric Power Maintenance Company strengthened the safety control of the operation site of Qaidam Converter Station. (Image courtesy of the respondent)

　　Li Xiaoming introduced that in recent years, while continuously strengthening the operation and maintenance management level of the Qinghai-Tibet network project, the State Grid Qinghai Electric Power Company has successively promoted the transmission capacity of the Qinghai-Tibet network project line and optimized the operation mode of the power grid through new technologies such as series compensation engineering and camera system. It not only ensures the "storage power delivery" during the flood season, but also "transmission of electricity into Tibet" during the dry season, and improves the safety and stability of the Northwest Power Grid and the Qila DC system, and alleviates the problem of new energy consumption in the Qaidam Basin.
