The Eighteenth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Eighth National People's Congress passed the "Energy Conservation Law of the People's Republic of China"

        Published: 2019-09-03         Tai Zhong small

The Law of the People's Republic of China on Energy Conservation is to promote the whole society to save energy, improve energy efficiency, protect and improve the environment, and promote comprehensive, coordinated and sustainable economic and social development. Adopted at the twenty-eighth meeting of the Standing Committee of the Eighth National People's Congress on November 1, 1997, and implemented on January 1, 1998.

On October 28, 2007, the 30th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Tenth National People's Congress was revised and adopted as of April 1, 2008.
