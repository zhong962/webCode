State Grid Gansu Power uses new technology to help farmers get rid of poverty

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

　　On August 23, in the bridge town of Xihe County, Weinan City, Gansu Province, the villagers praised the new technology applied to the poverty alleviation industry assisted by the State Grid Gansu Electric Power Company.

　　In the Huajiao Garden of Wangshan Village, Daqiao Town, Xihe County, a large number of villagers gathered around the drone to spray pesticides for the first time. Watching the drone hover in the air, hovering in the air, flying fast, flying and evenly spraying on the pepper tree head, the villagers happily praised the new changes brought by technology.

　　“The drone is suitable for operations in complex areas such as deep paddy fields, hills, mountains, and shrubs. It has low altitude, low drift, and can be hovered in the air. There is no need for a dedicated take-off and landing airport. The downward airflow generated by the rotor helps to increase. Logistics for crop penetration. UAVs can be remotely operated remotely, operators avoid the danger of exposure to pesticides and improve the safety of spraying operations,” said Li Hong, Li Hong Law and Innovation Studio, Gansu Power Transmission and Distribution Company. The UAV adopts satellite GPS positioning, which can realize seamless and non-repetitive spraying of drugs. Compared with traditional artificial spraying pesticides, this method saves time and effort. The weight of the drone is 8 kg, the maximum take-off weight is 28 kg, the maximum load is 20 kg, the flight time is 20 minutes, the lithium battery is powered, the 2.4G signal is controlled, and the operating efficiency is 100 mu/hour, which can meet the requirements of spraying pesticides on farmland crops.

　　"I have heard of the use of drones to spray pesticides. I have seen them on TV. I finally saw them today. I really didn't think that poor towns like the town of Daqiao also had the opportunity to use high-tech products to help the development of agriculture." Ma Hongliang, a cadre of Baocun, was filled with emotion.

　　The advantages of human-machine plant protection are “high efficiency”, “good effect”, “adapt to the environment” and “safety for personal safety”. Compared with manual work, the work efficiency is 40 times that of manual labor, which can save 50% of pesticide use and save 90% of water consumption. UAV plant protection drugs are sprayed evenly, saving labor and effort, safety and environmental protection, no mechanical damage to crops, reducing resource costs, speeding up the automation process of plant protection and mechanization, and improving the efficiency of pest control. Gansu Electric Power Co., Ltd. uses employee innovations in the village, and uses drone ground remote control or navigation flight control to achieve spraying operations, which can spray chemicals, seeds, powders, etc. This high-tech UAV technology will help modern agricultural development, realize agricultural industrialization management, promote pesticide reduction, increase efficiency and control, and promote the development of green agriculture.

　　With the continuous development of rural agricultural socialization services, the agricultural application methods have gradually changed from traditional extensive application to mechanized precision application. The production costs of key links such as planting, spraying, weeding and fertilization have become the main production factors. Gansu Electric Power Co., Ltd. introduced the new innovations of the labor model innovation laboratories in the help of the laboratories in the help of the village, so that the majority of farmers reduced production costs, shortened labor time, reduced labor intensity, improved planting efficiency, and the use of drones. The field management of crops in Daqiao Town has realized intelligent “takeoff”.
