State Grid Qinghai Power's first batch of 4 electric business environment experience centers unveiled

        Published: 2019-09-03         Big mean small

　　In order to create a “winning power” benchmark window and establish a “model” for optimizing the business environment, on August 27th, the first batch of four power supply business halls in Qinghai Province, “Electricity Business Environment Experience Center”, was unveiled. On the way to continuously optimize the business environment, the State Grid Qinghai Electric Power Company has taken another practical step.

　　The first batch of “Electricity Business Environment Experience Center” was set up in the four power supply business halls of Chengxi, Xiaoqiao, Haihu and Qiyi Roads under the Qinghai Xining Power Supply Company, and was awarded by the Business Administration of Qinghai Provincial Development and Reform Commission. The experience center integrates intelligence, market, experience, and online and offline. Through the innovation of ideas, mechanisms, and technologies, it provides citizens with better, more convenient, and more personalized services. Into the power business environment experience center, citizens can use the robot, VR, self-service terminal, mobile APP and other information equipment facilities to experience the online power, one card acceptance, reduction link, streamline data, reduce costs, etc. Optimize the business environment work initiative to effectively enhance the perception and acquisition of “acquisition of electricity”.

　　In recent years, Qinghai Power of the State Grid has fully implemented the decision-making arrangements of the Qinghai Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial Government on optimizing the business environment, insisting on customer-centered, and actively focusing on issues such as long-term power-making, long time, high cost, and multiple road runs. Through the innovation of ideas, mechanisms and technologies, we will vigorously promote large and medium-sized enterprises to save effort, save time and save money, and provide a new model of zero-to-door, zero-approval and zero-investment services for small and micro enterprises, and continuously improve customer satisfaction and satisfaction. degree. In the process of building a power supply service brand for the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau, the company also launched the “Two-Year Action Plan for Continuously Optimizing the Business Environment and Improving the Power Supply Service Level”, and implemented “four free, three reductions, two reductions, one run at a time, one The “business improvement measures” have achieved remarkable results such as reduction in power-making links, shortened power-on time, lower power-saving costs, and improved power supply reliability.

　　"For small and micro enterprises, the power-making link has been reduced from the original 8 to 2 links, and the power supply has realized 'one-card acceptance'. The simple business has not run once, and the power-on and power-on time has been compressed on average. 20 days, low-voltage customers receive electricity 'zero investment', this is a real sense of power!" At the scene, Qinghai Times New Energy Technology Co., Ltd. Manager Li enjoys the benefits brought by the change of the power business environment, and sincerely sigh.

　　Recently, State Grid Qinghai Electric Power has also actively promoted the Qinghai Provincial Development and Reform Commission, the Provincial Public Security Department, the Provincial Natural Resources Department, the Provincial Housing and Construction Department, the Provincial Communications Department, and the Provincial Administrative Services Supervision Bureau to jointly issue the Work Plan for Optimizing the Power Access Business Environment. Clear the relevant approval party's responsibilities and time limits for processing, and the approval procedures shall be optimized from “multiple strings and less” to “synchronous acceptance, parallel approval, and time-limited completion”. 

　　Behind the improvement in power acquisition is the embodiment of continuous optimization of the power business environment. In the next step, State Grid Qinghai Power will take the first batch of electric business environment experience center as a pilot, continue to enrich the content of personalized, packaged, interactive and intelligent services, and actively create new highlights for customers' electricity service, and explore the formation with “point and face” It can promote and replicate the pioneering experience, sort out the typical practices, continue to promote the optimization of business environment, continuously improve the convenience and satisfaction of customers' electricity, and strive to build the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau power supply service brand.
