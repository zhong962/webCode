70 years of growth 1028 times! How strong is China's power?

China's electric power does not take the unusual course of the ordinary road...

In the water counter, the star fire lights up

Time flies, rushing that year.

More than 100 years ago, on July 26 , 1882, the first 12-kilowatt unit in Shanghai generated electricity. On the 6.4-kilometer boulevard on the Bund, 15 盏 arc lamps were lit. At this point, China Electric Power was born.

The fire of the stars of electric power civilization began to ignite in this vicissitudes of the Chinese land.

However, the twists and turns experienced during that period have affected the speed of China's power development.

By the end of 1949, China’s power industry had gone through 67 Spring and Autumn Periods.

The total installed capacity of power generation in the country is only 1,846,000 kilowatts.

The annual power generation is 4.31 billion kwh.

They were ranked first in the world 21 and 2 5.

In 1949, the actual electricity consumption was 3.46 billion kwh.

It is only equivalent to the industrial electricity consumption in a new district in Qingdao in the first half of 2018 .

The per capita annual electricity consumption is 7.94 kWh.

It is equivalent to the current electricity consumption of the vertical cabinet air conditioner for 4 hours .

1949 is a watershed. If the old China witnessed the previous life of China's electric power, then the new China after 1949 is the life of China's electric power.

In development, open the counterattack

From 1949 to 2019, in the past 70 years, especially since the reform and opening up, as an important basic industry of the national economy, China's electric power industry has achieved many world firsts, witnessing China's speed and power miracle.

The world's first power generation capacity

—1028 times —

In 1949, China's total installed generating capacity of 1 848.6 thousand kilowatts.

At the end of 2018, China's power generation installed capacity was 189.967 million kilowatts.

It grew by 1028 times and ranked first in the world.

The national installed capacity of power generation has been negligible from 1949 to 100 million kilowatts in 1987, and China has shared 38 years; 

It took eight years to develop from 100 million kilowatts to 200 million kilowatts ;

From 200 million kilowatts to 300 million kilowatts, shortened to 5 years;

From 300 million kilowatts to 400 million kilowatts, it has been shortened to 4 years;

From 400 million kilowatts to 500 million kilowatts, it took only 19 months;

From 500 million kilowatts to 600 million kilowatts, from 600 million kilowatts to 700 million kilowatts, we have spent less than 12 months each !

At this point, China's power generation installed capacity has opened up the growth rate. In 2013, China's power generation installed capacity surpassed the United States and began to rank first in the world.

The world's first national power generation

—1652.9 times —

In 1949, China's annual power generation was only 4.3 billion kWh.

In 2018, it reached 7,111.77 billion kWh, an increase of 1652.9 times, an average annual increase of 11.3%, and the total amount exceeded one quarter of the global power generation (26614.8 TWh) .

In 2011, China’s power generation reached the top spot in the world, and China became the world’s number one power power.

In 2016, China overtook the United States to become the world's largest renewable energy producer.

In 2018, when the world's power generation growth rate was 3.7% , China still led the world with a rapid growth rate of nearly 10% .

△The above picture is the data of 2018

At present, China's power generation has been more than 50% more than the United States , about twice the size of the European Union, equivalent to the sum of the United States, Japan, and Russia.

The world’s largest electricity consumption in the whole society

—1978 times —

In 1949, China’s actual electricity consumption for the whole year was only 3.46 billion kWh;

In 2018, the total electricity consumption of the whole society was 684.9 billion kWh, an increase of 8.5% year-on-year , which was 1978 times that of 1949 .

In 2018, the annual electricity consumption of US end users was 394.6 billion kWh, a year-on-year increase of only 2.1%.

At present, China's industrial electricity is almost equivalent to the total of all OECD countries and is the most powerful proof of the global manufacturing center.

Of course, while the electricity consumption is growing rapidly, the replacement of green energy is not far behind. In 2018, China's total energy replacement capacity in the whole society increased by 23% compared with the previous year. Among them, every 10 degrees of electricity used 3 degrees from clean energy.

Behind the increase in electricity consumption, there is a higher quality power supply guarantee. In 2018, the reliability of power supply in the national grid service area increased by more than N after the decimal point of 99%.
