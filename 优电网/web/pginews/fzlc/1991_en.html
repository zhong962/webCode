The first nuclear power plant designed and built by China

        Published: 2019-08-23         Tai Zhong small

China's first nuclear power plant, the Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant, has built China's power generation to make China one of the few countries in the world to design and build nuclear power plants.




This is the core reactor gold pool of the Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant. The operator is working on refueling nuclear fuel. As the most complicated energy system designed by humans, the nuclear power industry in New China starts from zero here.



Yu Hongfu, the first director of the original Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant of China National Nuclear Corporation: It was a small flat at the beginning, and there was a small wind blowing inside the wind. So a big room, everyone sitting on a small bench, just sitting here, less than 10 minutes, I heard the wooden floor slamming, everyone was crippling because it couldn't stand the freezing.

At the end of 1982, the central government officially approved the Qinshan nuclear power plant to start, starting from zero, which must overcome the problem of lack of talent, technology and equipment. At first, the experts finally completed the design of the Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant by remembering several foreign nuclear power plant drawings. A nuclear power plant consists of hundreds of thousands of equipment components, and the quality is not allowed to be wrong. In the past ten years, dozens of domestic units have carried out scientific research and experiments, laying the foundation for the first nuclear power plant.



On March 20, 1985, the first 300,000 KW pressurized water reactor nuclear power plant designed by China was officially opened. At 0:15 on December 15, 1991, under the order of the grid connection, the operator of Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant pressed the start button, and the new nuclear power was merged into the East China Power Grid. The history of nuclear power in China was over.

Yu Hongfu, the first director of the original Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant of China National Nuclear Corporation: This is a breakthrough of zero. How many years of hard work, everyone hopes that this day will eventually be realized. Laughing, moving, excited, tears fell. From small to large, from 1 to now it is 9 reactors. This road in Qinshan is too difficult, but it is ultimately successful.

From 300,000 kilowatts to the completion of a million kilowatts, the annual power generation has also increased from 1.5 billion to 50 billion degrees. The Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant, the first nuclear power plant in New China, has accumulated more than 560 billion kWh of electricity, making it the largest nuclear power base in the country. From one to many, with the Qinshan Nuclear Power Plant as the starting point, the Daya Bay Nuclear Power Station, Jiangsu Tianwan, Zhejiang Sanmen, Fujian Fuqing and other nuclear power plants have been built.

As of the first half of 2019, China's mainland has 46 nuclear power generating units, ranking third in the world. It is estimated that in 2020, the nuclear power plant will reach 58 million kilowatts.

From the glory of the country to the national business card, in 1991, 15 days after the Qinshan nuclear power plant was connected to the grid, Pakistan copied and introduced China's nuclear power unit. In 2015, China’s nuclear power brand, Hualong No.1, with its own independent intellectual property rights, landed overseas for the first time.
