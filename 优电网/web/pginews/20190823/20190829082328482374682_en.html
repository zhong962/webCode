The performance of the reading robot increased by 8 times, saving the grid system and saving money. Intel accelerated the AI ​​reasoning details.

        Published: 2019-08-11         Big mean small

On July 13th, the Intel AIDC Developers Conference was held in Shenzhen on Thursday. In the morning, Intel demonstrated an array of AI products around hardware, software and ecological activities. (Intel AIDC Show Muscle: Show AI Software & Hardware + Eco Panorama)

The afternoon session focused more on hands-on demonstrations and case-sharing. Two members of the Intel Artificial Intelligence Builder Program, Jianpei Technology and Setters, shared their details of collaboration between medical imaging analysis, grid and transportation, and Intel.

Jianpei Technology is engaged in medical image analysis and has many deployments in primary care and physical examination. Setters is a software-defined communication solution provider. At present, it mainly applies AI to the national network to prevent electric shock and maritime monitoring.

During the period, Zhiwu and a small number of media exchanged in-depth exchanges with Cheng Guohua, chairman of Jianpei Technology, and Cai Xuyang, architect of Information Technology Solutions, to understand how Intel software and hardware products helped them improve their AI reasoning performance and revealed the next step. The direction of cooperation optimization.

First, Jian Pei reading robot: accuracy rate shoulder radiologist

According to Cheng Guohua, chairman of Jianpei Technology, medical imaging runs through the entire medical treatment process, and 80% of medical data in China is image data. For most hospitals, the storage of image data is costly and burdensome, and the computational problem needs to be solved urgently.

As a pioneer in the domestic smart medical solution, Jian Pei proposed an "one accelerator + 1 network + 1 doctor" program and provided financial level 4 security protection.

Due to the complexity of AI reasoning in medical imaging, it is necessary to overcome data diversification, in-depth analysis, complex markup, etc., and support 3D or even 4D deep neural network architecture, highly dependent on platform memory.

The “Medical Doctor” reading robot created by Jianpei Technology is constructed with advanced AI algorithm and deep learning technology, which can make qualitative and quantitative intelligent diagnosis of medical imaging diseases, which can be applied to clinical decision-making systems and shortened by radiologists. The time of diagnosis.

As early as 2016, this reading robot achieved a 1:1 record in the “human-machine war” of the world's first medical imaging robot and radiologist, and its efficiency was much higher than that of labor.

It is reported that Jianpei Technology has used more than 200 hospital customers to use its reading robots. Each customer can diagnose 80 medical records per day and provide more than 20,000 tuberculosis screenings every day.

As a veteran of smart medical care, Cheng Guohua also talked about his changes in the demand for computing power in the medical industry. In the past, medical platforms were more dependent on the cloud. Nowadays, the demand for primary medical institutions is increasing, and low-cost, fast-deployed edge computing becomes a trend.

Second, Intel boosts image analysis performance by 8 times

Before working with Intel, Jianpei also tested on other platforms, but experienced more or less unsuitable problems.

For example, when the reading robot was preparing for the CCTV program, Jianpei originally designed 16 GPU solutions, but it takes at least 2 months to buy so many GPUs, and the schedule of CCTV programs is approaching, which makes it necessary for Jianpei to find another A solution.

In the process of trying to apply, Jianpei successively discovered that there are various situations in other programs, and they often have to give up some other performance to meet the demand for speed. And Intel processor 384GB of ultra-high memory, fully meet their requirements for high-throughput, 3D computing.

In order to better support medical image big data and accelerate the imaging analysis and diagnosis supporting AI, Jianpei Technology will migrate the reading robot to Intel scalable processor.

Taking into account the high security requirements of medical data, Intel directly integrates the server into the training environment of Jianpei, completely without passing the cloud, and strictly guarantees the privacy of data.

In terms of hardware, the "Doctor" doctor uses the Intel Xeon Gold 6140 processor.

In terms of software, Dr. Yan uses Intel's optimized version of TensorFlow and Intel's MKL-DNN acceleration library to greatly improve diagnostic efficiency and accuracy, and use it with hardware.

Intel has made more than 5,000 optimizations for TensorFlow. The current generation of instruction sets supports AVX512, and the latest generation of Cascade Lake's instruction set name has been changed to AVX512-VNNI. The instruction set has been further expanded to include a variable neural network instruction set.

Now with a variable VNNI, you can perform tasks with the previous three commands with just one command, bringing the speed of the operation from the bottom.

As shown in the figure, through the cooperation of the two parties, the performance of medical digital imaging and communication (DICOM) image analysis is improved by 8 times, and the performance of medical segmentation analysis is improved by 6 times.

This is due to Intel's optimization of the algorithm.

At first, Intel and Jianpei based on the semantic segmentation model UNet for configuration optimization, its accuracy has been improved, but when they try to use some different methods, such as increasing memory, changing the parameters of the framework, the increase is not obvious.

In the second experiment, they changed their minds and chose to use the convolutional neural network (Faster R-CNN) as a breakthrough. Intel's optimized version of TensorFlow fully supports the Faster R-CNN, and the optimization results are significantly improved with an accuracy of 90%.

This year, the upgrade of Intel and Jianpei's new round of cooperation is first reflected in the hardware level. Last year's cooperation was based on the first generation of Xeon's scalable processor Gold Edition 6140. This year will be a new level, using the second. On behalf of the strong scalable processor Platinum Edition 8260, to create a new generation of doctors reading robot.

In addition, Cheng Guohua revealed that he hopes to have more software-level cooperation with Intel, such as considering the introduction of the OpenVINO toolkit.

Third, Setters smart grid and transportation system: mature applications have landed

The AI ​​reasoning enhancement brought by the OpenVINO toolkit has been specifically demonstrated in the AI ​​application of Setters Technology. The AI ​​application between Setters and Intel is specifically in the power grid and transportation fields, and has mature applications.

In the field of power grids, lakers have frequent electric shocks, and warning signs often do not work. According to reports, according to the statistics of relevant data, there are more than 100 accidents caused by fishing electric shock every year, accounting for about 1/3 of the total electric shock accidents.

According to Cai Xuyang, Architect's Information Technology Solution Architect, in order to reduce such incidents, Setters built a grid fishing monitoring system. This system has been deployed at a certain city level since April this year, and has handled more than 280 incidents. So far, no electric shock has occurred.

In the transportation field, ship traffic tends to be saturated. In order to reduce the workload of maritime duty personnel and effectively improve the accuracy of ship statistics, Setters and a national maritime bureau jointly developed a maritime intelligent ship statistics system.

The system mainly realizes three types of functions. One is to automatically count the number of passing ships on the surface of the river, the second is the normative statistics of ship behavior, and the third is the full range tracking test of dangerous chemicals. In addition, the system also provides operation and maintenance functions to detect video quality, enabling operation around the clock without manual intervention.

The basic structure and processing flow of these two systems are similar, consisting of a front-end smart camera, a central analysis platform, a desktop and mobile display, and an audible and visual alarm device.

Firstly, image preprocessing is performed on the edge device, and then the depth learning algorithm is used for target detection. After the target is detected, the image is sent to the central analysis platform through the network. The platform uses the deep learning algorithm to detect, track and track the target in detail. Analysis to determine whether there is a violation of the person or the ship.

Fourth, based on Intel's OpenVINO three optimization

Intel's optimization of the Setters intelligent system is mainly reflected in three aspects: smart camera, cloud edge collaboration and algorithm optimization.

The optimized speed and target recognition accuracy of the optimized system are greatly improved compared with the previous methods. The calculation speed of deep learning target detection is 26.1 times higher than the general version.

According to another report, Intel and Setters plan to focus on selecting a more suitable topology network, provided that it is supported on Intel Optimized Caffe, and OpenVINO introduces INT8 technology to improve overall performance without loss of accuracy. .

1, hardware optimization: smart camera

The system of Setters has undergone three iterations.

The first generation of the program uses a non-intelligent camera. The cost of the device is very low, but this means that all video data is sent to the central analysis platform for processing. The network transmission requirements are very high, and it consumes a lot of platform computing power. The pressure on the central server is very large.

The second-generation solution switched to a front-end smart camera with a GPU. This solution will greatly increase the speed, but at the same time it will also cause a significant increase in cost. The cost of a camera exceeds 8,000 yuan.

In the third generation, the system of Intel's system uses Intel architecture front-end smart camera and optimized with Intel OpenVINO toolkit, which saves the camera's procurement cost. The cost of a camera is only two or three thousand yuan.

At the same time, the intelligent algorithm distributed deployment scheme is used to reduce the dependence on the network transmission and reduce the computational pressure of the central analysis platform.

Previously, a server could only detect 8 channels of video, and now one server can detect 20 or even 30 channels of video. The original 100-channel video needs to deploy twenty or thirty central servers. Now it only needs to deploy five central servers. The price of one central server is about 50,000-60,000 yuan, and about 10-600,000 yuan is saved by saving 10 central servers. In this way, the procurement cost of many server hardware is saved.

The new solution also has an improvement in detection speed. The processing speed of the second-generation GPU solution is 9 frames per second, while the third-generation solution increases the speed to 12 frames per second, which will increase the detection speed of the GPU by 1.4 compared with the second-generation solution. Times.

2. Deployment plan optimization: cloud side collaboration

In the past, Setters focused on deploying hardware acceleration resources on servers in the central analytics platform, and now uses the “cloud-side collaboration” approach of edge intelligence devices + central servers.

Cloud-side collaboration is limited by the edge computing itself. It usually faces the problem of limited computing power and slow speed of edge devices, and the problem that the edge device hardware platform and software system are not uniform.

Intel OpenVINO can solve the heterogeneous problem effectively. OpenVINO can be deployed in all Intel architecture platforms in one development. It can use Intel's CPU, GPU, VPU, FPGA and other hardware-accelerated acceleration resources to provide different loading for different hardware terminals and increase the entire deep learning model. Generality.

At the same time, OpenVINO also provides improved performance for deep learning algorithm inference performance, and supports heterogeneous processing and asynchronous execution during execution, which can reduce the waiting time due to system resources.

3, algorithm optimization: performance improvement

Intel OpenVINO uses optimized OpenCV and OpenVX, while providing many application examples that can reduce development time.

These libraries all support heterogeneous execution, and once written, they can run on other hardware platforms through heterogeneous interface support.

Cai Xuyang said that the algorithm optimized by Intel OpenVINO has improved the accuracy of the algorithm on the GPU.
