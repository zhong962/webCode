Power generation increased coal price decreased Longyuan Power's first half of the year net profit increased by 955.17%

        Published: 2019-08-23         Big mean small

　　CSI News (Reporter Hu Yu) On the evening of August 27, Changyuan Power (000966) disclosed the semi-annual report for 2019. During the reporting period, the company achieved operating income of 3.403 billion yuan, an increase of 17.74%; net profit attributable to shareholders of listed companies was 255 million yuan, an increase of 955.17%.

　　The main business of Changyuan Power is electric power, heat production and sales. All the electricity produced is imported into Hubei Power Grid, and the heat is mainly supplied to local enterprises and institutions. According to the company, the increase in operating profit in the first half of the year was mainly due to the year-on-year increase in power generation and the year-on-year decrease in coal prices; the increase in sales of heat; the decrease in financial expenses; the increase in power generation led to an increase in non-electrical income; In the first half of 2019, Changyuan Power completed 8.844 billion kWh of power generation, accounting for 6.55% of the first half of Hubei Province's power generation of 135.07 billion kWh, an increase of 1.242 billion kWh, an increase of 16.34%; completed sales of 48.21 million JI. , an increase of 82,800 GJ, an increase of 1.75%.

　　Changyuan Power said that in the first half of the year, the national economy of Hubei Province was generally stable, and the added value of industrial enterprises above designated size was higher than the national average. The demand for electricity consumption in the whole society increased rapidly, and the growth rate was higher than the national average. The electricity market grew strongly. Strong power demand is conducive to the company's power business development and growth. The company expects that demand for electricity in Hubei is expected to maintain medium-speed growth in the second half of the year.
