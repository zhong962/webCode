Brazil's beautiful mountain UHV phase II project achieved substantial breakthrough

        发布时间：2019-08-24        大中小

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 24 (Reporter Jiang Lin) The reporter learned from the State Grid Corporation on the 24th that the second phase of the UHV DC transmission project in Brazil's Meishan, which was invested, constructed and operated by the company, successfully completed the system commissioning and rated power. The test was run and the key 1.33 times overload test was carried out to achieve a substantial breakthrough.

According to the person in charge of the State Grid Corporation, the rated voltage of the project is ±800 kV, the rated transmission power is 4 million kilowatts, and the total length of the line is 2539 kilometers. It is the largest transmission project in Brazil and an important energy transmission channel in the north and south directions. It was completed and put into operation in November 2019. Upon completion, the clean water and electricity in the Amazon basin in northern Brazil can be transported to the Rio load center in the southeast to meet the electricity needs of the local 22 million people.

As an important achievement of the State-owned Network Corporation in the construction of the “Belt and Road” and a model of cooperation in the infrastructure sector between China and Pakistan, the above-mentioned projects are of great significance for promoting the “going out” of China's UHV technology and equipment and promoting international cooperation in advantageous production capacity.
