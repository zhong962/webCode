China Southern Power Grid achieves world-class breakthrough

        Published: 2019-08-23         Big mean small

　　 “Hybrid DC transmission technology has been verified to be feasible.” On July 18th, the UHV three-terminal hybrid DC innovation platform of China Southern Power Grid verified the feasibility of hybrid DC transmission technology in actual grid operation through DC fault clearing and restart test. This indicates that China Southern Power Grid Co., Ltd. has overcome many technical difficulties such as complete sets of hybrid DC system design. The hybrid DC technology concept has realized a major leap from theory to practice for the first time. It can realize the most basic functions of HVDC transmission system and meet the high standards of power grid. Reliability and dynamic performance requirements, even if there is a short-term fault in the high-voltage transmission line, the system does not need to be shut down, the main equipment such as the converter valve can be cleared, and the grid operation system can still operate normally. This is a leading engineering technology and a breakthrough in the world of DC transmission technology.

　　The hybrid DC technology was first proposed by China Southern Power Grid Corporation. It uses traditional DC technology at the power transmission end and flexible DC technology at the power receiving end. "Flexibility" is relative to the traditional DC transmission technology, which can flexibly and quickly change the size and direction of power transmission according to the needs of the power grid. Hybrid DC technology is not simply superimposing these two technologies. Instead, it fully integrates these two DC transmission technologies with different characteristics into an organic whole through technical means, and tries to avoid technical shortcomings while exerting their respective technological advantages. “If we compare the grid system to a river, we have the ability to quickly handle the blockage and restore the smooth flow of the river in half a second, which can ensure the safety and stability of the grid system operation,” said Zhou Yuebin, a researcher at the DC Research Institute of Nanwang Research Institute.

　　According to reports, the three-terminal hybrid DC innovation platform is the world's first three-terminal hybrid DC innovation platform built by China Southern Power Grid Corporation. It is a ±10.5 kV hybrid three-terminal DC system with a maximum capacity of 66 MW. The delivery end is a conventional converter valve. The two receiving ends are soft and straight converter valves. The test system is designed to create the test conditions for the entire UHV hybrid multi-terminal HVDC project from the power module level to the station level complete physical test verification and control protection strategy full link test and verification.

　　The Kunyulong DC project under construction by China Southern Power Grid Corporation is the world's first UHV hybrid multi-terminal DC transmission project. Through the three-terminal hybrid DC innovation platform, it can more realistically simulate the operation of the main equipment of the verification project, which is beneficial to early exposure and solving various technical problems, and meets the comprehensive inspection and verification of the Kunlulong DC engineering technical solution. Li Yan, a senior technical expert at China Southern Power Grid Corporation, said that in the future, flexible wind direct access to offshore wind power, large-capacity soft straight back-to-back and other new soft and straight projects can be fully verified on the platform, which will lead the DC technology in capturing the commanding heights of DC transmission technology. Development is of great significance.
