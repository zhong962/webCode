The third episode of "Lighting the New Silk Road"

         Published:2019-05-15        大中小

Egypt is going to build a new capital, but it is suffering from power shortage. Once, Egypt was a Chinese teacher in power technology, and it was not easy for them to accept the Chinese plan. However, in the past ten years, China's rapid development has already led China's power technology into the world's leading level. The EETC500 kilovolt transmission line project jointly constructed by China and Egypt is an important achievement in the capacity building cooperation between the two countries in the construction of "one belt and one road". The project spans the Nile River and delivers steady electricity to Cairo.
