National Grid Service “Belt and Road” Construction – Brazil's Trispils Power Concession Phase II Project Enters Commercial Operation in Advance

        Published:2019-08-24        大中小

On January 13th, Brazil local time, the second phase of the Brazilian Trispils Power Concession was successfully completed and put into commercial operation in advance. The construction period of the project created a new record for the construction of large-scale power transmission and transformation projects in Brazil.

Background introduction

The second phase of the Trispils Power Concession Project is located in Mato Grosso in the north-central part of Brazil. It is a supporting project for hydropower development in the Trispils River Basin and a key project for the Central and South-East Network of the Brazilian Power Grid, including C and O. Bid sections. The scope of the project includes 1272 kilometers of new transmission lines, 5 substations built by new (expanded), passing through more than 20 cities, and the franchise period is 30 years. The project was successfully won by the State Grid Corporation on April 13, 2016, and the O tender was put into operation on June 3, 2018.

On January 13th, Brazil local time, State Grid Co., Ltd. successfully completed the first large-scale green space 500 kV transmission concession project in Brazil, the second phase of the Brazilian Trispils Transmission Concession, which was successfully completed and put into commercial operation. The commissioning date stipulated in the concession agreement is two years, five months and 11 days ahead of schedule. The 31-month construction period has created a new record for the construction of large-scale power transmission and transformation projects in Brazil, and has written a new legend in the history of electric power construction in Brazil. The project has become a classic demonstration of the win-win cooperation between China and Pakistan, the integration of the people and the general public. The work.

Scientific planning, excellence,

Display the image of an efficient professional international leader

Through in-depth research, the State Grid Corporation scientifically judged the Brazilian market and firmly grasped the investment opportunity. In the context of the Brazilian Electricity Regulatory Bureau's two consecutive improvements in project returns, it successfully won the bid for Trispils Power Transmission in April 2016 at zero discount rate. The second phase of the concession.

After winning the bid, State Grid Corporation promoted the project environmental assessment land acquisition work efficiently and professionally, submitted 20 volumes of environmental investigation report and environmental impact diagnosis and evaluation report exceeding 50G capacity, and proposed 20 related issues such as geographical environmental protection, animal and plant protection and malaria prevention and control. The plan, in early May 2017, obtained an environmental protection construction permit, which laid a solid foundation for the final success of the project.

The differences in social and natural environments have caused the difficulties and challenges faced by State Grid Corporation in the construction of greenfield transmission projects in Brazil. The front and rear teams of State Grid Corporation are based on reality, calm analysis, combined with the facts, and improve the program. On the one hand, the company's overall technical advantages, capital advantages, and professional advantages are brought together, scientific optimization, interlocking, careful deployment, and precise docking to ensure To achieve multiple objectives of engineering safety, quality, schedule and economic benefits; on the other hand, to establish a professional management team of China-Pakistan hybrid, with the overall goal of project success, fully communicate and integrate, coordinate and complement each other, effectively overcome cultural differences, Become the "escort formation" of the project success.

While strengthening the coordination and coordination, the project team of the State Grid Corporation pursues excellence and excellence, carefully organizes the construction and construction, continuously optimizes the plan during the 20-month construction period, and rationally increases the construction resources to ensure that the overall progress of the project is controllable and controlled; The effective measures were taken to obtain the environmental protection operation permit on December 20, 2018, which laid a solid foundation for the project to be put into operation in advance. During the construction of the project, more than 1,000 kilometers of transmission lines were completed, and five substation electrical equipments were installed and commissioned. No major quality, personal injury or death and public health accidents occurred. The actual construction period and early commissioning time of the project all created large-scale transmission in Brazil. A new record in project construction, the project will receive additional concession income, which will greatly increase the return on investment of the project.

Environmental protection first, green development,

Show responsibility as a world-class corporate style

Brazil is one of the countries with the most environmental regulations in the world and the first country in the world to fully write environmental content into the Constitution. How to answer the “Questionnaire” of the EIA will directly determine the overall progress and quality of the project. The State Grid Corporation, which has left the country, attaches great importance to fulfilling social responsibilities such as environmental protection and overcomes unimaginable difficulties. While transporting green energy, the project itself is also full of greenery.

The project stretches more than 1,000 kilometers in Brazil, which means that it has to undertake environmental protection obligations along the route of more than 1,000 kilometers, including animal and plant migration, soil and water conservation, cultural relics investigation, protection of Indian and black tribes, residents and Impact analysis of sensitive points and obtaining permission from landowners.

After the project was launched, State Grid Corporation took the opportunity of service and participation in the “Belt and Road” construction, and always displayed the image of responsible central enterprises, integrated the concept of green development into the implementation of the project, and acted as a benefit to benefit the people along the project and become an overseas project for Chinese enterprises. A model and benchmark for green development. During the construction period, State Grid Corporation built and repaired more than 2,000 kilometers of roads and 200 bridges, implemented 51 social environmental protection plans, 200 hectares of natural forest compensation, more than 3,000 species of species, and implemented birds, reptiles, etc. More than 1,000 endangered and rare animal protection rescues and migrations have saved more than 1,500 epiphytic plants such as Orchidaceae, Bromeliaceae, and Cactaceae.

Safe and economical, win-win cooperation,

Write a new chapter in China-Brazil power capacity cooperation

The project operation has played an important role in alleviating the power tension in southeastern Brazil, optimizing the structure of the Brazilian transmission network, and strengthening the security and stability of the Brazilian power grid. At the same time, it has also injected a strong impetus into Brazil's social and economic development and the promotion of local employment.

With a rated capacity of 1.5 million kilowatts, the project delivers electricity from the Trispils hydropower station to the load center in southeastern Brazil, meeting the annual electricity needs of 8 million people. During the construction of the project, more than 4,000 direct jobs were created, and the tax revenue for Brazil was over 80 million reais. It was highly evaluated by the government departments, institutions and the society of the Brazilian Ministry of Energy and Mines, the Electricity Regulatory Bureau, and the National Electric Power Dispatching Center. The project also effectively led the export of China's electrical equipment and engineering construction services such as CLP Equipment, Nanrui Group, Shandong Power Construction and Fujian Power Construction to more than 2 billion yuan, and wrote a new chapter in the cooperation between China and Pakistan's power production capacity.
