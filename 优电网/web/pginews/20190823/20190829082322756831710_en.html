The investment in power grid continues to grow steadily. The investment scale in 2017 is nearly 600 billion.

        Published: 2019-08-23         Big mean small

    During the “Eleventh Five-Year Plan” period, the cumulative investment of the State Grid exceeded 1.2 trillion yuan, and the scale of the domestic power grid has doubled. The power grid industry in China has entered a period of rapid development. The national power industry investment will reach 6.1 trillion yuan, an increase of 88.3% over the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan", including 2.9 trillion yuan in power grid investment, accounting for 48%. During the "13th Five-Year Plan" period, the national power industry investment will reach 7.1 trillion yuan. Compared with the “Twelfth Five-Year Plan”, it increased by 16.4%, of which the grid investment was 3.5 trillion yuan, accounting for 49%. From the actual completion, in 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016, the total investment of the power grid was 368.7 billion yuan, 366.1 billion yuan, 385.6 billion yuan, 411.9 billion yuan, 464 billion yuan and The amount of investment in the power grid has continued to grow steadily and has accelerated in recent years.

    In recent years, in order to achieve power reduction and clean production, reduce power transmission losses, comprehensively optimize the entire process of power production, transportation, and consumption, and promote the development of low-carbon economy, China has proposed a smart grid construction plan. In the 2014 Government Work Report, Premier Li Keqiang clearly stated: “To increase the proportion of non-fossil energy power generation, develop smart grids and distributed energy sources”. China's smart grid is based on the strong grid of the UHV grid as the backbone grid and coordinated development of all levels of power grids. It is supported by the communication information platform and has the characteristics of informationization, automation and interaction, including power generation and transmission of power systems. The six links of substation, power distribution, power consumption and dispatching cover all voltage levels to realize a highly integrated modern power grid with “power flow, information flow and business flow”. The construction of smart grid is divided into the following three stages: The first stage is the planning pilot stage (2009-2010), which is mainly about the intelligent construction of all aspects of the smart grid, and the development of key, basic and shared technologies. Research work, strengthen basic capacity building, conduct technology and application pilots, and fully accumulate practical experience to lay the foundation for the next phase of construction. The second phase is the comprehensive construction phase (2011-2015). A strong national power grid with UHV as the core will be formed. The level of informationization, automation and interaction of the power grid will be significantly improved to meet the requirements of large-scale renewable energy access and transportation. . Intelligent key technologies and equipment have achieved major breakthroughs, smart energy meters are widely used, and grid intelligence has reached a high level. The third stage is to lead the promotion phase (2016-2020), and will basically build a strong smart grid. Intelligent key technologies and equipment are widely used. Communication and information platforms are fully built, and transmission, substation, power distribution, power consumption and dispatching The link is basically fully intelligent, and the national intelligent grid is leading the international intelligence level, effectively promoting the overall improvement of power system operation efficiency, power grid reliability and power quality.

    As can be seen from the above table, the investment in electricity consumption accounts for the highest proportion of smart grid investment, reaching 30.8%. The investment in electricity consumption will be mainly used for the construction of electricity information collection system to realize the grid enterprise and power users. Two-way interaction of energy flow, information flow, and business flow. The construction of the electricity information collection system includes two aspects, one is the accurate measurement of the electricity information, and the other is the transmission of the electricity information. The report pointed out that the biggest problem in the current power consumption link is that "the company's intelligent two-way interactive service platform has not yet been established, and the two-way interactive service with power users has not yet been carried out; the technical support system such as the power information collection system and the intelligent power service system is waiting for Construction and improvement; key technologies such as intelligent power community/building, user-side distributed power supply and energy storage need to be studied in depth; detection and management of intelligent metering devices, and detection of emerging intelligent power devices have not been carried out; The standard system needs to be improved." It can be seen that in the future, there is still much room for improvement in the entire industry in terms of accurate measurement of electricity consumption information and transmission of electricity information. The measurement of electricity consumption information is mainly realized by electric energy meter. At present, the electric energy meter installed in China's power grid includes electromechanical electric energy meter and electronic electric energy meter. The core components of electronic electric energy meter are electric energy metering chip and electric energy metering chip. The application has greatly improved the overall performance of the electronic energy meter compared to the electromechanical energy meter, and has also been expanded in function.

    Specifically, in terms of reliability, the energy metering chip ensures that the accuracy is constant for a long time, without the need for wheel alignment, and is not affected by installation and transportation. Secondly, in terms of accuracy, the energy metering chip has a wide range, a wide power factor, and a sensitive start. And other advantages; again, in terms of functions, the function integration of the energy metering chip can realize centralized meter reading, multi-rate, pre-paid, anti-stealing, two-way metering and other functions; finally, in terms of cost performance, the energy metering chip is cost-effective. At the same time, the extension function can be reserved. Since the electronic energy meter is superior to the traditional electromechanical energy meter in terms of measurement accuracy, reliability, function and cost performance, with the advancement of smart grid construction, the existing electromechanical energy meter will be gradually replaced with an electronic energy meter. . The transmission of electricity information can be realized by means of power line carrier communication, wireless public network or fiber-optic private network. Power line carrier communication refers to the power line as the information transmission medium, and the signal is subjected to carrier modulation technology to realize data between various nodes of the power grid. A communication technology for transmission. The technology relies on the power line network, covers a wide range, does not require rewiring, and has the advantages of low construction and operation costs. It is the preferred technology for power transmission information of smart grids in China. The power line carrier communication product includes a carrier power meter and a concentrator, wherein the core component is a power line carrier communication chip. The power line carrier communication chip enables the two-way communication of the electric appliance on the power line, which is an important basis for realizing the interaction between the grid enterprise and the power user information, and an important means for realizing the demand side management of the intelligent community and the power user.
