Shang Nuclear Overseas Market Project: Harbin Clean Coal-fired Power Plant Project in Dubai, UAE

        Published: 2018-09-06        大中小

This year, in order to actively respond to the national “Belt and Road” construction and BRICS cooperation policies, nuclear power has seized the opportunity to continuously expand overseas markets, and combined with the company's own advantages, targeted new strategies and new models adapted to its own development. . While steadily increasing the domestic market share, it has gradually established long-term international cooperation and established a stable overseas sales network and channels through business contacts and economic and trade cooperation with more than ten countries including Europe, Africa, Central Asia and West Asia. . A number of key projects have received special reports and close attention from CCTV News, Xinhuanet and the Ministry of Commerce.

Background of the project:

Dubai, a city that rises in the desert. The local government launched the Dubai Haxiang clean coal-fired power station project in July 2014 based on the need for energy structure adjustment. Under the national “Belt and Road Initiative” initiative, the Dubai Haxiang project successfully achieved the “going out” strategy of Chinese enterprises and adopted a successful example of the international capacity cooperation model. It will provide Dubai with 20% of its electricity and energy, significantly reducing the cost of electricity for local residents.

The Dubai Haxiang clean coal-fired power station project is a key project of the Dubai government's energy strategic planning. It is the first coal-fired power station project in the Middle East and a key project of the “Belt and Road” that China has been working hard to build. It has realized China's investment, China's financing, China's general contracting, China's design, China's manufacturing, and China's construction. It is a typical case of Chinese enterprises' strong alliance, solidarity and common service overseas. The power station consists of four 600 MW units, and the first unit is scheduled to be put into commercial operation in March 2020.


Focus on overseas:

In 2018, Shang Nuclear Power actively responded to the national “going out” call and implemented the “One Belt, One Road” initiative. It successfully signed the contract for the desalination pipeline fittings of Dubai Haxiang Clean Coal-fired Power Plant Phase I (4X600MW Net) project and Dubai Haxiang. Clean coal-fired power station Phase I (4X600MW Net) project boiler supply water pipe fittings contract. According to the contract requirements, the Haxiang project implements the highest standards in the world and adopts the international project management system standard that is the highest part of the standards of American Standard, European Standard, German Standard and Japanese Standard. At the same time, in view of the fact that the construction area is located in the local nature reserve, the Dubai Haxiang project also has extremely high requirements for environmental protection.

In order to ensure the successful completion of the Dubai Haxiang project, Shang Nuclear Power has set up a working group for the Harbin project, from raw material procurement, equipment manufacturing to quality inspection, clear responsibility, and control at all levels, strictly in accordance with the high standards required by the owners, ensuring timely Delivered, and strive to make the "Shanghe brand" a fine example.
