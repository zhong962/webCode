The first episode of "Lighting the New Silk Road"

         Published: 2019-05-13        大中小

Summary of this episode:

In February 2008, the Philippine State Grid Corporation was established. This is the first time that China has obtained the right to operate overseas state-level transmission grids. China and the Philippines will jointly guarantee the electricity consumption of the Filipino people. At present, China has established 10 transnational transmission channels with neighboring countries such as Russia. Lonely and fast-moving, this ancient Chinese concept has allowed China to move forward with its neighboring countries.
