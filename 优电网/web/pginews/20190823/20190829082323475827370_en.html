Brazil's beautiful mountain sent a second phase of UHV DC project to achieve a substantial breakthrough

        Published: 2019-08-26         Big mean small
        At 0:00 on August 23, 2019, local time, the beautiful mountain II UHV DC transmission project invested, constructed and operated by State Grid Corporation successfully completed system commissioning, rated power operation test and key 1.33 times overload test. Smooth operation, normal equipment status, and substantial breakthroughs.

        The rated voltage of the project is ±800 kV, the rated transmission power is 4 million kilowatts, and the total length of the line is 2539 km. The clean water and electricity in the Amazon basin in northern Brazil is transported to the Rio load center in the southeast to meet the electricity consumption of the local population of 22 million. Demand is the largest transmission project in Brazil and an important energy transmission channel in the north and south. Construction started in August 2017 and is scheduled to be completed and put into operation in November 2019.

        The project is a key project for China's UHV to go out. The State Grid Corporation serves and participates in the important achievements of the “Belt and Road” construction. The model of China-Brazil infrastructure cooperation is to promote China's advanced transmission technology and equipment to go abroad and promote superior production capacity. Cooperation and mutual benefit are of great significance.

        During the preparation and implementation of the heavy load test, the project team carefully organized and completed the pre-test elimination work in time to provide quality assurance for the smooth operation of the heavy load test. One day before the commissioning, in the face of sudden DC line stolen damage accidents, the US Second Project Company timely coordinated the Brazilian national adjustment to arrange new commissioning time. The CEO went to the scene to direct the organization of the EPC contractor to promptly carry out the repair work within 10 days. The work of repairing the 5 base towers and re-launching the wires and cables was completed, and the task of “impossible to complete” was completed, and valuable time was obtained for the timely execution of the heavy load test. During the commissioning period, the project company executives and the CMB team worked overnight for a week, insisted on the scene, accurately formulated the commissioning plan, closely communicated with the Brazilian national standard, coordinated and solved the problems during commissioning, and ensured the high-quality test. carry out.

        Source of information: Brazilian holding company, market development department
