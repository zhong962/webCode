National Development and Reform Commission: National implementation of green buildings, clean heating, clean energy to replace 52 million kilowatts

         Published:2019-05-30        大中小

Original title: National Development and Reform Commission: National implementation of green buildings, clean heating, clean energy alternatives

Recently, the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Science and Technology issued the “Guiding Opinions on Building a Market-Oriented Green Technology Innovation System”, which mentioned:

1. Implement major projects and demonstration projects for urban green building , building energy conservation, clean heating , sponge cities, energy efficient electrical appliances, clean energy replacement , and other technologies.

2. To carry out the selection and promotion of the top ten best energy-saving technologies and the top ten best energy-saving practices (“Double Ten Best”) to promote the promotion and application of outstanding green technology achievements.

3. Carry out the identification of green technology innovation enterprises. We will carry out the “Ten Thousand Thousands” initiative of green technology innovation, and cultivate 10 green technology innovation leading enterprises with an annual output value of more than 50 billion yuan, support 100 enterprises to create a national green enterprise technology center, and identify 1,000 green technology innovation enterprises.

The original opinion is as follows:

Guiding Opinions of the Ministry of Science and Technology of the National Development and Reform Commission on Building a Market-Oriented Green Technology Innovation System

Development and Reformation [2019] 689

Ministry of Education, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, Ministry of Natural Resources, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, Ministry of Commerce, People's Bank of China, State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission, Taxation General Administration, General Administration of Markets, China Banking Regulatory Commission, China Securities Regulatory Commission, National Energy Administration, State Forestry and Forestry Bureau, State Intellectual Property Office, Development and Reform Commissions of various provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities directly under the Central Government, Science and Technology Department (committee, bureau):

Green technology refers to emerging technologies that reduce consumption, reduce pollution, improve ecology, promote ecological civilization, and achieve harmonious coexistence between people and nature, including energy conservation and environmental protection, clean production, clean energy, ecological protection and restoration, urban and rural green infrastructure, and ecological agriculture. In other fields, technologies covering product design, production, consumption, recycling, etc. Green technology innovation is becoming an important emerging field in the new round of industrial revolution and technological competition in the world. With the establishment and improvement of China's green low-carbon cycle development economic system, green technology innovation has increasingly become an important driving force for green development, and has become an important support for fighting pollution prevention and control, promoting ecological civilization construction, and promoting high-quality development. In order to strengthen the guidance of scientific and technological innovation, accelerate the construction of ecological civilization, and promote high-quality development, the following opinions are proposed for the construction of a market-oriented green technology innovation system.

First, the overall requirements

(1) The overall idea.

Fully implement the spirit of the Party's 19th and 19th Central Committees and the 3rd Plenary Session, conscientiously implement the decision-making arrangements of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, adhere to the basic national policy of conserving resources and protecting the environment, and focus on the construction of ecological civilization to solve the problem of resource and environment ecology. The goal is to stimulate the demand of green technology market as a breakthrough, to strengthen the main body of innovation and enhance the vitality of innovation as the core, to optimize the innovation environment as the focus, strengthen the green management of product life cycle, accelerate the construction of enterprises as the main body, deep integration of production, study and research, foundation The green technology innovation system with complete facilities and service system, efficient resource allocation and smooth transformation of results forms a new situation of green technology innovation in research and development, application promotion and industrial development.

(2) Basic principles.

Stick to the green concept. Carry out the concept of harmonious coexistence between man and nature, build a green technology standard system, product life cycle management, green finance and other measures to shape the green technology innovation environment, gather all social forces, focus on reducing consumption, reducing pollution and improving ecological technology. Supply and industrialization provide the basic impetus for the economic and social transformation to a green development mode and lifestyle.

Adhere to market orientation. Respect and grasp the market rules of green technology innovation, and give full play to the decisive role of the market in the field of green technology innovation, technology route selection and innovation resource allocation. Give full play to the main role of enterprises in green technology research and development, results transformation, demonstration application and industrialization, and foster the development of a number of leading enterprises in green technology innovation. We will give full play to the driving role of the company and promote the deep integration and collaborative innovation of the “Industry, Research and Research Institute”.

Adhere to the improvement mechanism. Accelerate the reform of the ecological civilization system and promote the transformation of environmental governance from the end to the whole life cycle management. Accelerate the reform of the science and technology system, innovate the government's management approach to green technology innovation, and further strengthen the service, improve the system and mechanism, improve the return rate of green technology innovation, stimulate the vitality of innovation, and promote the transformation and application of results.

Adhere to open cooperation. Plan green technology innovation with an international perspective, actively participate in global environmental governance, and strengthen international exchange and cooperation on green technology innovation. We will increase the opening up of green technology innovation, actively introduce, digest and absorb international advanced green technologies, promote domestic enterprises to “go global”, and comprehensively enhance the pattern and status of China's green technology innovation.

(3) Main objectives.

By 2022, a market-oriented green technology innovation system will be basically established. The status of corporate green technology innovation has been strengthened, and a number of leading enterprises have emerged. The “industry, research and research” is deeply integrated and synergistic; the green technology innovation guiding mechanism is more perfect, the green technology market is prosperous, and talents, capital, knowledge and other factors are available. The resources are effectively concentrated in the field of green technology innovation, and the factor value is fully reflected; the green technology innovation comprehensive demonstration zone, the green technology engineering research center, the innovation center, etc. form a systematic layout, operate efficiently, and the innovation achievements are constantly emerging and fully transformed and applied; The rule of law, policy, and financing environment for green technology innovation are fully optimized, international cooperation is pragmatic, and the ability to innovate is significantly enhanced.

Second, cultivate and strengthen the main body of green technology innovation

(4) Strengthening the main position of the enterprise's green technology innovation.

Study and formulate standards for the identification of green technology innovation enterprises, and carry out the identification of green technology innovation enterprises. We will carry out the “Ten Thousand Thousands” initiative of green technology innovation, and cultivate 10 green technology innovation leading enterprises with an annual output value of more than 50 billion yuan, support 100 enterprises to create a national green enterprise technology center, and identify 1,000 green technology innovation enterprises. Actively support the “100-dollar” enterprises to undertake key green technology innovation projects deployed by the state and local governments. Study and formulate policies and measures to support certified green technology innovation companies.

Increase support for corporate green technology innovation, non-basic green technology research and development projects supported by financial funds, and market-oriented green technology innovation projects must have enterprise participation, green supported by national major science and technology projects and national key research and development programs. The proportion of technology research and development projects led by enterprises is not less than 55%. (Ministry of Science and Technology, National Development and Reform Commission is responsible for division of responsibilities)

(5) Stimulating the vitality of green technology innovation in universities and research institutes.

Improve the evaluation and incentive mechanism for scientific research personnel, and increase the proportion of the number, quality and economic benefits of green technology innovation and scientific and technological achievements in performance appraisal, scientific research appraisal and professional title appraisal. The green technology inventor or the research and development team is allowed to obtain the technology transfer conversion income in the form of holding equity, dividends, etc., and the scientific research personnel still maintain the right to hold the equity after leaving the post. In the case of technology transfer, licensing or investment in investment, the inventor or R&D team shall receive a net income of not less than 50%. If the cash income obtained by scientific and technological personnel from the transformation of green technology innovations meets the conditions stipulated by the current tax policy, they can enjoy the preferential income tax policy of “salary and salary income” of the scientific and technical personnel in the month. The income from the transformation of green technology innovations obtained by scientific research personnel of universities and research institutes according to law is not subject to the total amount of performance wages of the unit, and is not included in the verification base of the total performance of wages.

Strengthen the cultivation of green technology innovation talents, set up a number of green technology innovation talent training bases in colleges and universities, strengthen the construction of green technology related disciplines, continue to deepen the construction of new engineering in the green field, and actively arrange the cultivation of green technology talents. We will select and use the leading figures in green technology innovation and top-notch talents, select some vocational education institutions to carry out pilot programs in green technology, and guide technical workers to work in green technology and serve green technology innovation. (The Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, and the State Administration of Taxation are responsible for the division of responsibilities)

(6) Advancing the deep integration of “production, study and research”.

Support leading enterprises to integrate universities, research institutes, industrial parks and other forces to establish a green technology innovation consortium with independent legal person status and market operation. Scientific research personnel can participate in technology and give priority to promote scientific research personnel, enterprises, universities and research institutes. "Financial institutions" and other "bundling" to achieve mutual catalysis, mutual penetration and mutual incentives between human capital, technological capital and financial capital.

Encourage and standardize the flow of green technology innovation talents. Science and technology personnel of universities and research institutes shall, according to relevant national policies, work for green technology innovation enterprises to work part-time, leave their jobs, and transform scientific and technological achievements, retain staffing, and normally declare their titles in the original units within three years. Innovative achievements are used as the basis for the evaluation of professional titles; universities and research institutes set up liquidity positions according to relevant national policies, introduce enterprise personnel to engage in scientific research part-time, and are not subject to part-time remuneration restrictions. They can serve as green technology innovation projects or project leaders and form scientific research teams. .

Give play to leading enterprises and key enterprises to take the initiative, enterprises to take the lead, joint universities, research institutes, intermediaries, financial capital, etc. to participate in the establishment of a number of professional green technology innovation alliances by category and by category. More efforts will be made to implement the industry-university cooperation and education project in the field of green technology, support the alliance to integrate upstream and downstream resources of the industrial chain, and jointly carry out research on green technology innovation technology. (Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, National Development and Reform Commission, and National Energy Administration are responsible for the division of responsibilities)

(7) Strengthening the construction of a platform for green technology innovation bases.

In the field of green technology, we will cultivate and build a number of innovative bases such as the National Engineering Research Center, the National Technology Innovation Center, and the National Science and Technology Resource Sharing Service Platform, with priority in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei, Yangtze River Economic Belt, and Pearl River Delta regions. In the country, we will build a basic long-term field ecological observation and research station and other scientific research and observation networks and a number of scientific data centers, and open data to the society as required. All kinds of green technology innovation base platforms supported by universities, research institutes, state-owned enterprises and the government should be open to the society and actively release the green technology research and development results to the whole society and continue to update dynamically. Establish an evaluation and evaluation incentive mechanism for various types of innovation base platforms, eliminate the non-standard innovation base platform, and establish a dynamic adjustment mechanism. The National Science and Technology Plan project is tilted towards the green technology innovation base platform. (The Ministry of Science and Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission take the lead, the Ministry of Natural Resources, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, the State Forestry and Forestry Bureau, etc.)

Third, strengthen the guiding mechanism of green technology innovation

(8) Strengthening the direction of green technology innovation.

Formulate a green industry guidance catalogue, a green technology promotion catalogue, a green technology and equipment elimination catalogue, guide the direction of green technology innovation, promote the upgrading of technical equipment in various industries, and encourage and guide social capital to invest in green industries.

Strengthen support for green technology innovation in key areas, focusing on energy-saving and environmental protection, clean production, clean energy, ecological protection and restoration, urban and rural green infrastructure, urban green development, ecological agriculture and other key common technologies, cutting-edge technology, modern engineering technology, Subversive technological innovation, benchmarking the international advanced level, through the national science and technology plan, forward-looking, systematic, strategic layout of a number of research and development projects, breaking through the technical bottlenecks of key materials, instruments and equipment, core processes, industrial control devices, and promoting the development of a Approve the key core green technologies with independent intellectual property rights and reach the international advanced level, and effectively enhance the original innovation capability.

Improve the project approval, acceptance and evaluation mechanism of green technology research projects supported by the government. Establish the concept of “projects from demand, results to applications”, establish a normal green technology demand collection mechanism, and closely deploy research projects around major key green technology needs. Reform the scientific research performance evaluation mechanism, establish a scientific classification, reasonable and diversified evaluation system, strengthen the target task assessment and on-site inspection, and focus on the actual effect, maturity and demonstration and promotion value of the technology. (National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Natural Resources, Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, National Energy Administration, State Forestry and Forestry Bureau are responsible for division of responsibilities)

(9) Strengthening the guidance of green technology standards.

Implement a special plan for the revision and revision of green technology standards, and clarify the standard revision and revision tasks in key areas. Strengthen the research on general standards for green technologies, and formulate a number of green technology standards in key areas such as ecological pollution prevention, resource conservation and recycling, urban green development, new energy, energy consumption and pollutants collaborative control technologies, and clarify the key performance of green technologies and Technical indicators to carry out green technology effect evaluation and verification.

We will improve the mandatory standards for energy efficiency, water efficiency, energy consumption limits, carbon emissions, and pollutant emissions in accordance with the law, and periodically review mandatory standards and update them in a timely manner. Strengthening the implementation of standards will force enterprises to carry out green technology innovation and adopt green technology for upgrading. (Involved by the General Administration of Market Supervision, the Ministry of Science and Technology, the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Ecology and Environment, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the Ministry of Natural Resources, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, and the National Energy Administration)

(10) Establish and improve the government green procurement system.

Expand the scope of government green procurement, and increase government procurement of products such as recycling, low-carbon, renewable, and organic products based on existing energy-saving and environmentally-friendly products. Encourage state-owned enterprises and other enterprises to independently carry out green procurement.

The government selects the key green technologies that are urgently needed in the market, has practical value and has a good development foundation. The government purchases technology by means of bidding and procurement, and promotes the application to the society free of charge through announcements. (The Ministry of Finance, the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Science and Technology, and the Ministry of Ecology and Environment take the lead, and the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of the State Council participates)

(11) Promote the evaluation and certification of green technology innovation.

Promote product green (ecological) design, publish a list of green (ecological) design products, and compile relevant evaluation technical specifications. Vigorously promote green production, improve the evaluation system of green factories, and carry out demonstrations of green factory construction. Promote enterprises to use the Internet information technology to establish a green supply chain management system covering raw material procurement, production, logistics, sales and recycling.

Continue to promote the establishment of a unified green product certification system, based on green technology standards for major products such as household appliances, automobiles, building materials, etc., from the design, materials, manufacturing, consumption, logistics and recycling, recycling links to the product life cycle and the whole industry Chain green certification. Actively carry out third-party certification, strengthen the adoption of certification results, and promote the certification body to assume joint responsibility for the certification results. (Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, General Administration of Market Supervision, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development are responsible for division of responsibilities)

Fourth, promote the demonstration application of green technology innovation achievements transformation

(12) Establish and improve the market trading system for green technology transfer and transformation.

Establish a comprehensive national green technology trading market and promote the transformation of green technology innovation results through market means. Encourage all regions and relevant units to rely on or integrate existing trading venues to build a green and technical trading market with distinctive regional and professional characteristics. Establish and improve the market management system and standardize the market order. For the technology of docking transactions through the green technology trading market, the local government and the local government where the project is located should actively support the project. Promote a comprehensive service platform and service model that combines the transfer of scientific and technological achievements with financial capital to improve the efficiency of green technology transfer and transformation.

Strengthen the capacity building of green technology transaction intermediaries, formulate evaluation norms and management systems for green technology innovation intermediaries, foster a number of intermediary service organizations such as third-party testing, evaluation and certification of green technology innovation, and cultivate a group of specialized green technology innovation brokers. people". (The Ministry of Science and Technology, the National Development and Reform Commission take the lead, the Ministry of Finance, China Banking Regulatory Commission, China Securities Regulatory Commission, etc.)

(13) Improve the transformation mechanism of green technology innovation achievements.

Implement the first (set) major technical equipment insurance compensation policy measures, and support the first (set) green technology innovation equipment demonstration application. Continue to implement the first batch of key insurance materials to apply the insurance compensation mechanism, and use market-based means to promote the promotion and application of key new materials.

Support enterprises, universities, scientific research institutions, etc. to establish green technology innovation project incubators, innovation and entrepreneurship bases. We will build green technology pilot public facilities, study and formulate relevant systems, and create loose conditions for the construction of green technology pilot facilities. We will adopt a government procurement service and other methods to improve the public service system for green technology innovation and support the transformation of start-up enterprises and achievements.

Actively play the role of the National Science and Technology Achievements Transformation and Guidance Fund, and select a batch of key green technology innovation achievements to support the transformation and application every year. Guide various types of angel investment, venture capital funds, local venture capital funds, etc. to support the transformation of green technology innovation results. (Ministry of Science and Technology, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, China Banking Regulatory Commission, China Securities Regulatory Commission, etc.)

(14) Strengthening the comprehensive demonstration of the transfer and transformation of green technology innovation.

Choose cities with better green technology innovation and build a comprehensive demonstration zone for green technology innovation. Encourage the organization and implementation mode of innovation “scientific + technology + engineering” in the comprehensive demonstration zone of green technology innovation, organize the advantages and innovation power, implement urban black and odor water treatment, comprehensive treatment of the Bohai Sea, protection of the Yangtze River, pollution control of agriculture and rural areas, clean and efficient use of coal, Major projects and demonstration projects for comprehensive research and development of solid waste, industrial energy conservation and water conservation, green building, building energy conservation, clean heating, sponge city, energy efficient electrical appliances, clean energy replacement, marine biological resources development and utilization, seawater desalination and comprehensive utilization Explore the synergy between green technology innovation and policy management innovation, and achieve green technology advancement and technological innovation to drive green development. Establish an assessment and evaluation mechanism for green technology innovation demonstration zones and eliminate demonstration zones that are not up to standard.

The “Garden in the Garden” model will be adopted to carry out green technology innovation transfer and transformation demonstration in national high-tech development zones and economic and technological development zones, and promote the transformation of conditional industrial agglomeration areas into green technology innovation clusters. Promote the construction of green eco-city and encourage the adoption of green new technologies in the process of green eco-city construction. (Involved in the Ministry of Science and Technology, the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the Ministry of Natural Resources, the Ministry of Ecology and Environment, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, the State Forestry and Forestry Bureau, and the National Energy Administration.)

V. Optimize the environment for green technology innovation

(15) Strengthening the protection and service of intellectual property rights in green technologies.

We will improve the intellectual property protection system for green technologies and strengthen the protection of intellectual property rights in all aspects of green technology research and development, demonstration, promotion, application and industrialization. The intellectual property department will work with relevant parties to establish a green technology intellectual property protection contact mechanism, a public service mechanism, a work linkage mechanism, and carry out special actions to crack down on infringement of green technology intellectual property rights. Establish information records on green technology infringements and incorporate relevant information into the national public credit sharing platform. Strengthen green technology innovation intellectual property services, promote the establishment of a “fast track” for green technology intellectual property review, provide comprehensive services for rapid review, rapid confirmation, and rapid rights protection for green technology intellectual property, and improve the monitoring of green technology intellectual property statistics. (The State Intellectual Property Office takes the lead, the Ministry of Science and Technology, the National Development and Reform Commission, etc.)

(16) Strengthen financial support for green technology innovation.

Guide banking financial institutions to rationally determine the financing threshold for green technology loans, actively carry out financial innovation, and support green technology innovation enterprises and project financing. Study and formulate green investment standards and behavioral guidelines for public and private equity funds, and make green technology innovation a priority support area. Develop multi-level capital markets and M&A markets, and improve the exit mechanism for green technology innovation enterprise investors. Encourage green technology innovation companies to make full use of domestic and international market listing financing. Insurers are encouraged to develop insurance products that support green technology innovation and green product applications. Local governments are encouraged to provide guarantees or other types of risk compensation for the transformation and demonstration of green technology innovation results through guarantee funds or commissioned professional guarantee companies. Relevant pilots involving green finance for green technology innovation will be tested first in the green finance reform and innovation pilot zone approved by the State Council. (The People's Bank of China, the National Development and Reform Commission, the China Banking Regulatory Commission, and the China Securities Regulatory Commission are responsible for the division of responsibilities)

(17) Promote green technology innovation in the whole society.

Organize the Green Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition to reward the winners, institutions and individuals of the competition. Encourage relevant units to carry out green technology innovation competitions, investment conferences, entrepreneurial roadshows, innovation forums and innovation results presentations, auctions, trade fairs and other service activities to promote the integration of green technology innovation entrepreneurs and investment and financing institutions. In accordance with the relevant provisions of the science and technology awards, the state rewards individuals or organizations that have overcome key green technologies and created significant economic and social benefits or ecological and environmental benefits.

Promote green technology creation, establish a green technology creation space in science parks and entrepreneurial bases where innovation resources are concentrated, and guide university researchers to establish green technology innovation enterprises. Encourage enterprises and scientific research institutions to carry out green technology innovation activities, establish incentive mechanisms, and raise employees' awareness of green innovation.

Through the national “double innovation” week, national energy conservation publicity week, six-five environmental day, national low-carbon day and other platforms to strengthen green technology innovation propaganda, guide all kinds of media to increase publicity, explore typical cases, and promote successful experience. Create a green technology innovation culture atmosphere in the whole society, promote green technology innovation information and knowledge dissemination, and actively guide green development methods and lifestyles. (Ministry of Science and Technology, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, China Banking Regulatory Commission, China Securities Regulatory Commission, etc.)

6. Strengthening green technology innovation, opening up and international cooperation

(18) Deepening international cooperation in green technology innovation.

Deeply participate in global environmental governance and promote international exchanges and cooperation in the field of green technology innovation. Based on the cooperation mechanism of the G20, the Belt and Road, and the BRICS countries, we will promote the establishment of the “One Belt, One Road” green technology innovation alliance and other cooperative institutions to strengthen the international exchange of green technology innovation.

Actively disseminate green technology innovation concepts and achievements through the holding of expositions, forums, etc., promote the international trade and transfer of green technologies, and promote leading enterprises to play a leading role in some international green technology research and development. We will carry out the selection and promotion of the top ten best energy-saving technologies and the top ten best energy-saving practices (“Double Ten Best”) to promote the promotion and application of outstanding green technology achievements. (Ministry of Science and Technology, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Ecology and Environment are responsible for division of responsibilities)

(19) Increase the opening of green technology innovation to the outside world.

Actively introduce international advanced green technologies, encourage international green technology holders to promote the transformation of green technology innovation achievements in China through technology shareholding and cooperation in setting up enterprises, and strengthen the protection of property rights of international green technologies. Support national economic and technological development zones and other international cooperative ecological parks. Foreign green technology innovation enterprises will set up green technology innovation parks or build “garden parks” in China, and enjoy the same preferential policies as domestic enterprises.

Actively create convenient conditions, encourage qualified enterprises, undergraduate colleges, vocational colleges and research institutes to “go global”, carry out mutually beneficial cooperation in accordance with international rules, and promote the transformation and application of mature green technologies in other countries. (Ministry of Science and Technology, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Commerce, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development are responsible for division of responsibilities)

Seven, organization and implementation

(20) Strengthen overall coordination.

The National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Science and Technology have taken the lead in establishing an inter-ministerial coordination mechanism for green technology innovation. All regions and departments should, in light of their respective realities, strengthen policy linkages, formulate implementation plans or strengthen innovation support in related fields, strengthen organizational leadership, clarify responsibilities, increase investment, and effectively implement various tasks and measures. (National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Science and Technology lead)

(21) Strengthen evaluation and assessment.

Strengthen the assessment and performance evaluation of green technology innovation policies, establish a green technology innovation evaluation system, and incorporate green technology innovation achievements, promotion and application into innovation-driven development, high-quality development, and evaluation of ecological civilization construction. (Leaded by the Ministry of Science and Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission)

(22) Strengthen demonstration and lead.

Bring into play the role of green technology innovation comprehensive demonstration zone, green technology engineering research center, green technology innovation center, green enterprise technology center, etc., explore the effective mode of green technology innovation and green management system synergy, and timely summarize the practices and success of replicable promotion Experience, play the role of demonstration. (Leaded by the Ministry of Science and Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission)
