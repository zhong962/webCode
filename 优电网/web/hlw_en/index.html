Smart grid

Smart grid is the intelligentization of the grid (Smart Power), also known as "Grid 2.0", which is based on an integrated, high-speed two-way communication network, through advanced sensing and measurement technology, advanced equipment technology. Advanced control methods and the application of advanced decision support system technologies to achieve reliable, safe, economical, efficient, environmentally friendly and safe use of the power grid. Its main features include self-healing, stimulating and protecting users, defending against attacks, and providing Meet the power quality of 21st century users, allow access to a variety of different forms of power generation, start the power market, and optimize the efficient operation of assets.

Life is convenient to improve people's quality of life

The smart grid will be integrated with smart homes to better provide the family's IoT services; at the same time, people's electricity consumption will be more secure and smarter.

Generate benefits to optimize energy resource allocation

China's energy resources and energy demand are reversed. More than 80% of coal, hydropower and wind energy resources are distributed in the western and northern regions, while more than 75% of energy demand is concentrated in the eastern and central regions.

Promote energy conservation and emission reduction and develop a low carbon economy

Under the background of the country's vigorous development of low-carbon economy and the emphasis on sustainable development of society, the construction and promotion of a strong smart grid with Chinese characteristics has become an inevitable trend and an inevitable choice for China's national economic development.
