Xinjiang's wheat yield per unit area has been renewed to 731.3 kg

  the north and south of Tianshan Mountains are in the summer grain harvest season. Xindong 60, a new winter wheat variety bred by Xinjiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences, has recently yielded 731.3 kg per unit area in Zepu County, Xinjiang, setting a new record of 724 kg per unit area of Winter Wheat in 2018.
  expert guidance group of wheat experts from the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Areas recently came to Zepu Base, wheat breeder, Xinjiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences, 100 mu high-yield demonstration field to check and accept the field actual yield of new winter wheat variety Xindong 60.
  the expert group inspected the field growth of wheat, and formed acceptance opinions by measuring, threshing, weighing, moisture measurement, sampling and determination of impurity rate in accordance with the requirements of "National Measures for Acceptance of Establishing Yield Measurement for High Grain Yield (Trial Implementation)".
  the panel randomly selected 3.18 mu of actual area, harvested 2346 kg of fresh grain weight, impurity rate was 0.1%, average moisture content was 13.68%. Converted by 13% standard moisture content, Xindong 60 per mu yield reached 731.3 kg. The Agricultural and Rural Department of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region confirmed that this has refreshed the record of winter wheat harvest per unit yield in Xinjiang.
  Lei Junjie, wheat cultivation expert of Xinjiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences, said that the demonstration field adopted a variety of cultivation techniques, such as good varieties and methods, water and fertilizer integration, high-efficiency water-saving drip irrigation, spraying and multi-prevention, water-saving and weight-reducing and drug-reducing, to achieve the goal of high-yield, high-efficiency, weight-reducing and green yield-increasing. The renewal of high-yielding record shows that wheat still has high potential for High-yielding in Southern Xinjiang. Breeding of new varieties can provide technical support for Xinjiang's food security and help the four prefectures in Southern Xinjiang to overcome poverty.
