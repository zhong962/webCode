High import prices of apples have become the biggest increase in imported fruits

Apple is one of the favorite fruits in our life. It contains abundant vitamins and various nutrients needed by human body. It is favored by many consumers at home and abroad. Recently, with the expansion of market demand, many imported fruits have gradually entered the lives of citizens'friends.

On July 23, the reporter learned from Changsha Customs that in the first half of this year, Our Province imported 26,000 tons of fresh and dried fruits and nuts, an increase of 1.1 times over the same period last year. Among them, the price of imported apples rose 1.25 times, becoming the largest increase in imported fruits.
  the price of imported fruits increased simultaneously
  this year, the price of apples doubled, causing many people to lament: eating an apple is reluctant to peel...
&emsp: "At present, the price of apples has reached its peak in nearly ten years." The price of Chile's imported Fuji King Apple rose to 16.8 yuan per kilogram, up 30% from last year, according to Cao, a saleswoman at the Pofu Plaza store, a fruit friend on Furong Road in Changsha.
In Ms. Cao's view, fruit prices have risen sharply and sales have also risen significantly this year. "Compared with previous years, the price of imported grapes has risen by 30%. The price of Green rhyme green extract made in Japan has risen from 39 yuan/kg to nearly 50 yuan/kg. Imported fruits are selling well this year, with sales at least doubling.
