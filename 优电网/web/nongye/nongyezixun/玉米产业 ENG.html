The "aristocratic" Fresh-eating maize, which has emerged as a new force in the maize industry, has seized the major markets

Fresh corn is the black horse in the fruit industry. It is also called fruit corn. It is far from the ordinary corn we see in our life. Fresh corn has the characteristics of sweet, waxy, tender and fragrant. Its taste and quality are very high, and its demand in the market is very wide.

 Recently, the 5th China (Wuqing) North Fresh Corn Conference was held in Wuqing District of Tianjin, which was jointly organized by China Seed Association, Tianjin Agricultural and Rural Committee and Tianjin Wuqing District People's Government. Entering the exhibition area, 246 fresh-eating maize varieties from 26 provinces, cities and 66 units in China competed for brilliance. After fierce competition, 24 varieties stood out and won excellent awards. Participants went deep into the field and tasted fresh corn with sweet, soft and waxy fragrance while communicating. It was very lively.

Setting up "Exhibition and Sales Plus" Platform and Planting "Tongwulang" Test Field


　　

With the deepening of supply-side structural reform and the growing demand for fresh-eating Maize in domestic and foreign markets, fresh-eating maize has gradually become a prominent aristocrat in the maize industry. In recent years, with the rapid development of industry and the emergence of new varieties, not only make the table more abundant for the people, but also raise the peasants'pockets.


  located in Wuqing District of Tianjin between Beijing and tianjin, in the strategic pattern of coordinated development between beijing, Tianjin and hebei, by virtue of its unique geographical advantages, it has become a fresh corn and vegetable supply base in Beijing and tianjin, where more than 100,000 mu of fresh corn and 250,000 mu of vegetables are planted. The North Fresh Corn Conference of China (Wuqing), sponsored by Wuqing District, has been held for five consecutive sessions. The Conference has set up a platform for the display, sale and processing of North Fresh Corn Varieties. In recent years, more and more people in the industry have paid attention to the conference, which has become an annual event of fresh corn industry in northern China.
  this conference includes fresh corn branch election, fresh corn industry summit forum, variety information conference, opening ceremony, excellent variety selection, on-site freshness and other links. Different from the past, the conference for the first time added a forum for the development of Fresh-eating maize planting areas and the revitalization of counties. The leaders of counties, industry experts, enterprise leaders, etc. gave guidance to the local fresh-eating maize planting, policy guidance, market circulation, intensive processing and bottlenecks encountered in the development of the local fresh-eating maize industry. In-depth exchanges were held on the topics and future expectations. After the meeting, the variety exhibition area held five consecutive days of field harvesting activities for the public, so that the people in picking fresh corn at the same time, experience the infinite pleasure of farming harvest.
  Yang Baojian, president of China Fresh Corn Circulation Industry Alliance, who has participated in five consecutive congresses, said: "The Congress has set up a platform for display, processing and marketing, gathering resources from all sectors of the industrial chain here, and we have exchanged with each other, studied and studied.
