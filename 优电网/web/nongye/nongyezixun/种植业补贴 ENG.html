Planting subsidy

In 2019, document No. 1 of the Central Committee made it clear that grain production should be stabilized. We will not relax our efforts to improve grain production, promote the implementation of land-based and technology-based grain storage, and ensure that the sown area of grain is stable at 1.65 billion mu. With regard to subsidies for grain crops, document No. 1 of the Central Committee in 2019 proposed that we should improve the minimum purchase price policy for rice and wheat and the subsidy policy for corn and soybean producers in accordance with the orientation of better playing the role of market mechanism.

  from the "target price" subsidy pilot, to the "three subsidies" (improved varieties subsidy, grain direct subsidy, agricultural subsidy) integration into "agricultural support and protection subsidy", and then to the adjustment of fishery oil price subsidy. China's agricultural subsidy policy at the central level is undergoing a series of major reforms.
  it is reported that there are more than 50 agricultural subsidy projects at the central level in China. In the next agricultural policy reform, the vast majority of agricultural subsidy policies will be vigorously reformed and adjusted. For example, "target price" subsidy will be extended from soybean and cotton to three main staple foods, and "three subsidy" integration pilot will be implemented nationwide in 2016.
  after the combination of the three subsidy policies, 20% of the total subsidy stock funds are used to support the moderate scale operation of grain. In addition, 80% plus farmers'direct subsidy and crop seed subsidy funds are used for the protection of arable land productivity. The object of subsidy is all farmers who have the right to contract for cultivated land. They can really achieve "who grows grain and who benefits". It is not like now, as long as the farmland is contracted in the countryside, we can enjoy all kinds of subsidy for planting land and grain.

Great, my agricultural country.
