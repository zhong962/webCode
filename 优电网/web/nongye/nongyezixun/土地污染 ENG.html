Land Pollution Prevention and Control Law

In recent years, aiming at the environmental and land pollution problems, the state has taken a series of measures, especially in the field of ecological environmental protection. The Law on the Prevention and Control of Soil Pollution, passed in August 2018, was formally implemented on January 1, 2019

  "soil pollution prevention and control law" focuses on establishing the corresponding legal system and system; strengthening environmental supervision of industrial and mining enterprises, cutting off the source of pollution and curbing the trend of expansion; implementing graded and classified management of polluted land, establishing its own technical system, and gradually promoting risk management and control.
  according to the survey results of soil pollution carried out by the relevant departments of the State Council, the overall situation of soil environment in China is grim and not optimistic, and some parts of the region are seriously polluted. The prevention and control of soil pollution concerns the safety of the people's tongue, directly or indirectly affects the safety of our food and drinking water, concerns the vital interests of the broad masses of people, and even concerns the sustainable development. The foundation of soil pollution prevention and control in China is relatively weak, and there is no special single law, which makes the governments at all levels and relevant departments lack the legal basis for effective supervision and urgently needs to be solved by legislation. Over the years, during the National People's Congress, representatives have put forward legislative proposals or suggestions. In this regard, the CPC Central Committee, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the State Council attach great importance to it. With the approval of the Central Committee, the Standing Committee of the current National People's Congress has included the soil pollution prevention and control law in the category of legislative planning, which is drafted and submitted for consideration by the Environmental Investment Commission.
  first, we should establish and improve the management system of soil pollution prevention and control, and clarify the division of responsibilities among government departments. Secondly, in accordance with the requirements of General Secretary Xi Jinping on innovation-driven, we should promote the capacity-building of science and technology, and establish and improve the standard system of investigation and monitoring of soil pollution prevention and control. Thirdly, we should clarify economic policies and increase investment. Fourth, focus on agricultural land and construction land, to ensure the quality and safety of farmers and human settlements. Fifthly, we should establish a mechanism of social participation, clarify the responsibilities of governments and enterprises at all levels, and clarify the ways and contents of public participation.
