Agricultural machinery subsidies

With the development of society, the improvement of science and technology and the level of agricultural machinery, the state has made corresponding adjustments to the purchase tax of agricultural machinery and the abandonment of agricultural machinery. According to relevant policies, the purchase tax of a agricultural machinery can enjoy up to 30,000 yuan of subsidy.

  agricultural machinery purchase subsidy is an important part of the national "three subsidy" policy of strengthening agriculture and benefiting farmers Arable land operation Arable land operation Rong is an important measure to implement document No. 1 of the Central Committee. It is of great significance to improve the structure of agricultural equipment, improve the level of agricultural mechanization, enhance the comprehensive production capacity of agriculture, develop modern agriculture and prosper the rural economy.
  as of August 30, 2012, a total of 14.29 billion yuan of subsidized funds for the purchase of agricultural machinery from the central government has been implemented nationwide. The first batch of subsidized funds from the central government has basically been implemented, and 4.052 million sets of agricultural machinery (sets) have been subsidized, benefiting 3.17 million households. In order to ensure the smooth development of the subsidy work for the purchase of agricultural machinery, the competent departments of agricultural mechanization and finance at all levels, in accordance with the overall requirements of "early, fast, strict, detailed and solid", started as soon as possible, accelerated implementation, carefully deployed, standardized operation and strict supervision, mainly carried out six aspects of work:

Mobilization and deployment for early implementation

  on January 6, the two general offices of the Ministry of Agriculture and Finance jointly issued the Guidelines for the Implementation of Agricultural Machinery Purchase Subsidies in 2012, and issued a list of subsidies for general-purpose machinery. On February 14, the Ministry of Agriculture held a national conference on subsidies for agricultural machinery purchase. It summarized the achievements, practices and experiences of the implementation of subsidies for agricultural machinery purchase in recent years, and deployed the implementation of subsidies for agricultural machinery purchase throughout the year and the construction of a clean government risk prevention and control mechanism. Sign responsibility letters for implementing subsidies for agricultural machinery purchase with the heads of provincial (district, city, corps, farming and reclamation) departments in charge of agricultural mechanization. Establish a responsibility mechanism of "the main leaders take overall responsibility, the responsible leaders take full responsibility, and the staff take direct responsibility", so as to achieve the goal of arriving at the post and taking responsibility to the people.

Perfecting mechanism and standardizing management

 The Ministry of Agriculture issued the "Implementation Plan of Extended Performance Management for the Implementation of the Policy of Strengthening Agriculture and Benefiting Farmers and Rich Farmers in 2012", which initiated the policy of subsidizing agricultural machinery purchase by provincial agricultural mechanization authorities to implement the extended performance management, and promoted the implementation of the Party's policy of strengthening agriculture and benefiting farmers and rich farmers. The General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture issued the Notice on Further Standardizing the Operation of Subsidized Products for Agricultural Machinery Purchase, further standardizing the Operation of Subsidized Products for Agricultural Machinery Purchase, severely punishing illegal production and marketing enterprises, and earnestly safeguarding the legitimate rights and interests of farmers and honest enterprises.

Careful examination and strict control

  organize a careful review of the provincial agricultural machinery purchase subsidy implementation plan, subsidy list, and the provisions of the policy documents and the requirements of the Ministry of Agriculture and Finance are inconsistent, timely put forward, comprehensive rectification, strict control of policy implementation from the source. The Ministry of Agriculture and Finance timely approved the pilot subsidy operation schemes reported by the provinces. It has jointly approved the pilot schemes of Shandong, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Hunan, Gansu, Guangxi and other 17 provinces (districts, cities) after purchasing the machines at full price, applying for subsidy on the basis of invoices and decentralizing the settlement level.
