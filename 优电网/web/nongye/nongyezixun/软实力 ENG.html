INFROMATION\ Agricultural Soft Power

and Promoting Soft Power by "Golden Business Card"

From the outskirts of the capital, agricultural areas, to the frontier of Beijing's reform and development, and the coordinated development of Beijing, Tianjin and Hebei, Daxing District is now focusing on relieving, renovating and promoting upgrading. On the one hand, Daxing District has made great efforts to introduce high-end industrial projects to build a new economic development area in Beijing, on the other hand, it has made great efforts to create a golden business card of Nanhai Ziyuan culture. To enhance the soft power of culture and to lay a solid cultural foundation for the "new country and new prosperity".

　　

  Zhongguancun High-end Medical Devices Industrial Park, where Huake is precisely located, is located in Daxing Biomedical Base, known as "Chinese Medicine Valley".
  the park integrates research and development, incubation, production and service of high-end medical devices. In the first phase, 48 high-end medical devices projects have been introduced and more than 20 high-end projects have been reserved. The second phase is expected to start at the end of the year, and 100 high-end medical devices enterprises will be introduced after completion.
  on the basis of last year, this year we started the construction of 300,000 square meters of incubator, so that high-end projects in three cities and one district quickly landed. Within three to five years, we will build a 100 billion-level industrial cluster.
  while speeding up the promotion of high-quality development, shaping a new pattern of strong industrial areas, and promoting the planning of 8.4 square kilometers expansion of biomedical bases, Daxing District also accelerates the upgrading of new media bases and promotes the transformation and reuse of old industrial plants.
In addition, Daxing District has also promoted 2.9 square kilometres of land evacuation in Qingyundian, Changziying and Chongyu industrial parks, and coordinated the promotion of "cage for bird" for inefficient industrial land.
  adjusted a number of current industrial and storage land, unified into the scope of Zhongguancun Daxing Park policy, and formed a "one garden one district" industrial pattern with the airport economic zone. With the basic clearance of industrial courtyards, Daxing District has gradually transformed from "relief and retreat" as the main line to a new stage of promoting high-quality development, upgrading and undertaking.
  in recent years, Daxing District has been promoting the cultural revival of Nanhaizi and enhancing the soft power of urban culture. Since 2013, a number of historical relics such as Tuanhe Xinggong and Deshou Temple have been effectively protected and repaired. At present, the main works of Tuanhe Xinggong and Deshou Temple have been basically completed.
In order to excavate the cultural value of Nanhaizi, Daxing District also plans to systematically collate the historical documents of Nanhaizi District, focusing on promoting the function of Tuanhe Xinggong Palace and the renovation and utilization of Deshou Temple. At the same time, the historical and cultural resources of Tuanhe Xinggong Palace are used to explore the construction of Tuanhe Xinggong Cultural Expo Park, and strive to build Tuanhe Xinggong Palace into a new position and an important window to display the excellent history and culture of the Chinese nation and to carry out international cultural exchanges.
