Han Changfu Meets Serbian Minister of Agriculture, Forestry and Water Resources Nadimovic

On the afternoon of July 30, Minister of Agriculture and Rural Areas Han Changfu met with visiting Serbian Minister of Agriculture, Forestry and Water Resources, Nadimovic, in Beijing.

　　

  Han Changfu highly appraised the achievements of agricultural cooperation and development between the two countries, and briefly reviewed the close contacts in the field of agriculture between the two countries in recent years. Han Changfu said that China and Seychelles are highly complementary in agriculture. China welcomes the export of high-quality agricultural products from Seychelles to China that meet the requirements. It is willing to accelerate the construction of fruit and vegetable industrial park projects, continuously deepen agricultural investment and cooperation in agricultural science and technology and other fields, and jointly promote the development of agricultural cooperation between China and Central and Eastern European countries.
  Neimovich agreed with Han Changfu and thanked China for its pragmatic measures to deepen agricultural cooperation. He expressed his willingness to further strengthen cooperation and exchanges and strive to push bilateral agricultural cooperation to a new level.
