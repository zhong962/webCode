INFROMATION\ Seawater rice

Yuan Longping's team tried to grow more than 1,000 mu of "seawater rice" in saline-alkali land before Wenchangpu this year.

"Seawater rice" does not grow directly in seawater

Liu Xiong, the project leader of Hainan Seawater Rice Research and Development Demonstration Base, introduced that "Seawater Rice" is a common name for salt-tolerant rice. It does not grow directly in seawater, but is a special kind of rice that survives and grows on saline-alkali land and beach. It has three characteristics: no fertilization, disease and insect resistance and salt-alkali tolerance, so it can make wasteland. Become a good farmland.

  "the concentration of sea water is about 3%, the highest salt tolerance concentration of salt-tolerant rice found at present is not more than 1.2%. It is impossible to plant rice directly with sea water in a predictable time." Luo Yuehua, an expert on rice breeding in Hainan Key Laboratory of Salt-tolerant Crop Biotechnology and a professor at the College of Agriculture and Forestry of Hainan University, introduced that after scientific breeding and cultivation, the value of promoting "seawater rice" has become increasingly prominent.
According to media reports, in the past two years in the experimental base of the Seawater Rice Research and Development Center of Licang District, Qingdao City, Shandong Province, the "seawater rice" produced by the technical team led by Yuan Longping, father of hybrid rice, has achieved a bumper harvest. The yield per mu can reach 620.95 kg by harvesting evaluation. On September 27, 2018, the "seawater rice" was cut in the saline-alkali land of Longgang New Town, Cangnan County, Wenzhou, which laid a foundation for the promotion and planting of "seawater rice" in the saline-alkali land along the Southeast coast. "Seawater rice" and "height" are shorter than conventional rice, and the planting cycle is different. Wan Jili, Technical Director of Qingdao Rice Research and Development Center, introduced that it was difficult to grow crops on saline-alkali land, with low survival rate and poor efficiency. It was a breakthrough to grow "seawater rice".
  on August 4, the responsible person of Wenchang merchants Bureau told reporters that the Wenchang municipal government is currently working on the implementation of land rental issues, and the demonstration base is located in the northern part of wenchang, in front of the pavement, in the area of jinshan.

This year, more than 1000 mu of "sea-water rice" will be planted in Wenchang.

With the planting year after year, the soil fertility and grain yield decrease, and the influence of typhoon and sea water irrigation, the land salinization in the northern part of Wenchang is becoming more and more serious. In 2014, the super typhoon "Wilmson" hit Wenchang directly, and 10,117 mu of good land was immersed in sea water. Because of the high salinity in the soil, the initial crop trial failed. Statistical data show that at the beginning of 2017, there were about 353,000 mu of abandoned land in Puqian Town, Jinshan Town, Fengpo Town, Wengtan Town and Luodou Farm in the north of Wenchang.

The coastline of Hainan Island is more than 1800 kilometers long, with a beach area of 2.17 million mu. "Rice is the preferred food crop for the development and utilization of coastal beach and saline-alkali land." Wang Fengning, a researcher at the Food Crop Research Institute of the Provincial Academy of Agricultural Sciences, said that rice, as an important cash crop, could solve the food problem of 200 million people if 100 million mu of saline-alkali land could be developed.
  Yuan Longping stressed in an interview with the media that China has less arable land and a large population, so it is necessary to develop agriculture.
It is known that from 2012, the research on seawater rice has become the focus of Yuan Longping's work. Yuan Longping hoped that through the research and development and promotion of salt-tolerant hybrid rice, saline-alkali land would benefit mankind like common cultivated land. He called the breakthrough and innovation of seawater rice technology the pioneer spirit.
  On the evening of August 4, Liu Xiong, the project leader of Hainan Seawater Rice Research and Development Demonstration Base, told Nanguo Metropolitan Daily: "At present, the relevant departments of Wenchang are cooperating with us to promote this project, and have discussed with farmers to implement some land. This year, we are going to rent more than 1000 mu of land in Wenchangpuqian Town and try to grow seawater rice. Now, we are going to breed 100 mu of seawater rice. The experts and technicians of our team have been stationed in Wenchang.

Great, my agricultural country.
