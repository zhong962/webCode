Establishment of China's Peasant Harvest Festival by Multi-Sector Cooperation

China is a big agricultural country, and "Chinese Peasant Harvest Festival" is the first festival belonging to peasants in the process of agricultural development, which plays an important role in the development of agriculture in China. Recently, 13 departments, such as the Central Agricultural Office and the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Areas, jointly issued the Guiding Opinions on Organizing and Implementing China's Farmers'Harvest Festival, making overall arrangements for the work related to the "Harvest Festival" in the coming period.


Farmers'Harvest Festival


  "Opinions" clearly shows that Chinese farmers'harvest festival adheres to the main body of farmers, close to the production and life of farmers, meet the material and spiritual needs of farmers, improve the participation of farmers and grass-roots coverage, give full play to the wisdom and strength of farmers, so that farmers can truly become the protagonists of the festival. All localities should proceed from reality, combine local folk culture and farming, organize and carry out colorful festival activities, highlight national and regional characteristics, and focus on the counties and villages, so as to avoid uniformity.
  The Opinion calls for mobilizing all parties to carry out extensive activities to celebrate the harvest and jointly promote the formation of farmers'custom of celebrating the harvest in the new era. We should give full play to the guiding role of various departments in various regions, constantly enrich the forms and contents of festival activities, give full play to the unique advantages and functions of group organizations, and constantly enhance the universality of the participation of the whole people, give full play to the advantages of excellent traditional folk culture resources, and promote the integration and development of harvest festival and traditional harvest festivals. Deeply carry out all-round publicity, and constantly enhance the social awareness and influence of the festival. Strengthen the research and protection of traditional folk customs, constantly enhance the cultural connotation of festivals, strengthen the protection and inheritance of farming culture, and promote the revitalization of rural culture. Vigorously cultivate the festival market, and constantly enhance the endogenous power of the festival. We should build a platform for agricultural products production and marketing to promote farmers'continuous income increase, fully tap the multi-function of agriculture and further prosper the rural market, encourage the development of harvest-saving products, and leave a rich and colorful harvest mark for the whole people.
  "Opinions" stressed that all departments should strengthen organizational leadership, put the organization and implementation of China's farmers'harvest festival as an important part of the "three rural" work on a long-term agenda, clarify the division of responsibilities, and strengthen research and deployment. Agricultural and rural departments should play a leading role in organizing festivals, plan festival celebrations in coordination with relevant departments, strengthen collaboration and cooperation, and form a long-term working mechanism. At the same time, we should strengthen the management and guidance of local celebrations, ensure that celebrations are not biased or tasteless, strictly abide by the rules of discipline, put the starting point and end point of activities on enhancing the sense of farmers'access, avoid formalism and extravagance, and strictly prevent hitchhiking for celebrations and increasing farmers' burden.

Great, my agricultural country.
