Infromation\ "Cloud" Technology

Look, the "cloud" technology is used for farming (tracking changes in high-quality agricultural technology in the field)

Planting land "facing the Loess and facing the sky behind it" has now become a history in some places. Entering the new agricultural stores in Yangxin County, Shandong Province, farmers use "cloud" technology and big data to enjoy intelligent services such as precise weather, remote sensing observation, water and fertilizer integration, and point mobile phones instead of hoes.

  Internet of things, intelligent agricultural machinery and other smart agricultural technology are changing the mode of agricultural production: from resources, elements to technology, efficiency, high-quality development of agriculture breeds vigorous new momentum.
  how does smart agriculture grow land?
  "others look at the sky, I look at the screen", big data have the final say
  sitting at home, looking at the screen, clicking on the mobile phone and doing farm work in thousands of acres, is there such a god?
This is what Sun Zhanfeng of Sun Jiaweizi Village in Laodian Town did. "Others look at the sky, I look at the screen; others have good experience, I have big data.
  what he said was no joke. That's not true. Just in time, a timely rain poured down. After days of drought, the farmland "drank enough", and clusters of maize seedlings emerged from the wheat stubble became greener. A few days ago, Sun Zhanfeng knew about the rain. The precise agricultural meteorological service with an accuracy of 1 square kilometre helped him greatly. "Forecast 48 hours ahead of time, sowing, spraying and harvesting depend on the sky. Farming arrangements are more reasonable
