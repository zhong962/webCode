INFROMATION\ New season peanut will be on the market soon

Peanuts in Southern China will be on the market in the new season

Peanut is an annual herb of Leguminosae, also known as peanut, beans, fruit, etc. It is an important oil cash crop in our country. It occupies an important advantage in the development of agriculture in our country. Nowadays, it has a wide planting area in all provinces and cities, especially in the southern region. At present, the market stale peanut trading has entered the final stage, and some traders from the producing areas have entered the state of closure. In half a month, the new season peanuts will be on the market in all parts of the south. At present, the main producing areas are very concerned about the growth of peanuts and the time of listing. This year, the southern region has suffered heavy rainfall. Will bad weather affect the growth of peanuts? Later, what about the price of new season peanuts?

  the peanut in Changqing Town, Yongtai County, Fujian Province is about to enter the mature stage. Recently, the first cross-strait Peanut Festival was held in Changqing Town, Yongtai County, which attracted practitioners from all fields of peanut industry to attend.
  at the event site, local peanut picking, peanut DIY painting competitions were held. This year, the local temperature in Fujian is suitable, the growth of peanuts is good, no accident, and it will be a bumper harvest year.
  in addition to Fujian Province, recently, the main peanut producing areas in southern China have also entered the Fruit-setting period. Take Hubei as an example, although this year suffered from many cycles of rainfall, but the growth of local peanuts is quite good, and the quality and price of peanuts in the later period is not expected to be too bad.
  due to the reduction of peanut planting area in the new season, the supply of peanut in the production season from 2019 to 2020 may be slightly lower than that in the previous season. Xu Chunhui believes that, from the current point of view, the purchase price of the new spring white sand rice on the market is not less than 9000 yuan per ton. The price of peanuts will be higher in the early stage of the new season.
  however, from next month, not only new peanuts from southern regions will be on the market, but also new peanuts from Henan regions will be on the market in late August. Later, with the northward migration of peanut production areas, a large number of new peanuts will be listed everywhere, and then the price of peanuts will fall. Xu Chunhui suggested that growers should pay more attention to the market situation and choose the right opportunity to ship.
