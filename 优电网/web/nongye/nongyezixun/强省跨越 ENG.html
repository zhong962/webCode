Spanning the Strong Agricultural Province

On the 28th, the Fifth Plenary Session of the 12th CPC Provincial Committee held a group discussion. Members of Group 8 in Conference Room 306 of the International Conference Center spoke enthusiastically and the meeting atmosphere was very warm. As the forerunner of agricultural modernization and the ballast stone of national food security, agriculture plays an important role in our province. The members of the Committee have made suggestions and suggestions on the construction of a strong agricultural province.

Talking about Agricultural Power and Time Saving

 Wang Jinhui, director of the provincial agricultural and rural department, believes that the deployment and implementation of the "Eight-Character Policy" by the provincial Party committee fully reflects the reality of the development of Longjiang, focusing on economic construction, basing on the potential advantages, aiming at bottlenecks and shortcomings, and putting forward overall requirements for promoting high-quality economic development. Agricultural and rural work in our province should closely focus on the goal of building a strong agricultural province, adhere to the general principle of giving priority to the development of agricultural and rural areas, take the promotion of high-quality development of modern agriculture as the core, and focus on the development of high-quality and efficient agriculture, the integration of three industries, agricultural external cooperation, county economic development and deepening rural reform. The concrete deployment and arrangement is an urgent need for our province to promote modern agriculture and achieve high-quality development of agriculture.


Talking about how to realize the leap from big agricultural province to strong agricultural province

 Wang Jinhui said that our province will continue to stabilize grain production capacity, focus on improving the supply capacity of agricultural products, implement the strategy of "storing grain on the ground", optimize the adjustment of agricultural structure, and expand high-quality and efficient crop cultivation. At the same time, we will continue to promote green development, focus on promoting agricultural products to the international market, accelerate the integration of development and improve the quality and efficiency of agricultural development, pay attention to strengthening the support of science and technology for agriculture, focus on enhancing the content of agricultural science and technology, and let agriculture insert the wings of Science and technology. In addition, we will focus on deepening rural reform, vigorously promoting rural development vitality, promoting the reform of collective property rights system, vigorously developing collective economy, and boosting County Economic development.

Great, my agricultural country.
