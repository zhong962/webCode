Urban and rural health insurance

In 2019, the new rural cooperative medical system will be formally incorporated into the urban medical insurance system, which will be unified into the urban and rural medical insurance system. The announcement of this news means that our country will realize the unification of urban and rural areas in medical treatment, and it will be more convenient for farmers to see a doctor in the future.

  The Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Several Major Issues Concerning the Comprehensive Deepening of Reform, which was deliberated and adopted at the Third Plenary Session of the Eighteenth Central Committee, mentioned the establishment of a more equitable and sustainable social security system. Integrate the basic medical insurance system for urban and rural residents.
  in the process of merging, the urban housing insurance, which was originally managed by the health department and the social security department, will be integrated into the urban and rural residents'medical insurance. "Dual-track parallelism results in waste of resources and high administrative costs. After the merger, the joint management system with the social sector as the main body has been implemented, and the unification of urban and rural health insurance policies, information systems and operating agencies has been achieved. Yi Shuzhen, head of the comprehensive group of the Municipal Medical Reform Office, said.

The number of reimbursed drugs increased to more than 2400

  from the beginning of the year to the present, Dongying urban and rural medical insurance pilot has achieved good results. Statistics show that the actual reimbursement rate of urban and rural residents'medical insurance hospitalization increased by 9.13% compared with the same period last year, and the rate of general outpatient service increased by 10.59%. The new policy has brought benefits to farmers.
  because before the new rural cooperative medical system was coordinated at the county level, the policies of the counties and districts were different. Taking Kenli County under the jurisdiction of Dongying City as an example, before the merger, farmers can reimburse up to 1127 kinds of medicines, many medicines can not be reported, while the same period of urban residential insurance reimbursement list of 2387 kinds; after the merger, farmers will enjoy the same treatment with the latter. From next year in Zibo, the list of medicines paid by the former New Rural Cooperative Medical System will also be expanded, from more than 1100 to more than 2400.
