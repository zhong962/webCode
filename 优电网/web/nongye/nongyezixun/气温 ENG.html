INFROMATION\ Rising seafood prices

Prices of shellfish products in China are rising due to the influence of temperature. Fresh fish prices are still at a high level

In the aquatic market, fresh scallops, clams and other shellfish products have become the main players in the market, often placed in the booth eye-catching position. With the increase of temperature, people's preference for meat, eggs and other foods is gradually occupied by seafood, shellfish and other food materials. Reporter visits found that in recent years, shellfish prices such as clams and scallops have increased significantly, while the price of fresh fish remains high.

  recently, the sales of seafood wholesale market in Zunyi, Guizhou ushered in a peak. The increasing demand for seafood in catering industry has increased the trading volume of Guizhou seafood wholesale market.
  dealer Mr. Zeng told reporters, with the rise of temperature, the demand for fish and aquatic products has increased slightly, but due to the fixed consumer population, the sales and prices of products did not fluctuate too much.
Mr. Zeng: The River regiment is thirteen yuan a kilo, and the grass carp is more than seven yuan. On the basis of previous years, more people barbecued this year. But Zunyi's market is not as good as Guiyang's, and Zunyi's sales are not going up much, much less. Consumers are relatively fixed, during which time they can sell two or three kilograms a day.
  Ms. Liu, a seafood distributor, said that shellfish products were the most popular products in the market, followed by shrimp and crab products.
  Ms. Liu, a seafood distributor: Scallops sell a little more. In addition, shrimp sales are better than usual. Shrimp is now more than thirty yuan a kilo. It usually sells five or six hundred kilograms a day and one thousand kilograms a weekend. Shellfish usually weigh about 2,000 kilograms and 27,800 kilograms on weekends.
  at present, the fishing suspension period is in full swing, and aquaculture and refrigerated products are the main types of seafood in Weifang market, Shandong Province, with relatively stable prices. Distributors generally reflect that the price has not changed much compared with the same period in previous years.

  in addition, the continuous high temperature weather in summer and the high water temperature in freshwater fish ponds have a negative impact on the production and management of breeders. Xu Chunhui, an analyst, suggested that farmers should take "rotation and rotation" measures to reduce the density of pond liners according to market conditions. At the same time, the main flood season has been in place in recent years, and rainstorms continue in southern production areas. Analysts suggest that farmers of Penaeus vannamei should pay close attention to the weather information at any time, and do a good job in shrimp breeding protection and production planning.
