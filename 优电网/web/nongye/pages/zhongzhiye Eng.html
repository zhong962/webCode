HOBQ DESERT It is the seventh largest desert in China. Kubuqi is Mongolian. It means the string on the bow. Because it is under the Yellow River like a string hanging on the Yellow River, it gets its name. In ancient times, it was called "sand accumulation in reservoirs" and "breaking Nesha". After 30 years of hard work, millions of acres of farming soil have emerged in the Kubuqi Desert. Yili Group has built a 242 km green ecological barrier in the desert. The greening area is nearly 60,000 square kilometers, and the desertification area has been successfully controlled by 11,000 square kilometers. The control area is equivalent to two Shanghai.
