Sustainable Development Strategy

Agriculture is about national food safety, resource security and ecological security. Vigorously promoting the sustainable development of agriculture is an inevitable choice for realizing the strategic layout of the "five in one" and building a beautiful China, and is an inherent requirement of the new agricultural modernization road with Chinese characteristics.

Adapt to local conditions

    Our country has a vast territory, and there are great differences in natural conditions and economic development level among different regions. Developing agricultural circular economy can not be "one size fits all".

Development technology

    Science and technology is an important support for the development of agricultural circular economy. The government should focus on organizing and developing resource-saving technology.

Waste utilization

    Through the food chain, ecological network and the regeneration and comprehensive utilization of agricultural waste in the ecosystem, we can make up for the shortage of resources.

Agricultural product export

Yunnan, which is located at the important node of the “Belt and Road”, has unique geographical advantages and can export temperate fruits from northern China to Southeast Asian countries.

China exports frozen shrimp to many Asian countries such as Japan. Many people in China are engaged in fisheries, and China has a higher income in the field of seafood.

The “Belt and Road” has always been a key area for China to carry out international cooperation in agriculture. Many provinces and regions have carried out fruitful and mutually beneficial cooperation with countries along the “Belt and Road”.

China's current seed exports are expanding, with annual exports exceeding US$300 million. The target market is also expanding, and in addition to neighboring Southeast Asian countries, it is also strengthening cooperation with African and Central Asian markets.

Ecological Protection

Agricultural and rural ecological environment protection is an important part of ecological environment protection in the new era. It is necessary to lay a solid battle for agricultural non-point source pollution control; promoting green development of agriculture is a profound revolution in the agricultural development concept.

The construction of agricultural and rural ecological environment is the key to beautiful China, making agriculture greener and establishing a three-dimensional agricultural economic system of science and technology, green and ecology.
