Great for my agriculture.

  • Facility agriculture is mainly a modern greenhouse, which uses computer to automatically control the skylight, shading system, temperature system, temperature and humidity system, automatic sprinkler irrigation system and other automatic facilities to ensure that crops grow at the most appropriate temperature. Generally, modern greenhouse is used for seedling production.
  • Tourism agriculture is also based on the basis of modern greenhouse. It emphasizes the organic combination of gardening, landscape art, cultivation techniques and regional culture, and carries out reasonable accommodation and layout so as to make it become a new agricultural form with tourism function.
  • Intelligent agriculture can effectively improve the ecological environment of farmland. Monitoring and analysis with sensors and large data can significantly improve the efficiency of agricultural production and change the mode of agricultural production and management based on experience alone.
  • Space agriculture is a new agricultural model derived from the development of space resources based on space technology. Using satellite to carry some crop seeds in space, some space rays are irradiated to induce chromosome variation of seeds, so that new varieties can be quickly bred and high yield can be achieved. However, it is not known whether the genetic variation caused by space agriculture will affect the human body. This new type of agriculture is still in the exploratory stage.
  • Soilless culture is a kind of production and cultivation method which abandons soil and uses nutrient solution to provide nutrients for plants, including hydroponics, aerosol culture and matrix culture. The main cultivation facilities are hydroponics, pipe cultivation, column cultivation, A-frame cultivation and stump cultivation. It mainly produces vegetables, flowers, fruits, tobacco leaves and other small crops.

agricultural pioneer

Li Denghai, Father of Compact Hybrid Maize

As a farmer inventor, Li Denghai is known as the father of China's compact hybrid maize. Over the past 30 years, more than 80 new maize varieties with high yield have been selected and bred successively. Six of them have set and refreshed the record of high yield of Summer Maize in China. He presided over the selection of new maize varieties of "Yedan" series and won the first prize of the National Science and Technology Progress Award.

Yuan Longping, Father of Hybrid Rice

Yuan Longping was the pioneer and leader in the field of hybrid rice research. He devoted himself to the research, application and popularization of hybrid rice technology. He invented "three-line method" of Indica Hybrid rice, successfully developed "two-line method" of hybrid rice and established the technical system of super hybrid rice. At the same time, Yuan Longping proposed and implemented it.
