Marine Ranch

Marine pasture refers to the artificial fishing ground set up in a specific sea area for the purpose of systematically cultivating and managing fishery resources.

Our country pays more and more attention to the protection of marine ecological environment, and the investment of marine ecological restoration is increasing.

MORE

The construction of marine pasture is a fishery production mode based on modern science and technology, marine ecosystem and engineering technology to restore and optimize the ecological environment, conserve and increase fishery resources in order to achieve sustainable and healthy development of fisheries

Land farm

With the advancement of land reform, China's land farms have received a policy dividend. And according to our national conditions, as well as the progress of science and technology, it will bring new vitality to all aspects of land farms in the future.

Because of China's vast territory and different topographic environment, China's land farms also have different policy plans, such as family farms, state-owned farms and so on. And with the development of science and technology, there will be a wave of new smart farms, such as shared farms, unmanned farms and so on
