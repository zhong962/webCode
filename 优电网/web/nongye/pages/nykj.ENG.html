Smart Technology Park

The modern agricultural park is characterized by technology intensiveness and is opened with technology.Development, demonstration, radiation and promotion as the main content to promote regional agriculture Industry restructuring and industrial upgrading are the goals.

Agricultural cloud platform

The Smart Agriculture Cloud Platform aims to effectively integrate agriculture-related resources through a new generation of IoT, big data, 3S and other information technology, form a “big agriculture” data center, and build an interconnected and shared “Internet + Agriculture” information service system.

Intelligent products

The plant protection drone, also known as the unmanned aerial vehicle, is a drone for the protection of agricultural and forestry plants. The use of agricultural drones to control pests and diseases on crops has enabled automated pesticide quantitative spraying.

Intelligent plant recognition software makes it easy for you to become a crop. Farmers upload photos of crops with pests and diseases, and the APP will identify which pests are being infested by the crops and give corresponding treatment plans. The APP also has a community where users and experts can communicate and discuss and communicate with the corresponding pests and diseases.

The first artificial intelligence agricultural robot officially began inspections around the clock in the intelligent fruit and vegetable greenhouses of the Israeli demonstration farm in China. The robot looks like a white cartoon character with clear facial features and hands and feet, which can be rotated and moved 360 degrees through the bottom wheel.
