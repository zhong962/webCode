GUO SANDUI

Guo Sandui, male, born in July 1950 in Qutou Village, Bagong Town, Zezhou County, Shanxi Province. Graduated from the Department of Biology, Peking University in 1975. Researcher, doctoral supervisor. It is known as the father of insect-resistant cotton in China.

He is currently the Director of Molecular Design Center, Institute of Biotechnology, Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences. In 2014, he was awarded the "Top Ten Meritorious Persons in Chinese Seed Industry".
In the field of plant genetic engineering research, a number of important achievements have been achieved with great influence at home and abroad.
It is the first "plant high sweetness fusion sweetening protein gene" with independent intellectual property rights which is 5500 times higher than sucrose sweetness in China, and produces high sweetness sweetening protein.
We took the lead in designing and synthesizing Bt monovalent, bivalent and fused insect-resistant genes with our own intellectual property rights, and transformed insect-resistant genes into cotton. The first domestic cotton with insect-resistant genes was successfully developed and used in production, breaking the monopoly of the United States on China.
The first cotton transgenic with bivalent insect-resistant gene with our own intellectual property rights was successfully developed and used in production, which made the research of insect-resistant cotton in China occupy a place in the world.
For the first time, the transgenic insect-resistant cotton with our own intellectual property rights has been successfully developed. At present, there is no similar report at home and abroad, and it is now entering production test. Using the insect-resistant gene and new germplasm of insect-resistant cotton developed by the national breeding institutes, nearly 100 new varieties of insect-resistant cotton have been cultivated, and more than 100 new varieties have been tested by provinces, and large-scale industrialization has been realized.
It is the first time to establish a molecular breeding technology system of transgenic three-line hybrid insect-resistant cotton with high yield, high purity, high efficiency, large-scale, low cost and direct application. The overall technology is in the lead in the world, which is another major breakthrough in cotton breeding and the main direction of future development of cotton hybrid breeding. By 2011, the cumulative popularization of domestic insect-resistant cotton exceeded 560 million mu, reduced pesticide 9000 kg, and increased income by more than 80 billion yuan.

Award-winning results


1. In 2003, "Development of Cotton Insect-Resistant Gene" won the second prize of National Technology Invention Award and the first person to complete it.  
2. In 2005, "Construction and Application of Large-scale Transgenic Technology System Platform for Cotton" won the second prize of National Science and Technology Progress and the third person to complete it.  
3. In 2006, "Breeding and Industrialization Development of Lumian 15, a Genetically Modified Insect-Resistant Hybrid Cotton with High Yield, Stable Yield, Wide Suitability and High Efficiency", won the second prize of National Science and Technology Progress and the second person to complete it.  
4. In 2009, Zhongmian 41, a high-efficiency, wide-range and suitable bivalent insect-resistant cotton, was awarded the second prize of national scientific and technological progress and the third accomplishor.  
5. In 2012, "Breeding and Application of High Yield, Disease Resistance and High Quality Hybrid Cotton Varieties GSYuza 35 and Yuza 37" won the second prize of National Science and Technology Progress and the fourth accomplisher.
