LIDENGHAI

Li Denghai, male, born in September 1949, is a native of Houdengcun, Laizhou City, Shandong Province. He is currently the chairman of Shandong Denghai Seed Industry Co., Ltd. and the director of the National Corn Engineering Technology Research Center (Shandong).

As a peasant inventor, Li Denghai is known as the "father of compact hybrid maize in China". He shares the reputation of "South Yuan, North Li" with "father of hybrid rice" Yuan Longping. Over the past 30 years, more than 80 new maize varieties with high yield have been selected and bred successively. Six of them have set and refreshed the record of high yield of Summer Maize in China. He presided over the selection of "Yedan" series of new maize varieties and won the first prize of the National Science and Technology Progress Award.
In the mid and late 1990s, he developed new maize varieties of "Denghai" series and became the main cross-century Maize Varieties in China. Denghai No. 9 is a new maize variety with outstanding characteristics of high quality, high yield and multi-resistance. Its yield is 11.4% higher than Yedan No. 13, which won the first prize of National Science and Technology Progress Award. It is suitable for planting in the northeast, Huanghuaihai, northwest and southern corn areas. From March 2000 to the end of 2007, the cumulative production and sales of Denghai No. 9 reached 562.408 million kilograms, with a cumulative area of 18.7469 million mu. The cumulative increase in grain production reached 1.140 billion kilograms, with a cumulative increase in social benefits of 1.14billion yuan, which contributed to ensuring food security in China. Denghai 9 has a high starch content and is favored by ethanol and gasoline producers. It also provides a strong variety support for China's energy security.
On September 25, 2015, the Ministry of Publicity and Propaganda publicly released to the whole society "model of the times" Li Wenxiang and Li Denghai.

Major contributions


In the history of Maize Cultivation in the world, only two people have been recorded. One is Wallace, the founder of Pioneer Seed Company of the United States, who holds the world record of high yield of spring maize; the other is Li Denghai of China, who holds the world record of high yield of summer maize.
From cultivation to success, the probability of a new maize variety is only one in 120,000. A farmer with junior high school education.
But it succeeded. In the autumn of 1979, in a farmland in Houdingcun, Laizhou, a record of 776.9 kilograms of summer maize per unit unit yield was set, named "Yedan 2". Li Denghai, a 31-year-old junior high school graduate, jumped to the top of China's corn research.
In the north, maize breeding can only be done once a year. In order to speed up the experiment, Li Denghai went to Hainan to breed in winter. Li Denghai failed to celebrate the Spring Festival with his grey-haired old mother at his home in Laizhou in 27 years. He bred three times a year, and his scientific life was prolonged three times.
Li Denghai's maize seed has raised China's land from 1 person per mu to 4.5 people per mu. His maize seed has been popularized on 1 billion mu of land in the country, directly increasing economic benefits by 100 billion yuan. At home, Li Denghai is as famous as Yuan Longping, the father of hybrid rice, and is praised as one of the two Li in the seed industry.
