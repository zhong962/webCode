Significant results have been achieved in the pilot experiment of grassland husbandry in China

The first (2019) China Forage Development Forum and China (Inner Mongolia) International Animal Husbandry Machinery Exposition were held in Chifeng City, Inner Mongolia, from July 5 to 7.

The first (2019) China Forage Development Forum and China (Inner Mongolia) International Animal Husbandry Machinery Exposition were held in Chifeng City, Inner Mongolia, from July 5 to 7. & It is understood that in 2015, document No. 1 of the Central Committee clearly stated that "accelerating the development of grassland and animal husbandry, supporting silage maize and alfalfa planting, carrying out the pilot model of combining grain with feeding and planting, and promoting the coordinated development of the three-dimensional planting structure of grain, cash crops and forage". In that year, 37 counties in 12 provinces and autonomous regions of the country were arranged to carry out experimental experiments on grassland husbandry. Over the past four years, various regions have actively explored and innovated, based on resources endowment, optimized industrial distribution and improved institutional mechanisms. In production practice, they have summed up the development models of pasture-nomadic integration, warm-grazing and cold-feeding production, pasture breeding and agriculture, intensive production of forage, efficient zoning and rotation of artificial grassland and integrated green cycle of pasture and pasture, which have prolonged the industry. Chain has promoted the value chain and achieved positive results. Driven by the pilot demonstration, the planting area of high-quality forage in China is growing rapidly, the production mode of herbivorous animal husbandry is changing rapidly, the production efficiency and level of grassland husbandry are continuously improving, and the development of grassland husbandry is accelerating.
At the same time, great progress has been made in the field of artificial grass cultivation, and the level of grass husbandry equipment has been constantly improved. We have introduced digestion, absorption and innovation from foreign countries, independently developed a number of machinery and equipment suitable for the development of grass husbandry, which has filled our pasture industry with a series of machinery and equipment from land preparation, cultivation, sowing, management and processing to harvest. The blank of development.
  the meeting pointed out that on the whole, the development of grassland and animal husbandry in China is still in its infancy, the foundation is not stable, and there are many difficulties and challenges. Under the current background of "promoting agriculture by quality" and "green development", it is necessary to keep pace with the times and study ways to solve the problem so as to create a better environment for the development of grassland and animal husbandry. We should optimize the industrial layout, tap the potential of resources, promote the transformation of modes, improve the management system, strengthen scientific and technological innovation, vigorously develop high-quality forage cultivation, optimize the three-dimensional structure of grain, economy and feeding, enhance the carrying capacity, build core competitiveness, strengthen the ecological characteristic brand, and satisfy consumers'greener, richer and richer forage and livestock products. The need for better quality and safety.
