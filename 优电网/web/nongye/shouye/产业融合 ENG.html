Promoting the Integration of Rural Industries and Developing Clusters into Chains

[Promoting the integration of rural industries and developing clusters into chains] Implementing the farmers'cooperatives' standardized promotion action and family farm cultivation plan, this year the central government plans to allocate 10 billion yuan of funds, focusing on supporting the development of primary processing of agricultural products, creating distinctive brands, building distinctive product bases, and so on.

  recently, from the National Conference on the promotion of rural industry revitalization, we learned that the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs will focus on cultivating and expanding new business entities, building rural industrial parks and industrial clusters, promoting rural "double creation", strengthening market docking, green leadership and support, etc., to gather resources and firmly promote the revitalization of rural industry. 。
  industrial prosperity is an important basis for rural revitalization. Recently, the State Council issued the Guiding Opinions on Promoting the Revitalization of Rural Industries. According to the deployment requirements of the Opinions, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Areas will focus on modern farming and breeding, local characteristic industries, processing and circulation of agricultural products, rural leisure tourism, new service industries and rural information industries to promote the integration of rural primary, secondary and tertiary industries. With the combination of development and cluster formation, we should promote the formation of rural industrial structure layout with obvious hierarchical and functional cohesion among county, Central Township (town) and central village, and build rural industrial system.
  the main body of new management is the backbone of rural industry development. This year, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Areas will identify 300 new leading enterprises on the basis of 1243 key national leading enterprises in agricultural industrialization. It will support the agricultural industrialization consortium and build a mechanism of combining agriculture with agriculture, so that more jobs and value-added benefits will be left to farmers.
In order to promote the integration and development of rural industries, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Areas will strengthen the construction of modern agricultural industrial parks and agricultural product processing parks, build a number of leisure agricultural demonstration counties, strive to build 1500 strong agricultural industrial towns in five years, build innovative and entrepreneurial platforms, and promote the intensive development of all kinds of "double-venture" main bodies, hold groups and start businesses in an orderly manner. We should guide industrial and commercial capital to rural areas to develop industries with high participation and wide benefits for farmers. At the same time, we should vigorously implement the promotion of agricultural brands, cultivate a number of regional public agricultural products brands with regional characteristics, create a number of corporate brands with good quality and credibility, and create a number of "local brand" and "local brand" product brands embodying local flavor.
In addition, in order to attract more funds, technology, talents, land and other resources to converge to the countryside, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs will also promote the implementation of land support policy, financial support policy and talent policy, and constantly optimize the development environment of rural industry.
  it is reported that in 2018, the business income of agricultural products processing industry above the scale of our country reached 14.9 trillion yuan, the business income of rural leisure tourism exceeded 800 billion yuan, and the total number of innovative entrepreneurs returning to the countryside reached 7.8 million, which provided strong support for rural revitalization.

Great, my agricultural country.
