Marine Ranch

Our country pays more and more attention to the protection of marine ecological environment, and the investment of marine ecological restoration is increasing. The construction of marine pasture is one of the important measures.

The construction of marine pasture is a fishery production mode based on modern science and technology, marine ecosystem and engineering technology to restore and optimize the ecological environment, conserve and increase fishery resources in order to achieve sustainable and healthy development of fisheries.

The Meaning of Marine Ranch

  the concept of marine pasture, whether abroad or at home, currently there is no recognized definition in academic circles.
  generally speaking, marine pasture refers to the artificial fishing ground set up for the purpose of systematically cultivating and managing fishery resources in a specific sea area. Firstly, a suitable habitat for the growth and reproduction of marine organisms is created, and aquatic organisms are released (cultured). Then, the attracted organisms and the artificially stocked organisms form artificial fishing grounds. Depending on a set of systematic fishing facilities and management system, all kinds of marine organisms are gathered together, such as herds of cattle and sheep. In the case of grazing on vast grasslands, the main purpose of establishing artificially controlled marine pastures is to ensure the stability and sustained growth of aquatic resources as the basis of fishery production.

Links and Processes of Marine Ranch Construction

  according to the international experience and relevant research, the content of marine pasture construction can be summarized into five main links and processes: < br >   1. Habitat construction, including the regulation and transformation of the environment and the restoration and improvement of the habitat. It mainly provides a good environment for fish to grow, breed and bait by putting artificial reefs and rebuilding beaches.
2. The cultivation and domestication of target organisms should be combined with artificial and natural seedling cultivation to expand the number of seedlings, improve the quality of seedlings through biological engineering, establish seedling domestication farms, and achieve large-scale reproduction, optimal selection, habitual domestication and planned stocking from egg collection, incubation to larval development.
  3. monitoring capacity building, including monitoring of ecological environment quality and monitoring of biological resources.
  4. management capacity building, including marine ranch management system construction and management policy research.
  5. supporting technology construction, including engineering technology, fish breeding technology, environmental improvement and restoration technology and fishery resource management technology.
