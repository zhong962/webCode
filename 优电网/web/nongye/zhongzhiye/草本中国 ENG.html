Herbal China

Traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) embodies profound philosophical wisdom and thousands of years of Chinese people's health concept and practical experience. It is also more in-depth in the cultivation of herbal medicine in China.

Chinese Medicine

  traditional Chinese medicine, generally refers to the traditional medicine created by the working people of the Han nationality in China, so it is also called Han medicine. It is a subject that studies human physiology, pathology, diagnosis and prevention of diseases.
  traditional Chinese medicine was born in primitive society, the theory of traditional Chinese medicine had basically formed in the Spring and Autumn Period and Warring States Period, and has been summarized and developed in successive dynasties. In addition, it has a far-reaching impact on the countries of Chinese character culture circle, such as Japanese Chinese medicine, Korean medicine, Korean medicine, Vietnamese medicine and so on, which are developed on the basis of traditional Chinese medicine.
  traditional Chinese medicine carries the experience and theoretical knowledge of the ancient Chinese people in the struggle against diseases. It is a medical theory system formed and developed gradually through long-term medical practice under the guidance of ancient simple materialism and spontaneous dialectics.
  traditional Chinese medicine regards the human body as the unity of qi, shape and spirit based on the theory of yin and Yang and five elements. Through the method of "seeing, hearing, interrogating and cutting" four diagnostic methods, the etiology, pathogenicity, location, pathogenesis and the changes of five zang-organs, meridians and collaterals, qi, blood and body fluid in the human body are analyzed, and the changes of pathogenic factors are judged, and the name of the disease is obtained. Based on the principle of syndrome differentiation and treatment, the treatment methods of sweating, vomiting, descending, harmony, warming, clearing, tonifying and eliminating are formulated, and various treatment methods such as traditional Chinese medicine, acupuncture, massage, cupping, Qigong and diet are used to make the human body recover from the harmony of yin and yang.
  on October 1, 2018, the World Health Organization (who) for the first time incorporated traditional Chinese medicine (tcm) into its globally influential medical program.

Herbal medicine

  herbal medicine is made from plant extracts, mainly using its therapeutic value. Because most plants contain chemical elements, they have physical therapies such as healing.
  herbal medicine is the oldest way of health care, and plays an important role in ancient times. Today, with the development of modern medicine, it is still an unheard-of technology.
  primitive humans used the vast flora around them to maximize, regardless of animal and plant observation and its components, herbal medicine was finally born.
  traditional Chinese medicine mainly consists of plant medicine (root, stem, leaf, fruit), animal medicine (viscera, skin, bone, organs, etc.) and mineral medicine. Because botanical medicine accounts for the majority of traditional Chinese medicine, traditional Chinese medicine is also known as Chinese herbal medicine.
  The purpose of using herbs is not just to target a symptom, but to help the whole body and strengthen itself, although there is no scientific evidence. Herbal medicine has actually proved effective in healing, and its value has been demonstrated in clinical trials. These include known lilac species, which are used to treat colds, St. John's Wort, which are used to treat mild depression (not using fluoxetine), and Hawthorne berries, which help with mild heart disease during recovery.
