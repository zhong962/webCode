cash crop

Cash crop is an important raw material for light industry and plays an important role in the development of national economy. In recent years, with the rise of modern agriculture, China's economic crop industry has developed rapidly, production layout has been gradually optimized, forming a product advantage area, which strongly promotes the development of China's economy and the increase of farmers'income.

  cash crop is also known as "industrial raw material crop" and "technical crop". Generally speaking, it refers to the crops that provide raw materials for industry, especially for light industry. There are many kinds of economic crops included in artificial cultivation in China, including fiber crops (such as cotton, hemp, etc.), oil crops (such as sesame, peanut, etc.), sugar crops (such as sugarcane, beet, etc.), three kinds of crops (beverage, spices, spices), medicinal crops, dye crops, ornamental crops, fruits and other cash crops.
  cash crops usually have the characteristics of strong regionality, high economic value, high technical requirements and high commodity rate. They require more strict natural conditions and are suitable for centralized specialized production.
  cash crop refers to the crop which has strict natural conditions, complex technical requirements, high economic value of products, mainly used as industrial raw materials, so it is also called "technology crop" or industrial raw material crop. There are many kinds of cash crops, including cotton, oil, hemp, mulberry tussah, tea, sugar, vegetables, tobacco leaves, fruits, medicinal materials, etc.
  the intensive and commercialized degree of cash crop production is high, and the potential of comprehensive utilization is great, requiring higher investment in human, material and financial resources. Therefore, we must pay attention to solving the contradiction between cash crop and grain crop in land, labor and capital struggle, as well as acquisition policy, price policy and reward and sale policy, so as to promote the development of cash crop.
