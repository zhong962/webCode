Academician Zhang Wei of the Chinese Academy of Sciences: Artificial Intelligence Promotes Industrialization

The China Zhigu Conference was held in Nanjing. Zhang Yu, an honorary director of the Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Technology and Systems of Tsinghua University, attended the meeting and gave a keynote speech on the industrialization of artificial intelligence.

Academician Zhang Wei pointed out that the important reason why artificial intelligence is hot now is the high industrial demand. The demand for intelligence in the manufacturing and service industries will become an important driving force for the development of artificial intelligence. Zhang Wei said that artificial intelligence actually allows the computer to realize the three functions of people: thinking, perception and action effects. The smart manufacturing industry needs to quickly meet the needs of the market, and the market demand is constantly changing. Therefore, artificial intelligence to make decision-making under the information is very suitable and contribute to the development of the industry.

Zhang Wei also revealed that the general direction that is being done now is to solve the problems within a certain range through big data analysis, so that the industry can develop better. It is understood that this data platform has collected some research results and materials from various places. At present, many predictions about infectious disease prediction and used car sales have been made. However, Zhang Wei also said that there are big data analysis companies all over the world, but many still lack deep and intelligent analysis.
