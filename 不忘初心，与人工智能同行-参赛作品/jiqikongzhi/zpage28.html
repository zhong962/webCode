Cultural entertainment robot

A robot that makes people feel interesting, happy and fun, and can improve people's cultural quality and intelligence, is called a cultural entertainment robot. With the continuous development of robotics, cultural sports and entertainment robots have taken the trend, they not only bring joy to people, but also provide people with a platform for learning and experimentation, which has increased people's intelligence. And because of the hardware design to make the appearance anthropomorphic, the development of various software makes it have the language ability and the athletic ability, not only can sing and dance, but even communicate with people, so the cultural entertainment robot slowly enters the society and the family. in. Modern cultural entertainment robots have the external characteristics of robots for people to watch and entertain. They can be like people, like animals, like fairy tales or characters in science fiction. At the same time, it has the function of a robot, can walk or complete the action, can have language ability, can sing, and has certain perceptual ability. Culture and entertainment robots can relieve your mental fatigue while enhancing your knowledge and abilities. Cultural entertainment robots include:

1, painting and calligraphy robot

The robot consists of the robot body, the computer and the corresponding control system. Specifically, the robot's autonomy is mainly derived from four aspects: central processor, software, sensors and joints. In order for the robot to lift the portrait, there must be a flexible machine joint, and the flexibility of the movement is high, in order to accurately reproduce the appearance of the painted object. More importantly, let the robot know how to draw, it depends on the intelligence of the robot brain. The degree of computer intelligence is the computing power of the central processing unit and software. With advanced central processing units, software and joints, it is not enough. Advanced "neural fibers" are needed to transmit signals. That is the sensor of the robot. . The excellent sensor instantly transfers the intent of the central processor to the joint, allowing the robot arm to perform painting tasks without delay.

The robot is in the process of painting. I don't look up at the painter from time to time like a traditional painter, and then draw a few strokes. The robot only needs to "look at" the photographer with the camera. The robot uses facial recognition technology to allow the painting robot to extract the most characteristic lines of the human face. The excellent sensor instantly transfers the intent of the central processor to the joint, allowing the robot arm to paint without delay.

The robot uses a visual recognition system to draw a sketch, extract features from the human head contour and the facial features, and then control the robotic arm to draw a human face image.

2. Sports competition robot

On the one hand, sports robots let people have fun from robot competitions, and on the other hand, robotic fighting is also popular with many viewers. In a large number of sports competitions and physical education, this form is very attractive to the audience. Robots in sports competitions include coach robots, game robots such as ping-pong robots, badminton robots, tennis robots, soccer robots, judo robots, boxing robots, fencing robots, ski robots, water skiing robots, and skating robots.

3. Performance robot

The performance robot has flexible joints to the extreme. Dancing is their masterpiece, hundreds of movements, rich body language, they are all high! If you push it down, you can get up. Through the sensors on both sides of his feet, you can avoid obstacles, flexible hands and feet, take things, and "kick people"! They have a good sense of sound, no matter which language, as long as it is pre-recorded, the robot will decode and edit, and then pronounce it in the language of the country. They have a sound tracking function that can accompany you to talk; more than two robots can be combined to form a robot society, which can communicate and play together and dance together. People can determine the wonderful movements of animals, cartoon characters, humans, etc. that are to be imitated in advance, and some of the actors' performances of theatrical dances are edited to the performance robots, and they can perform imitative performances. We have seen that performance robots have gradually found their own stage in theme parks and exhibitions. Their movements are often designed to be very delicate, and people have done a lot of work on the material of the skin, and the performance is vivid.
