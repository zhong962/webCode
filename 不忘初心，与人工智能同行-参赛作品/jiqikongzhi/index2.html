Machine

  • Robots are the product of advanced integration of cybernetics, mechatronics, computers, materials and bionics.It has important USES in industry, medicine, agriculture, construction and even the military.Robot is a kind of machine that realizes various functions by its own power and control ability.The United Nations standardization organization adopted the definition given to robots by the American society of robotics: "a programmable and versatile operator;Or specialized systems that can be changed and programmed to perform different tasks.It can bring a lot of convenience to mankind!

Intelligent Robot

Intelligent robot is called intelligent robot because it has a quite developed "brain".In the brain is the central processing unit, a computer directly related to the person who operates it.Most of all, such a computer can perform the intended action.That's why we say these robots are real robots, even though they may look different.To understand the so-called intelligent robot in a broad sense, its most impressive is a unique "living creature" with self-control.In fact, the main organs of self-control are not as subtle and complex as real people.

Intelligent robot can understand human language, use human language to talk with the operator, in its own "consciousness" alone formed a kind of "survival" of the external environment - the detailed model of the actual situation.

Type of Robot

Sensing robot

There is no intelligent unit in the body of the sensing robot, only the executive mechanism and induction mechanism. It has the ability to use the sensing information (including vision, hearing, touch, proximity, force and infrared, ultrasound and laser, etc.) to process the sensing information and realize control and operation.Controlled by external computers, the robots used in today's mini-group matches at the World Cup of robotics fall into this category.

Interactive robot

The robot is controlled and operated by human - machine dialogue with operator or programmer through computer system.Although it has some processing and decision-making functions and can realize some functions such as track planning and simple obstacle avoidance independently, it still needs external control.For example, food ordering robot of catering industry, introduction robot of education industry and so on.

Autonomous robot

After being designed and built, the robot can complete various human tasks automatically without human intervention.Autonomous robot has modules of perception, processing, decision-making and execution on its ontology, which can act and deal with problems independently just like an autonomous person.Many countries attach great importance to the research of fully autonomous mobile robots.

Virtual robot

The control system of the virtual robot is a smart phone, which is used for the management, communication, kinematics and dynamics calculation of the system, interpolation operation and servo control processing, to achieve the given motion, and to send command information to the actuator, driving device, detection device and so on.It is an intelligent robot integrating advanced technology of multi-disciplines such as machinery, electronics, control, computer, sensor and artificial intelligence.
