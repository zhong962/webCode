Huanong invented fruit picking robot: picking up a lychee

Want to pick fresh lychee in the orchard and eat the weather is too hot? After picking the lychee, you don't have to do it yourself! At the matchmaking meeting of modern agricultural science and technology innovation achievements in Guangdong Province, exhibitors showed a smart fruit picking robot. This kind of robot can be used by two people, "one person", and has been "rearing" in some fruit cooperatives in Guangdong to carry out non-destructive picking operations on melon and fruit products. At the docking meeting, the “high-top” technology of agriculture emerged endlessly, attracting more than 500 leading agricultural enterprises to “treasure hunt”.

The person in charge of the relevant team of South China Agricultural University who developed the intelligent picking robot told the reporter that the most prominent strength of this robot is that it has the same eye-catching effect as Astro Boy. It can use binocular stereo vision to locate the fruit in the orchard and obtain the vision. Multiple random fruit targets are included, and then the mathematical planning method is used to independently plan the picking operation path, and finally the anthropomorphic finger at the end of the robot arm is extended to pick the fruit. It will not be very rude when picking the fruit, first clamp the fruit branches with the fingers, and then cut the fruit branches by cutting. Because the end of the actuator has a certain versatility, it can work on a variety of fruits, including lychee, citrus, cucumber and so on.

According to the development team, in terms of work efficiency, the robot can pick up 40 pounds of lychee per hour, which is twice the number of people. If the work site has completed the hard-bottom construction and there is a flat cement road everywhere, the robot plus the AGV car can move freely, and in some rugged gardens, it is still necessary to use a small push truck to work.
