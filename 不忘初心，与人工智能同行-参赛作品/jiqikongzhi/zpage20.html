Security robots quietly rise when industry changes are going on

At present, there are only 15 companies that have security robots in China. Recently, there are well-known companies such as Jingdong and Youbie to launch their security/patrol robots. It can be said that the security industry has become an important attempt for service robot companies to explore the direction of landing.

15 companies explored

We have compiled a list of domestic security/patrol robot layout companies and found that although this area is still in the early stages of development, there are currently 15 companies in China. In the application scenario, it also distinguishes between indoor and outdoor aspects. In terms of product form, there are also two basic forms of wheeled and orbital. Among the 15 players, we selected 6 representative representatives to see the basic ideas of these players in the development of security robots.

1. Zhongzhi Ketron Robot: a pioneer in domestic security robots

China's first security robot appeared in 2015 and was developed by Zhongzhi Ketron Robot.

The robot integrates high-definition camera, infrared thermal imaging, visual laser navigation, environmental sensor and police light device. It has various functions such as autonomous navigation, self-executing tasks, 24-hour comprehensive audio and video monitoring, and automatic alarm of abnormal conditions.

Zhongzhike's security robot products are abundant, there are security service robots for indoor use, and all-weather multi-directional security patrol robots, mobile sentry robots and home guard robots for outdoor environments, and the layout is very comprehensive. Zhongzhikechuang started earlier on security robots, and its products are more subdivided and diversified. It can be said to be the forerunner in the domestic security robot industry.

2, mainland wisdom source: layout of all terrain chassis strength

Founded in 2015, Continental Zhiyuan is mainly engaged in mobile robot chassis, and the typical product is security robot - Andi. The Andi security inspection robot of Continental Zhiyuan has three highlights: all-terrain site, liftable body and 360-degree video + thermal imaging + 4 recorded video real-time transmission. Since the core positioning of the mainland Zhiyuan is to produce robotic chassis, Andi's most prominent advantage is that it can achieve all-terrain walking.

At present, Continental Zhiyuan only launches a robot product of Andi Robot. Its chassis design is the core technology of Continental Zhiyuan. Therefore, it also enables Andi to cover all terrain, which is crucial for the expansion of its application scenarios.

3, Jingdong inspection robot: to solve the need for their own warehouse inspection

In recent years, Jingdong's layout on robots has been frequent and extensive. Recently, Jingdong's second-generation inspection robot was officially launched in Beijing's “Asian No.1” in Jingdong. This product is an upgraded version of Jingdong's first-generation patrol robot "Walley". From inspection methods to information collection to alarm linkage, etc., the product performance has been comprehensively improved, and the inspection route can be planned through self-built maps. It can also perform 360° all-round video surveillance and upload inspection information in real time. At the same time, it can also be based on thermal imaging, temperature, humidity, smoke sensor information, fusion judgment of fire hazard level, and self-calling nearby patrol robot companions to fire.

Jingdong's inspection robots were developed for the needs of their own warehouses. However, according to industry insiders, Jingdong's inspection robots should be from the hands of partners and not sold. But on the other hand, it also indicates that the inspection robot will have a very large development space in the closed scene, especially in the e-commerce warehouse.

4, the country from the robot: build a track-type, wheeled multi-product line

The company was founded in 2011 and its products are mainly for electric power, automobile, rubber tires, logistics, coal, railway and other industries. The products are also very rich, ranging from standard wheeled inspection robots to wheeled inventory and wheeled inspection robots, as well as orbital inspection robots.

The above picture is the country's intelligent security robot product, which integrates a variety of intelligent technologies such as autonomous sports, audio and video intelligent identification, hazard warning, intelligent parking space management, etc., can help people complete the monitoring and patrol security work of important places, and can be combined with fixed The monitoring and analysis of environmental big data constitutes a complete security monitoring system, effectively avoiding the various drawbacks of manual patrol, and meeting the security protection requirements of smart cities, large warehousing, smart communities, various commercial complexes and other places.

Since the country has positioned itself in the power, logistics, railway and other industries since the beginning, it has become more aware of the needs of users. Its products are also very rich, ranging from wheeled to rail.

5, Shenzhou Yunhai: differentiated appearance focused on outdoor inspection

Shenzhou Yunhai was established in 2015, and currently has three products: Ava series service robot, inspection robot and cleaning robot.

The Ava inspection robot is suitable for outdoor patrol inspection missions and can be widely used in open areas such as factories and airports. It is characterized by being able to work all the time around the clock, greatly assisting security personnel to reduce blind spots in inspection, reducing abnormal response time, reducing unsupervised time periods, improving inspection efficiency and improving security quality. Professional function, Ava inspection robot can achieve garbage monitoring, vehicle management, environmental monitoring, active warning and other functions, and can also be linked with the background data and analysis.

6, Avenue Zhichuang: expand the application scenario through the open SDK

Founded in 2015, Avenue Zhichuang is a company specializing in machine vision technology development and application. One year after its establishment, Avenue Zhichuang launched the security inspection robot, the "e-patrol" machine sheriff. The robot is equipped with 360-degree real-time panoramic monitoring, and combines thermal imaging system, ultrasonic array, infrared array, TOF depth camera and planar lidar multi-sensor to collect environmental information.

Combined with hardware products, Avenue Zhichuang also set up a corresponding intelligent control center to collect real-time video surveillance, robot status and position feedback, and on-site environmental information, and analyze the video in real time through deep learning algorithms to monitor and alert abnormal conditions. The follow-up will also seamlessly connect to existing community management systems through an open SDK interface. Gradually form a dynamic security service operation system for security robots and security monitoring systems.

In addition, the Kosovo-invested Xianzhi robot, the Haier holding subsidiary Klude robot, the Junwang robot invested by Zhongke Chuangke College, and the newly established Lulu robot are also developing security robots. We also learned that Ubisoft will also launch security inspection robot products at this year's World Robotics Conference.
