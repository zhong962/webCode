Guangzhou economic development zone and deep eye control technology AI negotiations to promote the implementation of national policies

On the morning of June 27, Shanghai Eye Control Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Eye Control Technology") welcomed Zhou Jingao, Vice Minister of Organization Department of Guangzhou Huangpu District Committee, Cui Ling, Director of Science and Technology Merchants Department of Guangzhou Development Zone Science and Technology Innovation Bureau, Yang Shengqiang, Talents Office of Organization Department of Guangzhou Huangpu District Committee, and Guangzhou Development Zone Science and Technology Innovation Bureau. Wujin Science and Technology Investment Department, Cai Guoshuai, Project Manager of Innovation and Entrepreneurship Service Center of Guangzhou Development Zone (1000 People Plan), Bufan, Director of Investment Management Department of Guangzhou Development Zone Talent Work Group, and Liu Huanmin, Investment Manager of Guangzhou Development Zone Industrial Fund Investment Group came to discuss AI project. We sincerely invite Eye Control Technology to be stationed in Guangzhou of Huangpu District to open the project. In the development area, artificial intelligence projects have been launched to help promote the implementation of the national "1000 person program" project. Zhou Kangming, founder, chairman and general manager of Eye Control Technology, Wang Chunyan, co-founder of Eye Control Technology, and Liang Weibin, vice-president of Eye Control Technology, were interviewed by senior executives and introduced the latest products of Eye Control Technology.

During the business negotiation, Chairman Zhou Kangming showed the guests the latest research and development and application of artificial intelligence technology of eye control technology, reported the outstanding achievements of eye control technology in the field of artificial intelligence, and was highly recognized by the government leaders. In addition, Vice Minister Zhou Jingao sincerely invited Eye Control Technology to lead the latest AI product projects to the Guangzhou Development Zone in Huangpu District to participate in the project inspection. After the inspection, the government will give tax, talent and other policy preferences.

Zhou Jingao, deputy director of the Organizational Department of the Guangzhou Huangpu District Committee, said, "The Guangzhou Economic Development Zone is a five-zone integration, an administrative team, and called the"Guangzhou Development Zone of the Huangpu District". The five major industries in the development zone are biology, medicine and artificial intelligence. In 2017, the Development Zone GDP3500 billion, fiscal and taxation revenue reached about 100000000000, the National Development Zone ranked first. " It is understood that the development zone has Alibaba, Giant Network, Jingchi Technology and other artificial intelligence enterprises have entered the zone.

Eye-control technology as a continuous innovation of artificial intelligence enterprises, since its establishment in 2009, is committed to "become the most innovative artificial intelligence enterprises". At present, it has 38 independent software intellectual property rights, 60 invention patents, a quality system certification, some of the technology has been in the world's leading level, in the recent global face recognition. In the MegaFace library, eye recognition technology has gained third of the world's face recognition technology.

Zhou Kangming, chairman of the board, said, "Eye-control technology will continue to innovate ideas and strategies to facilitate the development and application of new technologies. We are looking forward to seeing eye-control technology bring the latest AI project to the Guangzhou Development Zone in Huangpu District, and help Huangpu District to promote the implementation of the National "Thousand People Plan" project.
