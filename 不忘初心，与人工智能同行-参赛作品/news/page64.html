Two sessions to mention artificial intelligence, the latest policy of AI industry in all parts of the country in 2018

In March 5, 2018, Premier Li Keqiang pointed out in the 2018 State Council's government work report that we should strengthen the research and application of the new generation of artificial intelligence, promote the "Internet +" in the fields of health care, pension, education, culture, sports and other fields, develop intelligent industries and expand intelligent life.

This is not the first time that artificial intelligence has entered the "Government Work Report". In the government work report of 2017, the Premier mentioned that "on the one hand, we should accelerate the cultivation of new materials, artificial intelligence, integrated circuits, biopharmaceuticals, the fifth generation of mobile communications and other emerging industries, on the other hand, we should apply large data, cloud computing, Internet of Things, and so on." We should accelerate the transformation and upgrading of traditional industries, and develop intelligent manufacturing as the main direction of attack.

Two consecutive years of government work reports mention artificial intelligence, can be seen in the artificial intelligence has become an important driving force for the development of science and technology in today's environment, the government's determination to raise artificial intelligence to the national will.

Policy emphasis shifted from artificial intelligence to technology and industry integration.

In addition, since 2017, the focus of the policy has shifted from artificial intelligence technology to the integration of technology and industry, especially in July 2017, the State Council issued the "New Generation of Artificial Intelligence Development Plan" clearly pointed out that "to accelerate the depth of application of artificial intelligence".

From the incomplete summary of the speeches of the two sessions in 2018, it can be seen that the integration of AI + industry will be the focus of the future, including the Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Civil Affairs and other official departments and Baidu, Tencent, Lenovo and other non-governmental representatives, have proposed artificial intelligence + industry, artificial intelligence + medical...

2018: artificial intelligence local level policy year

According to the summary of the Institute of Prospective Industry Research, only in the first three months of 2018, six provinces and cities, including Guangdong, Tianjin, Liaoning, Heilongjiang, Fujian and Sichuan, issued artificial intelligence plans. In addition, the provinces and cities that issued policies last year, by March 2018, 15 provinces and municipalities in China have issued artificial intelligence plans, of which 12 have formulated specific industrial scale development goals.

In addition, 27 provinces and cities mentioned artificial intelligence in the "Internet +" plan, 22 provinces and cities set up "artificial intelligence special" in the strategic emerging industry planning, 19 provinces and cities mentioned artificial intelligence in big data planning, and 9 provinces and cities set up "human intelligence chapter" in the scientific and technological innovation plan.

For AI industry, 2017 is a policy year at the national level, 2018 will be a policy year at the local level. In the next few months, the remaining 16 provinces and cities that have not yet had AI planning will be released one after another. According to the incomplete understanding of the Institute of Prospective Industry, Jiangsu is in the process of compiling. Shandong, Henan, Shaanxi and other provinces and cities.

429 billion VS 150 billion, 2020, the prospect of AI is far beyond imagination!

According to the Foresight Industry Research Institute's "Global Artificial Intelligence Chip Industry Market Foresight and Investment Strategic Planning Analysis Report 2018-2023", the 12 provinces and municipalities have formulated specific industrial scale development goals. The scale targets of these 12 provinces and municipalities by 2020 have reached 429 billion yuan, far exceeding the national level target of 150 billion yuan.
