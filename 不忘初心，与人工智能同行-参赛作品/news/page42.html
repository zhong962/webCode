China's AI develops into fast lane

In June 26th, the State Council held a conference on the 2018 China International Intelligent Industry Expo. Tang Liangzhi, mayor of Chongqing Municipal Government, introduced at the meeting that the first "Intelligence Expo" will be held in Chongqing from August 23 to 25, with the theme of "Intelligence: Enabling Economy and Enriching Life". At present, the preparatory work of the "intelligence Expo" is proceeding in a solid and orderly manner.

It is understood that the China International Intelligent Industry Expo is jointly sponsored by the Chongqing Municipal People's Government, the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Chinese Academy of Engineering and the Chinese Association for Science and Technology, and will be held annually in Chongqing from this year on.

The exhibition will focus on professionalism, internationalization and experience, hold the Summit of Large Data Intelligence, and set up four thematic exhibition areas: comprehensive exhibition, large enterprise exhibition, Innovation Exhibition and special exhibition. The exhibition area is about 150,000 square meters. Five major competitions, such as unmanned driving, black technology selection, seven thematic forums, such as semiconductors and artificial intelligence, two thematic events, the Global Digital Economy 100 Forum and the International Friendly City Mayors Round Table, and eight professional forums, such as the Alibaba Chongqing Summit and Tencent Cloud + Future, will be held. Tang Liangzhi said.

At the same time, this "Intelligent Expo" will focus on large data intelligence to lead innovation and development, centralize the release of a number of cutting-edge technological achievements, display a number of "black technology" products, showing the future scene of intelligent life.

Zhou Changkui, a member of the Party group of the Ministry of Science and Technology, said that after years of continuous accumulation, China has made important progress in the field of artificial intelligence. Among them, the publication of international scientific papers and the authorization of invention patents have ranked second in the world, and some key technologies in some fields have achieved important breakthroughs, such as speech recognition, visual recognition technology in the world's leading position, self-adaptive learning, intuitive perception, comprehensive reasoning, hybrid intelligence, swarm intelligence and so on. Initially with the development capacity, Chinese information processing, intelligent monitoring, biometric identification, industrial robots, service robots, unmanned pilots have gradually entered the stage of practical application, artificial intelligence innovation and entrepreneurship are increasingly active, a number of leading enterprises accelerate the growth of international attention and recognition. . Wang Xinzhe, chief economist of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, pointed out that the development of artificial intelligence in China has entered a fast lane.
