The third bank financial technology summit will lead the future intelligently.

In April 9, 2018, China Construction Bank "unmanned bank" officially appeared in Jiujiang Road, Shanghai. It is understood that this is the first unmanned bank in China's banking industry. This is also the first scenic sharing place that combines banking, trading and entertainment with bookstores and brand stores. It has completely changed people's impression of the stylization and specialization of traditional banking outlets and formally opened up artificial intelligence in the traditional banking industry. The curtain of application.

Then, on April 20, ABC's first national financial technology experience store, DIY Intelligent Bank, unveiled at the Liberation Monument in Chongqing. Unlike traditional bank outlets, DIY Intelligent Bank, located in the center of the Liberation Monument in Chongqing, is the first DIY Intelligent Bank in China, whether it is an interactive intelligent display window with a column outside the outlet or an increase inside the outlet. The design and layout of each function of DIY Intelligent Bank highlights the concepts and practices of technology, the future and interactive intelligent banks.
