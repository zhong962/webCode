The latest development trend of artificial intelligence in 2018

AI (Artificial Intelligence), the abbreviation of artificial intelligence. It is a new technical science to study and develop the theory, method, technology and application system of human intelligence for simulation, extension and extension. Next, let's talk about the development trend of AI.

Potential applications in vertical field

The artificial intelligence market has great potential in the vertical fields of retail, transportation and automation, manufacturing and agriculture. The main factor driving the market is the application and construction of AI technology in various vertical fields of end users, especially whether it can provide more humanized services for consumers.

There are many factors affecting the development of the artificial intelligence market, such as the perfect IT infrastructure, the popularity of smart phones and smart wearable devices and so on. Among them, natural language processing (NLP) is particularly critical. With the advancement of natural language processing technology, the service field of artificial intelligence will be greatly expanded, thus stimulating the development of the consumer market.

The key to development is the chip.

The core of AI chip is semiconductor and algorithm. AI hardware must satisfy the requirements of fast instruction cycle, low function consumption, and combination with depth learning algorithm. At present, AI chips include GPU, DSP, ASIC, FPGA and neural chip. GPU is faster than FPGA in computing speed, and FPGA is better than GPU in power efficiency, so the choice of AI hardware depends on the needs of product vendors. Take Apple's FaceID face recognition application for example, which integrates up to eight components, requires higher power efficiency, and pays attention to the user's biometric experience, so Apple finally chose the FPGA.

Autonomous learning is the goal.

AI "brain" becomes smart in stages, from machine learning to deep learning, and then to autonomous learning. At present, AI is still in the stage of machine learning and deep learning from a global perspective. To achieve autonomous learning, four key problems need to be solved. The first is to create an AI platform for autonomous machines, and to provide a virtual environment for Autonomous machines to learn independently; the second is to conform to the physical laws, collision, pressure, and effect must be the same as the real world; the third is to put the AI "brain" in the framework of autonomous machines; the fourth is to establish a virtual environment. World entrance (VR).
