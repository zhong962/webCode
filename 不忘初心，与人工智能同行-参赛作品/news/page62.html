Development of artificial intelligence: 2018 policy review of AI industry

In 2017, the field of artificial intelligence is flourishing. The research on artificial intelligence technology in China mainly focuses on computer vision, natural language processing, and machine learning. China's huge consumer market can spawn countless sub-fields. The penetration of the Internet into various industries provides the most authentic reference for AI applications, and the application scenario of AI will only be broader than that of the Internet.

The state has always maintained close attention to the artificial intelligence industry, and relevant policies have been issued frequently to remove obstacles for the development of artificial intelligence industry. At present, the new generation of artificial intelligence development planning, marking the completion of the top-level design framework, the artificial intelligence industry will speed up the development of policy support.
