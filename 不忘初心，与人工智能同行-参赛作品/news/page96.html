Two, intelligent financial customer service

The important goal of GSP Finance is to provide users with efficient and personalized customer service experience. Intelligent customer service with AI technology as the core plays an important role in improving the user experience.

The essence of intelligent customer service is to fully understand the intention of users, accurately find the matching knowledge points in the knowledge base, so as to answer user questions or provide solutions. The whole intelligent customer service is built around the user problem, and the problem similarity calculation is the core technology that runs through the construction of intelligent customer service knowledge base, online problem matching, full-link data operation and almost all links. The development of this technology and the construction of related data will certainly promote the progress of the entire customer service industry. Step.

At the core of the algorithm development, that is, in the process of building an artificial intelligence engine, ant golden suit has also encountered many challenges, such as making machine learning engine can quickly re-judge, how to face very small samples to achieve learning and prediction.

"The first thing we do here is on intelligent customer service, hoping to make Alipay more intelligent through multiple rounds of dialogue, and understand the meaning behind the dialogue." Lacquer yuan added.

This is very important in intelligent customer service, but behind it is not simple.

First of all, the most important point of customer service is to understand the user's intentions, and then in the knowledge base to match the relevant knowledge points. At present, the most popular matching method in industry is to calculate the similarity between the user's questions and all the questions in the knowledge base one by one, take the most similar questions as the user's intention, and then return its answers to the user.

Besides, similarity calculation is also meaningful for all aspects of customer service. For example, the knowledge base is actually used for text-based automatic mining, which is also based on computing the similarity between any two texts, or the distance between the two high-dimensional spaces.

Similarity computing is also a very core and basic problem in all aspects of data operation, including search recommendation, dialogue and other fields.

At present, the biggest challenge and problem of NLP similarity calculation is precisely that there are a lot of diversity problems in intelligent customer service, including dialects, spoken language and various expressions of the different places, the matching of the problem put forward quite high requirements. However, the progress of NLP mainly focuses on affective analysis and translation. In these scenarios, most of the time, it is in a relatively fixed data set, which can get good results on a relatively simple and clean data set.

We often say in theory what we can do if we have large data, but it is difficult to achieve such an ideal environment in the actual engineering process. Whether it is transfer learning or unsupervised learning, how to use fewer samples to achieve higher accuracy, faster recognition rate, and improve the generalization ability of machine learning, are very challenging issues in the next stage.
