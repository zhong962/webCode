2018 global intelligence + Education Summit unveiled in Shanghai

Since last year, "artificial intelligence" has become a new outlet, 2018 is known as "the first year of the artificial intelligence industry landing". With the promotion of national policies and the development of technology, artificial intelligence began to be applied in various industries, education industry is no exception.

In 2017, the State Council issued the "New Generation of Artificial Intelligence Development Plan", which mentioned in the field of education, will be established online intelligent education platform, gradually improve the system of artificial intelligence education; encourage the implementation of national intelligent education projects, and gradually promote programming education.

On April 10, 2018, the Ministry of Education published a circular on the publication of the Action Plan for Artificial Intelligence Innovation in Colleges and Universities. The Plan of Action proposes to form a new training mode of "Artificial Intelligence + X" composite specialty, to build 100 "Artificial Intelligence + X" composite specialties by 2020, and to establish 50 AI colleges, research institutes or cross-research centers.

Under the influence of policy and technology, artificial intelligence has been fully developed in the education industry in recent years. Adaptive learning, educational robots, AI teachers and so on have become hot spots in the education industry. More and more educational enterprises begin to explore the application scenarios of artificial intelligence. In this context, the "2018 Global Intelligence + New Business Summit" jointly sponsored by the Shanghai Economic and Information Committee, the Shanghai Commercial Committee and the Shanghai Changning District People's Government, the Shanghai Changning Youth Federation and Euro Corporation will be held at the Shanghai Changning World Trade Exhibition Hall from June 13 to 15, 2018.

On June 15, the "Intelligence + Education Summit" as one of the summit sessions will invite many education industry leaders, practitioners and industry followers to discuss the "AI + education" and other issues of concern for the development of many industries.

The "Intelligence + Education Summit" will take the form of seven keynote speeches + one round table forum. Euro 100 million will issue a research report on programming education before the meeting begins. Seven keynote speeches will focus on the following topics:

First, AI is coming. What are the dangers and opportunities faced by the education industry?

Two, AI empowering K12, how to fight in the second half of online counseling? ;

Three, algorithms and data are changing the way of education logic and education.

Four, adaptive learning makes it possible to teach students in accordance with their aptitude.

Five, AI Customization: AI application in preschool education industry

Six, online education ushered in the 4 era, AI innovation in the field of quality education.

Seven. AI landing education to explore the development potential of emotion recognition technology.

Up to now, the guests who have been confirmed to attend the "Intelligence + Education Summit" include Zhang Yi, CEO of Haifeng Education, Cheng Wen-cheng, founder of school education, Li Haoyang, CEO of Growth Protection, Wang Haoping, CEO of small station education, and Guo Changchen, CEO of Zhitong. In addition, good future president Bai Yunfeng, genuine fund partner Zheng Chaoyu, onion mathematics CEO Yang Linfeng and many other heavyweight guests are inviting confirmation.

At the summit site, there will be an exhibition area of nearly 2,000 square meters. During the summit, the latest products and services of artificial intelligence will be displayed, so that ordinary audiences can have a real sense of participation and more sense of gain. The summit will support innovative AI companies and give free showcases and reports on qualified AI products.
