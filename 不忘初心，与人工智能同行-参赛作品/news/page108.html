UAV matchmaking? China's amazing technology

A video of Chinese engineers building suspension bridges with unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) has been broadcast on foreign social media recently, with a volume of more than 100,000. Netizens from all over the world are astonishing. The crooked nuts have thumbs up to praise China's ultra high technology.

At that time, the details are like this, China is a world-wide infrastructure power, since the early days of the founding of the People's Republic to the present, countless bridges leap north and south, communicating the two places that could not have been connected. The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is sung by Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, and symbolizes the resurgence of infrastructure construction such as bridges. And the engineers in the incident suddenly fantasized about the portability of the UAV, maneuvering the UAV to pull suspension cables across the Chishui River to the towers on both sides.

As UAVs continue to enter our lives and work, more and more places can see them. The UAV traction bridge suspension is also a small part of the UAV application, but greatly improved the efficiency of work, reducing a lot of unnecessary things. In addition to this function, the most common is to check the bridge, engineers now only need to play remote control aircraft like the bridge can complete the monitoring. It's easier, safer and more efficient than the traditional way.

The progress of the times is the impetus of scientific and technological forces, UAVs bring about improvements in all aspects, the era of UAVs has come. At the same time, we also look forward to UAV and other technology products can be used in more traditional industries, open the door ahead of time!
