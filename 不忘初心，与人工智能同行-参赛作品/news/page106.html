Raymarine launches new unmanned aerial imaging applications

Raymarine Rezon announced the launch of the Axiom UAV, a new unmanned aerial imaging application for Axiom, Axiom Pro, and Axiom XL Multifunctional Display (MFD). The new LightHouse 3.6 OS update for Axiom MFD also provides integration with DJI Mavic Pro UAV.

Axiom UAV applications automatically perform many common tasks, including launching, airborne imaging, in-flight navigation, and returning ships. This additional aerial imaging platform is integrated on the ship, enabling fishing and cruise ships to benefit.

The functions include:

Reconnaissance in front of the boat. When equipped with an optional polarizing lens, Mavic Pro's 4K UHD camera system can see fish, bait, weed lines, sand bars, coral reefs, etc. This technology allowed many of the fish surveillance advantages of small vessels to be limited to very specific sizes and costs of vessels.

The Fish-On function of the_Axiom drone immediately starts the drone, starts video recording, and automatically sends the camera into orbit around the ship. Convenient screen control allows users to fine-tune the UAV's altitude, orbit radius, orbit speed, and camera angle from Raymarine Axiom or Axiom Pro screens.

View images on Raymarine Axiom or Axiom Pro MFD screen. Capturing video, still images, and other images visible from the air can be easily saved to Mavic's onboard memory card.

Flight point or chart location. The UAV GOTO command on the screen of Axiom or Axiom Pro chartplotter will be sent to the UAV. Using GPS navigation, Mavic Pro will fly to the selected precise location and hovering at the station. The air user can see the UAV and its course, speed, altitude and camera direction on the navigation map.

Axiom UAV applications offer a variety of options to control Mavic Pro in flight, including the automatic "return" function. In addition to the automatic flight mode, the Axiom UAV also provides a virtual screen joystick for manual flight. Users can also use DJI Mavic Pro joystick controller to control at any time.
