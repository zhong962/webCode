2018 global intelligence Fair opens, cool Jiale is selected for AI innovation application 50

In May 10th, the 2018 World Expo on artificial intelligence products was opened in Suzhou. The Expo is under the guidance of the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the People's Government of Jiangsu Province, sponsored by the Suzhou People's Government and hosted by the Suzhou Industrial Park Administrative Committee. The Expo aims to create a high-standard, high-tech and high-tech AI annual grand meeting.

The theme of this conference is Intelligent Experience, Intelligent Life, focusing on the application of products, focusing on the display of terminal products based on artificial intelligence technology and algorithms. 150 enterprises and artificial intelligence institutions from 10 countries participated in the exhibition. The exhibition area is about 18,000 square meters, showing more than 1,000 kinds of artificial intelligence products.
