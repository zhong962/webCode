Gao Xiaosong interviewed Bill Gates: the problem of AI is not yet solved.

It is understood that 10 years ago, Buffett gave most of his wealth to the Gates Foundation to fight disease and reduce inequality. At the end of last year, Buffett sent a letter to the Gates foundation, urging the foundation to review its work. So the Gates Foundation responded to Buffett in an open letter, announcing the benefits of Buffett's special "investment" 10 years ago. The conversation between Mr. Gao Xiaosong and Mr. Bill Gates began.

In an interview with Bill Gates, Gao Xiaosong and Bill Gates talked a lot about artificial intelligence. Bill Gates said that artificial intelligence will be used in all areas of society to help children tap their intellectual potential. "Artificial intelligence has a very amazing impact," Gates said. "Go is a milestone. It's growing faster than we expected and has a positive impact.

Bill Gates pointed out that artificial intelligence has made great progress in visual and phonetic abilities, but AI still needs to work hard on "reading" abilities. He mentioned that AI still can't read a book or pass an exam or operate an experiment, which is an unsolved problem.
