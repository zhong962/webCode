2018 AI World Cup war! Who will dominate in computer vision?

According to the recently published "IDC 2018 China Computer Vision Application Market Research", there are four small dragons in the field of artificial intelligence computer vision: Shangtang Technology, Yuncong Technology, Broadview Technology and Yitu Technology. Many people think that 2018 will be a year when computer vision seize the industry and further escalate the war. Before that, however, the four dragons have chosen their own direction and decided their future.

The two most distinctive unicorns of the four dragons are Shangtang Technology of the Financing School and Yuncong Technology of the Practical School.

Financing wild faction: Shang Tang Technology

Let's start with the famous Shang Tang technology.

Shangtang Technology and China Mobile Online Services Limited (China Mobile Online) held a strategic cooperation agreement signing ceremony in Beijing to jointly accelerate the development of the artificial intelligence market. According to Soup from the media, recently released millet 8 and Oneplus 8 are also using Soup technology (including the Millet 8 Exploratory Edition). But according to Moving Point Technology and other media reports, Millet 8 Exploratory 3D structured light technology was developed independently by Israel's Mantis Vision.

In April 2018 -5, within a month and a half, Shang Tang completed two rounds of financing. Financed up to $1 billion 220 million. Here is the question: why do companies need to raise large sums of money at such a high frequency?

Pragmatic literature and Art: cloud from technology

At the same time, it is also the Chinese AI enterprise that has the most self-control in the face of temptation. Except for Cloud Technologies, other companies have overseas capital, and Cloud Technologies has accepted only domestic capital from start to finish.

Although the initiative to give up a large part of capital intervention, cloud from the ability to control the industry can not be underestimated. On the "Top 100 Artificial Intelligence Business Landing List of China in 2018" just released by Euro 100, Yuncong and Shangtang are among the top companies in the forecast revenue of 1-2 billion yuan in 2018.

Perhaps the ability to control the industry has little to do with financing.

The concern of the state has never been linked to financing.

From May 28 to June 1, during the meeting of the academicians'Congress of the two academies, the designated Journal of the congress, China Science Journal, devoted a large amount of space to the introduction of Yuncong's achievements in the industrialization of science and technology in the second edition.
