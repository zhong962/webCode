At the World Artificial Intelligence Conference, nearly 100 Chinese and foreign companies will focus on the AI ​​application experience.

On August 18th, the 2018 World Artificial Intelligence Conference, which received much attention from all walks of life, ushered in a countdown of 30 days. On the same day, the image logo of the 2018 World Artificial Intelligence Conference and the first batch of strategic partners were officially released.

As a window of China's dialogue with the world in the field of artificial intelligence, Shanghai will seize the opportunity to hold this conference, promote the development of a new generation of artificial intelligence, and take the lead in building the artificial intelligence highland on behalf of the country.

High-end, international, professional, market-oriented

The image of the 2018 World Artificial Intelligence Conference is based on the first letter "WAIC" of the English "World Artificial Intelligence Conference" of the World Artificial Intelligence Conference. The nine core elements above the letter icon represent the philosophy of artificial intelligence, cognitive science, mathematics, Computer science, information theory, cybernetics, neurophysiology, psychology, uncertainty theory and other nine university fields, combined with the dynamic design, symbolizes the rapid development of the world's artificial intelligence industry and the infinite possibilities of the future.

The 2018 World Artificial Intelligence Conference, co-sponsored by the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the National Network Office, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Chinese Academy of Engineering and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, was held in Shanghai on the 17th of September this year. The theme of the conference is “A new era of artificial intelligence empowerment”. It is based on the judgment of global artificial intelligence technology, industry and application development trends. From Shanghai to the global artificial intelligence community, an invitation is invited, and people of insight from all walks of life gather in Shanghai. Explore the vision of a new generation of artificial intelligence. The main contents include how to seize opportunities, meet challenges, and benefit all mankind; how to make AI empower the industrial economy and social life, transform the real world; how to promote the development of new theories and technologies, and meet the new changes that are ready to be launched, etc. .

According to Chen Mingbo, director of the Municipal Economic Information Commission, the conference is characterized by "high-end, international, professional, and market-oriented." High-end is to gather the latest cutting-edge applications, invite the most influential entrepreneurs and scientists in the global AI field to conduct high-end dialogue. Internationalization means standing in the perspective of the human world to define and deeply explore the future of AI and jointly plan the vision of AI. Specialization is the highest level conference in the international standard, gathering domestic and foreign AI leading enterprises and top industry talents, providing professional display from industry solutions to excellent life applications. Marketization is to invite many industry leaders to participate in the industry, and to create an industrial ecology of “industry, industry, research and research”.

Seven “AI+” themes reflect the most cutting-edge applications

The conference paid special attention to the application experience and live demonstration. According to the geographical conditions of Longhua Heliport and Tanker Art Park, the organizers will organize a centralized, scene-based and immersive AI application experience display according to local conditions and preferably nearly 100 Chinese and foreign enterprises. A total of seven “AI+” themes will be designed. It embodies the concept that technology innovation is closely related to industry application.

The “AI+Traffic” application experience will reflect “car-way collaboration and intelligent interoperability” through “millisecond security” of driverless vehicles and technological innovations such as drone service “smart city”. The “AI+Finance” application experience will be carried out around unmanned banks, personal wealth management, and smart venture capital, reflecting “intelligent risk control and financial transformation”. The “AI+Retail” application experience will be launched around unmanned supermarkets and unmanned containers, reflecting “smart new retail and new consumption ecology”. The “AI+Health” application experience demonstrates the combination of artificial intelligence and medical health through surgical robots, rehabilitation robots, medical records, etc., reflecting “optimizing resources and improving people's livelihood”. The “AI+Smart” application experience demonstrates the combination of artificial intelligence and industrial manufacturing through virtual visualization factories, industrial intelligence, smart homes, etc., reflecting “energy efficient, smart production”. The “AI+ Education” application experience allows the audience to experience a variety of artificial intelligence education and entertainment methods, embodying “education and learning, personalized learning”. “AI+ Services” is distributed throughout the venues, demonstrating that AI helps humans achieve some goals, bring a better life, and embody “human-computer interaction, by your side”.

At present, more than 260 speakers have been confirmed to attend the conference, including more than 50 academic leaders who have won the Turing Award and Nobel Prize, more than 100 industry leaders, and leaders of international organizations and foreign governments. More than 150 companies participate in AI application experiences and exhibitions.

The first batch of strategic partners released

In addition, the theme of the conference center exhibition area is “City Brain + Smart Core + Innovative Algorithm”, which reflects the three elements of “data, computing power, algorithm”, including Google, ARM, Huawei, Ali, Tencent, Shang Tang, etc. Chinese and foreign companies participated in the event. During the conference, relevant enterprises were also invited to hold corresponding activities such as new product and new technology release and project signing. As an important part of the conference, the 2018 World Artificial Intelligence Innovation Competition was organized by the Municipal Economic Information Development Research Center. It was officially launched on June 13th with the theme of “Enlightening Intelligence and Setting the Future”, focusing on “Human-Computer Interaction, The four directions of driverless, medical innovation and intelligent robots are for recruiting teams from all over the world. The contest will select their respective winning projects at the end of August and hold an award ceremony during the conference.
