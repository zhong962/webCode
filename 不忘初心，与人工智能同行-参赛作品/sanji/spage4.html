In-depth analysis of artificial intelligence technology and applications to effectively understand the artificial intelligence industry chain!

Internet of technology, big data, artificial intelligence and other technical hot words seem to have appeared in the headlines every day in the past few years. Which technology has a greater role in promoting the development of human society? I don’t know if anyone has this. The doubts, in fact, they are all future trends, and there is no contradiction between them.

Some people say that in the era of the Internet of Everything, big data is the new oil of the future. How do you understand this sentence? We know that IoT technology is mainly to achieve the fusion of the physical world (real world) and the information world, so as to obtain massive, dynamic, polymorphic and related data in the real world. The big data concept is an abstract description of these data, usually refers to the scale beyond the traditional meaning, and the software tools are difficult to capture, store, manage and analyze the data. Furthermore, it is artificial intelligence technology, which is a technology based on a large amount of data, allowing the machine to think like a human.

When it comes to artificial intelligence, you have to mention machine learning and deep learning. Many people don't know much about this. Here I use a graph that represents a collection to visually describe the relationship between the three.

1. Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence technology was proposed in 1956 and is considered to be one of the three cutting-edge technologies of the 21st century. It is a simulation of the information process of human thinking and consciousness. The concept of artificial intelligence is broad and can be understood on the surface to make machines think and solve problems like people. In fact, the core technology of artificial intelligence includes many aspects, reasoning, knowledge, planning, learning, communication, perception, movement and ability to manipulate objects.

Usually, we divide artificial intelligence into the following three categories according to their strength:

Weak artificial intelligence: Expertise in one aspect of artificial intelligence. For example, there is artificial intelligence that can defeat the chess world champion, but it only plays chess. You have to ask how it can better store data on the hard disk. It doesn't know how to answer you.

Strong artificial intelligence: artificial intelligence at the human level. Strong artificial intelligence refers to the artificial intelligence that can be compared with human beings in all aspects. Human capable brain power can do it all. Creating strong artificial intelligence is much harder than creating weak artificial intelligence.

Ultra-artificial intelligence: Super artificial intelligence can be stronger than humans in all aspects, or it can be several times stronger than human beings in all aspects. In the end, there is no unified statement in the industry.

There are two different ways in which artificial intelligence can be implemented on a computer. One is to use traditional programming techniques to make the system intelligent, regardless of whether its specific structure is similar or identical to the human brain. The other is the simulation method, which not only depends on the final result, but also requires its structure to be similar or identical to the human brain.

2, machine learning

Machine learning examines how computers are based on data learning. One of its main areas of research is that computer programs automatically learn to identify complex patterns based on data and make intelligent decisions. Machine learning is a fast-growing discipline. Here, we introduce some classic, machine learning problems related to data mining.

Supervised learning: basically synonymous with classification. The supervision in learning comes from the marked instances in the training data set.

Unsupervised learning: essentially synonymous with clustering. The learning process is unsupervised because the input instance has no class tag.

Semi-supervised learning: A type of machine learning technique that uses both labeled and unmarked instances when learning a model. In one approach, an instance of a tag is used to learn the class model, while an unmarked instance is used to improve the class boundary.

Active Learning: A machine learning method that allows users to take an active role in the learning process. Active learning methods may require users to tag instances that may be from unmarked instance sets or synthesized by a learning program. Given the number of instances that can be marked, the goal is to improve the quality of the model by proactively acquiring knowledge from the user.

3. Deep learning

Deep learning is a new field in machine learning research. Its motivation is to build and simulate a neural network for human brain analysis and learning. It mimics the mechanism of the human brain to interpret data such as images, sounds and texts. The concept of deep learning stems from the study of artificial neural networks. A multilayer perceptron with multiple hidden layers is a deep learning structure. Deep learning combines low-level features to form more abstract high-level representation attribute categories or features to discover distributed feature representations of data. The obscure concept is slightly incomprehensible, but behind its high cold, there are far-reaching application scenarios and futures.

At the moment, machines trained in deep learning techniques are better at identifying images than humans, such as recognizing cats, identifying cancer cell features in blood, and identifying tumors in MRI scans.

Usually, we divide the artificial intelligence industry chain into three core links: artificial intelligence basic technology, artificial intelligence algorithm platform and artificial intelligence application. Let us then interpret the artificial intelligence industry chain.

1. Artificial intelligence basic technology

The basic technology of artificial intelligence is mainly big data management and cloud computing. After several years of development, domestic big data management and cloud computing technology have gradually changed from a new field to a basic platform for mass service. The basic technology provides the basic back-end guarantee for the realization of artificial intelligence technology and the application of it, and it is also the premise of all artificial intelligence technology and application implementation.

Cloud computing is divided into Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS) according to the level of use.

IaaS: divided into three types: public cloud, private cloud and hybrid cloud. The service provided to consumers is the use of all facilities, including processors, storage, network and other basic computing resources. Users can deploy and run any software. , including operating systems and applications.

PaaS: The software development platform is presented as a service to users in the SaaS model.

SaaS: The service provided to the customer is the application that the operator runs on the cloud computing infrastructure, and the user can access it through the client interface on various devices, such as a browser.

At present, the key players in the domestic cloud computing are Aliyun, Tencent Cloud, Huawei Cloud, Baidu Cloud and so on.

2. Artificial intelligence algorithm platform

Different from the basic technology providing platform, the artificial intelligence technology platform mainly focuses on machine learning, pattern recognition and human-computer interaction. The fields involved include machine vision, fingerprint recognition, face recognition, retina recognition, iris recognition, palmprint recognition, and experts. Systems, automated planning, intelligent search, theorem proving, gaming, automated programming, intelligent control, robotics, language and image understanding, and genetic programming.

Pattern recognition: Pattern recognition is the use of computer mathematical methods to study the automatic processing and interpretation of patterns, which focuses on the processing of non-intuitive data such as signals, images, speech, text, fingerprints, such as speech recognition, face recognition, etc. Achieve certain goals by extracting relevant features. Text recognition, speech recognition, fingerprint recognition and image recognition are all scene applications for pattern recognition.

Human-computer interaction: Human-computer interaction is a study of the interaction between the system and the user. The system can be a wide variety of machines or computerized systems and software. At the application level, it includes both the human-system voice interaction and the physical interaction between the human and the robotic entity.

At present, the domestic artificial intelligence technology platform focuses on computer vision, speech recognition and language technology processing at the application level. Representative enterprises include Keda Xunfei, Geling Deep, Jietong Huasheng (Lingyun), Horizon, SenseTime, Yonghong Technology, Defiance Technology, Yunzhisheng, etc.

3. Artificial intelligence application

Artificial intelligence applications involve both specialized and general aspects, which are the implementation forms of three artificial intelligence technologies, such as machine learning, pattern recognition and human-computer interaction. At present, domestic artificial intelligence applications are in the development stage of transition from professional applications to general applications.

Among them, the application in the special field covers most of the current domestic artificial intelligence applications, including face recognition, speech recognition and service robots; while the general field applications focus on medical, smart home, finance and so on.
