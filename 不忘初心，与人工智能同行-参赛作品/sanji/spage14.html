The biggest challenge in sports events: clarity and fluency

In the past, viewers only watched the World Cup on TV. It was very small when the picture was stuck or unclear. However, the live webcast is not as stable as the live broadcast of the TV. Due to factors such as network bandwidth, the probability of picture cardon, audio and video timestamps being out of sync, etc. is high.

Usually, in order to ensure smooth viewing of the video, the user has to lower the video bit rate and select a smooth or standard definition picture to watch the video. However, for a heavyweight event like the World Cup, this is obviously not the result of the audience and the broadcaster. In fact, video is the most bandwidth-intensive content form on the Internet. Realizing the clarity and smoothness of video high-frequency motion pictures has always been the biggest challenge for Internet sports events.

So, how can we ensure that users watch the quality of the live broadcast and ensure the clarity of the picture? How does Tencent Cloud do it?

At the recent Tencent Cloud Media Salon, Li Haiqi, senior product manager of Tencent Cloud, said: "For video companies, bandwidth spending accounts for a large part of operating costs. If you want to provide users with high-definition video experience, you must increase the video stream. The code rate and the total cost of the corresponding bandwidth will be further improved. Tencent Cloud Extreme HD is solving this problem and can provide users with higher definition video at a lower bit rate."

The technology behind CCTV World Cup live broadcast - Tencent Cloud Extreme HD

Li Haiqi told reporters that there are many quality problems in ordinary transcoding technology, such as transcoding distortion, low resolution blur, lens jitter, loud noise, low bit rate sawtooth block and so on. These are some of the basic issues to deal with during the entire transcoding process.

Tencent Cloud Extreme Speed ​​HD technology (also known as "Intelligent Dynamic Coding Technology") mainly uses machine depth learning, intelligent classification of video scenes, coding parameter matching, pre-processing, coding dynamic optimization, code rate intelligent control, detail reduce, ROI processing. Such technologies and processes intelligently process live or on-demand video streams to match optimal coding parameters, thereby providing users with a higher-definition live video stream at a lower bandwidth cost.

The technology will classify the real-time recognition results according to the scene, and combine the video source rate, frame rate, resolution, texture and motion variation, and the dimensions of the integrated machine load and image quality to select the optimal coding parameters. At the same time, in the image quality processing, it can be classified according to different scenes, combined with video source picture texture and real-time motion detection results to sharpen, softly blur, de-interlace, deblock, denoise, color scale compensation, frame down, etc. Set processing.

According to reports, Tencent Cloud Extreme Speed ​​HD technology mainly realizes intelligent coding of live video stream through three modules, namely: scene intelligent recognition, video dynamic coding and video enhancement.

In the aspect of intelligent recognition of the scene, during the high-speed HD technology service, the live stream can be detected and analyzed in real time, and the corresponding scene model is matched. According to the video stream situation to identify the scene of the live video, whether it is a sporting event, if it is, football, basketball or tennis. At the same time, smart recognition is also adopted for a single scene. If it is a football match, it is necessary to identify in real time whether it is a close shot, whether it is a distant view, whether it is a shot or the like. Then, the real-time recognition result is classified according to the scene, and the video is dynamically encoded. Match the current base code library first, then match the next more detailed code base according to the real-time motion scene. Finally, implement video enhancements. The key areas of concern to the audience have been enhanced and the non-key areas have been weakened. For example, the audience area and the billboard area can be weakened.

Why does CCTV choose Tencent Cloud?

As the most important customer in the entire video cloud industry, CCTV is a must for all cloud vendors. Before the World Cup, CCTV decided to introduce new technologies to enhance the clarity of the game during the World Cup. Each cloud service provider provided its own solution.

Tencent Cloud has carried out dozens of service upgrades and optimizations in modules such as rate control, deblurring, deblocking, de-interlacing, motion search and block decision. After a round of PK, CCTV finally chose Tencent Cloud. With a resolution of 1024*576 and a frame rate of 25, Tencent Cloud Extreme HD technology achieves a lower bit rate of 887 kbps, and the remaining two cloud service vendors have a code rate of 963 kbps and 941 kbps. The VMAF model is used to evaluate video quality. Tencent's cloud code rate is about 8%-10% less than that of friends.
