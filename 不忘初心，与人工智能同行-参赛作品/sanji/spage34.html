Artificial Intelligence in the Eyes of Xi Jinping

According to relevant reports, the scale of China's artificial intelligence core industry exceeded 70 billion yuan in 2017. It is estimated that by 2020, China's artificial intelligence core industry will exceed 160 billion yuan, with a growth rate of 26.2%. In the foreseeable future, the development of artificial intelligence The scale may be more than expected.

Why develop artificial intelligence? How to develop artificial intelligence? Since the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, General Secretary Xi Jinping has placed innovation at the core of the country's overall development and attached great importance to technological innovation. At the same time, he has repeatedly talked about the importance of artificial intelligence and the development path of artificial intelligence on different occasions.

Why develop artificial intelligence?

The International Robotics Federation predicts that the "robot revolution" will create a trillion-dollar market. As the integration of next-generation information technology such as big data, cloud computing, and mobile Internet with robotics is accelerating, 3D printing and artificial intelligence are developing rapidly, the hardware and software technologies for manufacturing robots are becoming more mature, the cost is continuously reduced, and the performance is continuously improved. Machines, autonomous vehicles, and housekeeping service robots have become a reality, and some artificial intelligence robots have a considerable degree of independent thinking and learning ability.

General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out at the 2014 academician meeting of the two academies that the "robot revolution" will create a trillion-dollar market. The robot is the “Pearl of the Top of the Manufacturing Industry”, and its R&D, manufacturing and application are important indicators for measuring the level of technological innovation and high-end manufacturing in a country. Major manufacturers and countries of the robot have stepped up their efforts to seize the technical and market commanding heights. We must not only raise the level of our robots, but also occupy the market as much as possible. There are still many new technologies and new fields. We must review the situation, consider the whole situation, grasp the plan, and push forward.

At the opening ceremony of the 2016 G20 Hangzhou Summit, Xi Jinping pointed out that a new round of technology and industrial revolution with the Internet as the core is poised for growth, new technologies such as artificial intelligence and virtual reality are changing with each passing day, and the combination of virtual economy and real economy will give People's production methods and lifestyles have revolutionized.

At the high-level dialogue between the Chinese Communist Party and the world political parties in 2017, Xi Jinping further emphasized the importance of artificial intelligence. "Today, the Internet, big data, cloud computing, quantum satellites, artificial intelligence are developing rapidly, and the connection of human life is unprecedented."

How to develop artificial intelligence?

China's economy has shifted from a high-speed growth stage to a high-quality development stage. It is in the process of transforming the development mode, optimizing the economic structure, and transforming the growth momentum. Building a modern economic system is an urgent requirement for crossing the border and a strategic goal for China's development. To build a modern economic system, we must take supply-side structural reform as the main line, and the key point of supply-side reform lies in developing advanced manufacturing and cultivating new economic growth points.

How to cultivate a new economy? How to develop artificial intelligence? Deep integration with the real economy has become an important path.

On November 10, 2017, Xi Jinping pointed out at the APEC CEO Summit that we will promote the in-depth integration of the Internet, big data, artificial intelligence and the real economy, and foster new growth momentum in the fields of digital economy, shared economy and clean energy. .

In the same year, the fourth World Internet Conference, Xi Jinping also proposed in the congratulatory letter "to promote the deep integration of the Internet, big data, artificial intelligence and the real economy, develop the digital economy, share the economy, foster new growth points, and form new kinetic energy."

From April 20 to 21, 2018, Xi Jinping once again emphasized the importance of integration with the real economy when attending the National Cyber ​​Security and Informatization Work Conference. "To promote the deep integration of the Internet, big data, artificial intelligence and the real economy, and accelerate Digitalization, networking, and intelligence in manufacturing, agriculture, and service industries."

In fact, the integration of artificial intelligence and the real economy in China has gradually deepened in recent years. An intelligent service robot called “Little Way” can not only independently and efficiently provide community residents with senior citizenship allowances, residence certificates, unemployed certificates and other hot items, but also conducts inquiries such as service guides, health, and Business consulting in various fields such as pensions and policies.

A WiFi transmitting device, a WiFi receiving device, can monitor the respiratory rate in real time, whether it is sitting or lying down, tracking the sleep state, and finding abnormal conditions such as respiratory arrest and respiratory disorder.

The combination of drones and infrared thermal imaging technology can also monitor the normality of urban pipelines based on data comparison.

According to the artificial intelligence innovations that the reporter saw at the second session of the World Intelligence Conference, it fully demonstrated the successful commercialization of China's new driving artificial intelligence.
