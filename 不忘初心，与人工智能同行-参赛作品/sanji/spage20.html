Japanese scholars put two feet on the drone, and jumped MJ to sign the moonwalk

The little mermaid drank the potion given by the witch, and thus gained the legs, and the drone had a pair of slender white legs under the brain of Japanese scholars; this drone with a leg can make The moon walks and shows a light dance, which is very flexible compared to the existing biped robot.

According to IEEE Spectrum, researchers at the University of Tokyo in Japan recently published a "two-legged drone" called Air-Biped, which moves the legs through the four propellers of the drone. Get rid of the constraints of gravity and make more action.

The inspiration for the "two-legged drone", according to the researchers, is derived from the 2016 BALLU robot that was released by Los Angeles. The BALLU consists of balloons and wire-like legs that reduce the legs through the buoyancy of the internal gas. Burden to make the movement smoother.

When you see this drone that is full of questions, you may think "What is the use of these legs?" Frankly speaking, it is really useless. Although the servo motor is powered by the joints of the legs, the speed of the propeller can be detected, and the machine learning is used to simulate the human walking movement. However, it is only "made" and does not support the weight of the drone.

The researchers said that this bipedal UAV is not a practical orientation, although it is still in its infancy, but it is planned to be used in entertainment fields such as performances and robotics (Animatronics) in the future, using its slender legs to dance. In addition, scholars believe that this research can free the robot from the physical limitations of the machine structure, and has the potential to reproduce the unique experience in the VR world in reality.
