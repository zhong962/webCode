Baidu Wang Haifeng: Multimodal deep semantic understanding will let AI understand the real world more deeply

Baidu AI Developers Conference (Baidu Create 2018) held a while ago, Bai Hai Senior Vice President, AI Technology Platform System (AIG) Chief Executive Wang Haifeng released Baidu Brain 3.0, and pointed out that the core of Baidu Brain 3.0 is "multimodal" "Deep semantic understanding" includes the semantics of data, the semantics of knowledge, and the understanding of images, video, sound, and speech.

How does multimodal depth semantic understanding be implemented technically? How much energy do you have? How will it be applied to change our lives? In the Baidu Brain Forum on the afternoon of the same day, the core technology of Baidu Brain 3.0 “shows” one by one, bringing a technical feast to developers.

The venue for 1,000 people was packed, the developers even sat on the ground, or stood at the venue and finally listened to the full field. One developer said, “There are too many dry goods and too little time. Go back and look for the playback video to digest it.”

From "seeing clearly and clearing" to "understanding and understanding"

"Multi-modal depth semantic understanding enables the machine to understand and understand clearly, to better understand the meaning behind it, to deeply understand the real world, and to better support various applications." Wang Haifeng said.

Visual semantics allows the machine to understand the video from the point of view and extract the structured semantic knowledge. Wu Zhongqin, director of Baidu's visual technology department, face technology department and augmented reality technology department, introduced that visual semantic technology first recognizes people, objects and scenes, and captures the behaviors and relationships between them through time series, digitization and structure. Forming semantic knowledge, and finally combining intelligent fields with fields and scenarios, and applying industry applications. For example, in the retail field, it can accurately determine the actions and behaviors of each person, and truly realize that customers pick up the goods and leave. In the future, visual semantic technology can be further extended. It combines new sensors and AI chips, which can be greatly improved at the perceptual level and computing level; combined with mobile phones can bring users a better experience. In the future, Baidu's visual semantic technology will be open to developers in the Baidu AI open platform.

The upgrade of voice technology allows the machine to better understand the world. Gao Liang, director of Baidu's voice technology department, said that Baidu's far-field-based voice and semantic integration technology has made a major breakthrough, providing the industry with a more advanced far-field voice technology. Speech semantic integration improves the accuracy of high-frequency Query recognition in far-field interaction by 10 points, and keeps the normal Query recognition rate from falling. Multi-language hybrid acoustic modeling based on Deep Peak2 greatly improves the accuracy of Chinese and English mixed Query recognition, relative error rate. Compared with the best competing products in the industry, it is reduced by 20%. The newly upgraded TTS technology industry is the first in the traditional splicing technology and the Wavenet technology integration solution to ensure the quality of the synthesis while greatly reducing the cost, making large-scale application landing become a reality. In addition, Baidu has launched a low-cost solution for far-field voice technology, "Diao Xiaoyun", which is based on Deep Peak V2 speech recognition technology, voice semantic integration technology and LSTM-VAD deep learning voice segmentation technology to achieve industry-leading five. Level-awake technology and a sophisticated sound library based on Wavenet, supporting far-field optimized audio communication technology. In the future, developers can access Baidu's far-field voice capabilities in an equal and convenient manner.

To make a machine as intelligent as a human being, it is indispensable to turn a language into knowledge. Wu Tian, ​​executive director of Baidu's AI technology platform system, said that after years of development, Baidu's natural language processing technology has formed a comprehensive and cutting-edge pattern. Baidu knowledge map has developed into a complex, multi-dimensional and comprehensive multi-semantic knowledge map, including entity map, industry map, event map, focus map, multimedia map, in which the entity map can cover 90% of the entities and their attributes in the general requirements. . In reading comprehension technology, Baidu brain has read hundreds of billions of articles, equivalent to the collection of 60,000 Chinese National Libraries, and thus accumulated knowledge of billions of entities, hundreds of billions of facts, and through "learning" The knowledge generated by real-world applications is constantly optimized. In terms of dialogue understanding, Baidu understands and upgrades the technology platform UNIT to 2.0, further enhances the cold start capability, supports learning in the dialogue like humans, and opens the first industrial-level dialogue system open source framework, lowers the building threshold and allows development. Seamlessly dock the cloud. Baidu will continue to open source new technologies, provide more flexible and convenient solutions, and promote the innovation and development of natural language understanding technology.

Consolidate the foundation: data, algorithms, and computing power go hand in hand

Data, algorithms, and computing power are important factors influencing the development of artificial intelligence technology. In the main forum, Wang Haifeng said that Baidu Brain 3.0 first incorporated the chip into the technical system, which drove the explosive growth of Baidu's brain. Baidu's self-developed China's first cloud full-featured AI chip "Kunlun" also made its debut at the conference. In the future, AI chips will be combined with Baidu's self-developed PaddlePaddle deep learning framework to promote the rapid development of the AI ​​industry.

Baidu's official open source PaddlePaddle in 2016 is the most suitable deep learning framework for Chinese developers. In the main forum, Wang Haifeng released PaddlePaddle3.0. In the afternoon, Yu Youping, general manager of Baidu AI Technology and Ecology Department, explained this in detail. PaddlePaddle 3.0 includes a complete core framework, as well as AI Studio, AutoDL, EasyDL and other platforms that give developers equal access to top AI capabilities. PaddlePaddle3.0 core framework is fully optimized for server version and mobile version. PaddlePaddle Fluid provides a rich API to meet the needs of common mainstream model construction, supports a wide range of model building, training Runtime can meet various types of model training needs; PaddlePaddle Serving Flexibility to adapt to multiple forecasting engines; PaddlePaddle Mobile integrates Baidu's mobile-side forecasting experience and provides multi-platform support.

In PaddlePaddle 3.0, AutoDL has received much attention. Bai Jun, director of Baidu Big Data (Beijing) Lab, said that AutoDL can search the neural network structure more efficiently and automatically, and developers can quickly get high-quality models without special hardware equipment. AutoDL offers a variety of capabilities to support the design of new deep learning network structures, as well as to optimize existing deep learning network structures and parameters while adapting to specific mission scenarios.
