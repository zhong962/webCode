Guilin University of Electronic Technology

  • Guilin university of electronic science and technology is located in the world famous scenic city and Chinese historical and cultural city of guilin. It is a university jointly built by the ministry of industry and information technology and guangxi zhuang autonomous region.

    Founded in 1960, the university was established in 1980 with the approval of the state council, and renamed guilin university of electronic science and technology in 2006.The school has been affiliated to the fourth machinery industry department, the ministry of electronic industry, the ministry of mechanical and electronic industry, China electronic industry corporation, and the ministry of information industry.In 2000, the management system was transformed into a system of joint construction between the central government and local governments, with local management as the main focus.In 1990, jiang zemin, then general secretary of the CPC central committee, visited the school and wrote an inscription for the school, "cultivate more qualified talents for the development of the electronic industry".

Guilin university of electronic science and technology establishes artificial intelligence college

On July 13, our school formally established the artificial intelligence college.The establishment of the institute, is aimed at around "a new generation of artificial intelligence development plan" and artificial intelligence in guangxi industry layout, further integrate campus academic resources, gathering scientific research resources at home and abroad, establish the direction of the artificial intelligence personnel training system, oriented to the future of artificial intelligence talents, explore new engineering talents cultivation, our school in emerging discipline construction mechanism and so on to carry out the reform and innovation, support future industry's basic theory and common key technologies of artificial intelligence research.

It is reported that the artificial intelligence institute is also one of the construction contents of guilin hujiang huigu electronic information entrepreneurship industrial park.At present, the construction plans of ai college, including talent cultivation, faculty construction, discipline and scientific research construction, administrative structure, institute system and mechanism, and school-running investment estimation have been determined. The next step is to build a model college for scientific and technological innovation, talent cultivation and technology application in the field of ai in guangxi according to specific plans.The college of artificial intelligence plans to set up majors such as artificial intelligence, robot engineering, intelligent science and technology.
