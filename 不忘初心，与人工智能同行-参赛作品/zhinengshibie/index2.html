Intelligent Identification

  • People living in modern society have added a lot of trouble that they did not have in the past, and the various passwords that they often forget are one of them.If you use a simple password, it's easy to remember but it's obviously less secure;If you use different meaningless passwords, you'll often forget them even though they're secure.So what do we do?The following is the related knowledge of intelligent identification technology.The so-called intelligent identification technology, also known as automatic identification technology, automatically obtains the related information of the identified object through the interaction between the identified object and the recognition device, and provides it to the computer system for further processing.

Image Recognition

Image recognition technology is an important field of artificial intelligence.It refers to the object recognition of images to identify different patterns of objects and image technology.In the process of image recognition, the perceptual mechanism must exclude the redundant information input and extract the key information.At the same time, there must be a mechanism in the brain responsible for integrating information, which can organize the information acquired in stages into a complete perceptual map.

There are many methods of image segmentation, such as threshold segmentation, edge detection, region extraction and segmentation based on specific theoretical tools.The types of images can be divided into gray image segmentation, color image segmentation and texture image segmentation.

Speech Recognition

Speech recognition technology is a technology that enables machines to transform human speech signals into corresponding text or commands through the recognition and understanding process, belonging to the category of multi-dimensional pattern recognition and intelligent computer interface.

Talking to a machine and getting it to understand what you're saying is something that people have been dreaming of for a long time.The Chinese Internet of things (iot) alliance aims to compare speech recognition to the "machine's hearing system".

The application field of speech recognition is very wide. Common application systems include: voice input system, voice control system, intelligent dialogue query system and so on.

Biometric Recognition

Fingerprint identification technology

Fingerprint recognition is to identify the details by comparing different fingerprint features. Fingerprint recognition technology involves image processing, pattern recognition, computer vision, mathematical morphology, wavelet analysis and many other disciplines. Because each person's fingerprints are different, that is, between the same person's ten fingers, fingerprints also have obvious differences, so fingerprints can be used for identification. Because the orientation of each printing is not exactly the same, different focus will bring varying degrees of deformation.

Palm-print recognition technology

Palm-print recognition is a new biometric recognition technology in recent years.Palmprint is an image of the palm of the hand from the end of the finger to the wrist.Many of these features can be used for identification: mainline, wrinkles, fine texture, ridge tip, bifurcation point, etc.Palm-print recognition system is the same in structure as other biometric recognition systems, but palm-print recognition is a non-invasive method compared with fingerprint recognition, which is more acceptable to users and less demanding for acquisition equipment.

Face recognition technology

Face recognition is a hot research field of computer technology. It belongs to the biometric recognition technology, which differentiates the biological characteristics of organisms (generally referring to human beings).The generalized face recognition actually includes a series of related technologies for constructing face recognition system, including face image acquisition, face location, face recognition preprocessing, identity verification and identity search.In the narrow sense, face recognition refers to the technology or system of identifying or finding identity through face.

Iris recognition technology

Iris recognition is an eye-related biometric technology that has less interference with humans.It USES fairly ordinary camera components and does not require user contact with the machine.In addition, it has the ability to achieve higher template matching performance.Therefore, it attracts the attention of all kinds of people.After the development of the fetus, the iris will remain unchanged throughout life.The uniqueness of iris features also determines the uniqueness of identity recognition.Therefore, the iris feature of the eye can be used as an identification object for everyone.
