More than 75% of smartphones have fingerprint recognition in 2020, and mobile payment has become popular.

Nowadays, fingerprint recognition has become the basic equipment for mid-range and high-end smartphones. Last year, although Apple's flagship model iPhone X did not use a fingerprint sensor and switched to face recognition, it has a smartphone with a fingerprint sensor. Shipments still reached 920 million, and the penetration rate is about 64%. It is estimated that the demand for smartphone shipments will increase year by year and mobile payment will increase. In 2020, the shipment of fingerprint sensor smartphones will reach 1.25 billion, penetration rate. Will exceed 75%.

According to DIGITIMES Research, Fingerprint Cards (FPC), a fingerprint sensor manufacturer, is a Swedish startup. Due to the business opportunities of fingerprint sensing technology, the company's revenue in 2016 has grown more than 650 times compared with 2013, but its rapid growth has also attracted the United States. In mainland China and Taiwan, manufacturers are actively investing. Under the circumstances of the sudden drop in average selling price (ASP) and the increase in competitors, it is estimated that FPC revenue in 2017 will drop by 53% compared with 2016, and the operating profit rate will also be 31% in 2015 and 2016. And 39% of the super high level, plummeted to the estimated 6%-7% in 2017.

In contrast, Goodix, after turning its main products into fingerprint sensors, its revenue in 2016 has grown more than four times compared with 2013. Although many manufacturers have joined the war in 2017, the annual revenue forecast can still grow by 25%. From the perspective of revenue, it has surpassed FPC and become the world's largest supplier of fingerprint recognition sensors.

According to DIGITIMES Research, because fingerprint recognition is the most cost-effective way to identify biometrics, it is not just for mobile phones, but for future applications in tablets, laptops, cars, government and corporate access, and even smart card applications with payment functions.
