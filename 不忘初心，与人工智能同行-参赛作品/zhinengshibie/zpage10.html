Virtual personal assistant gives people a smart life

Everyone wants to have a caring personal assistant to help with all kinds of chores. In many sci-fi movies, there have been scenes of virtual personal assistant services entering human life. In the era of mobile Internet, the demand for assistant services is highlighted. The continuous development of information and communication technology is gradually breaking through the imagination of human beings. People are using new technical means to obtain "good secretary" or "good housekeeping" services. In reality, more and more "non-human" assistants have emerged around people to help with day-to-day affairs.

A virtual personal assistant is a powerful software application that has a deep understanding of people's needs. It can replace people in performing certain tasks – by integrating these services in some way to optimally meet people's needs.

The virtual personal assistant provides assistant services to people in three steps: receiving information, analyzing searches, and performing tasks. The virtual personal assistant “discovers” people's needs through human orders or according to people's itinerary, habits and other monitoring data, and then through comprehensive analysis, searches for “services” that best meet people's needs, and then performs tasks for people.

Virtual personal assistants collect and record data about individuals and families to provide effective services. According to the results of data accumulation, that is, people's habits, preferences, and needs, appropriate services are provided for people. The virtual personal assistant cyclically applies relevant data and adjusts the habits, preferences and demand records of people according to changes in the environment, personal and family life status, and dynamically optimizes the “intelligent” personal assistant service. Virtual personal assistants can also become a "control center" according to people's wishes, and arrange people's lives in all aspects. Virtual personal assistants can be a key node for human beings to connect with everything, and humans will be freed from chores and choices. Evolving artificial intelligence will make virtual personal assistants stronger, and excellent virtual personal assistants can even learn some of the complex “soft skills” that humans have, not only helping people with daily affairs, but also helping people make vital things. decision.

Nowadays, artificial intelligence has been promoted and used in more and more industries, and it has become an important scientific and technological means to improve people's quality of life. The virtual personal assistant will be the core product of the "artificial intelligence +" era of Internet applications. A variety of mobile applications make smartphones a “universal machine”. With the "clouding" of mobile applications, people will not need to download any mobile applications, just install a virtual personal assistant. It will do a good job for everyone, and provide more practical services by connecting to the cloud mobile app. The virtual personal assistant combines with the TV set-top box to convert people from "watching TV" mode to "using TV" mode. People can use voice to talk to virtual personal assistants, and enjoy practical smart services such as finding videos, searching for information, on-demand, weather inquiries, and appointment reminders. The virtual personal assistant will act as a search engine, searching for people, and people will no longer "look at the vast amount of information." Virtual personal assistants will create a more powerful world of data networks and become a super-entry to the future, truly changing people's lives. Future virtual personal assistants will be smarter and understand and answer questions like a real-life assistant. Because it is based on the cloud, it can be continuously learned and updated through a huge database, and by analyzing relevant data, it analyzes various problems faced by people and provides solutions. Future virtual personal assistants will be more privately tailored to provide more targeted advice for different people, as well as for a variety of mobile phones, tablets or wearables.

The future development of virtual personal assistants is promising, but there are still many problems that need to be solved and solved. The current bottlenecks mainly include artificial intelligence algorithms such as basic data collection, opening and natural language processing, and automatic reasoning. Of course, the promotion and application of virtual personal assistants, in addition to their own development breakthroughs, depends on the development of related technologies such as Internet of Things and artificial intelligence.
