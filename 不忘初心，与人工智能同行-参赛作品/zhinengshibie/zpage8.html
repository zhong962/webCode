20 players entered the game, machine vision ushered in the "hundred schools of thought" era

Since 2016, machine vision and collaborative robots have become hot spots due to the intelligent upgrade of robots. However, in the first half of 2018, cooperative robots have obviously a tendency to weaken, but machine vision has been heating up all the way. At present, machine vision companies with large amounts of financing are looking for a quick landing, and in the future, how many fresh “faces” will emerge in this emerging field? Many people in the industry have shouted that the machine vision has arrived, and will break out in the next 3-5 years.

Capital boosts a hundred schools of thought

If the industrial robot industry or the industrial robot-related enterprises, which type of enterprise financing the most, the most frequent, non-machine vision enterprises. From 2017 to the first half of 2018, according to incomplete statistics, there were nearly 20 machine vision enterprise financing events, and in the previous communication with investors, they also expressed interest in machine vision enterprises.

From January 2018 to the present, 11 companies focused on machine vision, including Aqiu Technology, Elegance Vision, Vision Technology, Lipper Technology, and High Vision Technology, have received financing from 8 million to 100 million yuan. Among them, in the field of industrial machine vision: In January, Aqiu Technology completed a $8 million Series A financing; at the end of February, Elegance Vision received tens of millions of Pre-A rounds of financing; in early July, Lipper Technology completed 50 million A+ rounds of financing In mid-July, Gaoshi Technology won 50 million A+ rounds of financing.

At present, China is becoming one of the most active regions in the world for machine vision development. It is the third largest machine vision market in the world after the United States and Japan, and its application covers almost all industries of the national economy. As the processing center of the global manufacturing industry, the industrial field is the largest proportion of machine vision applications, and many internationally advanced machine vision systems and application experience have also entered China.

With the help of capital, China's machine vision market has accelerated its fermentation, and it has formed a situation of "hundred schools of thought." According to the survey of China Machine Vision Industry Alliance (CMVU), there are nearly 200 international machine vision brands that have entered China. There are more than 100 machine vision brands in China, and machine vision products have surpassed all kinds of agents. There are more than 300 professional machine vision system integrators.

In this context, machine vision hardware equipment suppliers represented by Hikvision, Huarui Technology, Cognex, OPT, etc. are also stepping up the layout of the industrial market, with Cooper, Mecamand, The machine vision professional solution providers represented by Aqiu Technology and others, in the context of clarifying their respective technological advantages, are accelerating their landing in various sub-segments in combination with process characteristics.

Logistics sorting

At present, industrial robots can only complete the capture of fixed-mode objects, and more than 90% of the identification and sorting of complex stacked objects are done manually, which greatly limits the efficiency of industrial production.

Founded in 2016, Mecamand completed three rounds of financing in just one year. Cooper, also established in 2016, also completed the third round of financing at the end of last year. Coincidentally, both companies are not. Order sorting field layout.

Mecamand uses cutting-edge technologies such as deep learning, 3D vision and intelligent path planning to introduce a variety of commodity classification systems and a wide variety of goods classification systems to support a wide range of goods of different sizes and types, as well as for reflective, Dark, with a complex pattern of goods, the goods can be closely fitted, or can be placed at will.

Cooper's mushroom intelligent classification system is currently the only food-grade sorting system in China. This system can identify 26 kinds of mushrooms, and the picking speed can reach 0.8s/piece, and the precision rate can reach 99.7%.

In addition to Mecamand and Cooper, Entropy Technology also uses its 3D vision and autonomous path planning techniques for visual sorting of robots; and for 3D recognition and positioning of scattered and disorderly stacked workpieces, Elson also launched The AT-D100 logistics 3D intelligent sorting and positioning system; in addition to the visual inspection system, another major business line of Aqiu Technology is the intelligent sorting system of 3D vision.

In the specific application scenarios, the logistics industry has become the focus of competition among enterprises. Cooper said that its mushroom intelligent classification system can also be applied in the logistics industry; currently, Mecamand has already cooperated with some logistics and robotic ontology companies. Many projects are also being shipped through some robotics and logistics equipment integrators.

As early as last year, the Elson AT-S1000 robot 3D positioning system has been applied to Jingdong's “unmanned warehouse”. At present, the projects that Enqizhi Technology has already implemented are mainly cooperating with some well-known daily chemical, logistics and e-commerce companies.
