Overview of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence, short for Al.It is a new technical science to study and develop the theory, method, technology and application system for simulating, extending and extending human intelligence.Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science that seeks to understand the essence of intelligence and produce a new kind of intelligent machine that can respond in a similar way to human intelligence, with research in the field including robotics, language recognition, image recognition, natural language processing and expert systems.Professor nelson of the center for artificial intelligence research at Stanford university made such a definition of artificial intelligence: "artificial intelligence is a discipline of knowledge -- the science of how to represent knowledge and how to obtain and use it.

Historical Origin of Artificial Intelligence

  • Embryo

    The idea of artificial intelligence dates back to the 17th century in busca and leibniz, who came up with the idea of intelligent machines.In the 19th century, British mathematicians Boer and demergen proposed the "law of thought", which is the beginning of artificial intelligence.In the 1920s, British scientist babbage designed the first "computing machine", which is considered to be the computer hardware and the predecessor of artificial intelligence hardware.The advent of electronic computers has made artificial intelligence research truly possible.

  • Development

    The 1950s and 1970s were the "age of reasoning" for artificial intelligence.In this period, it is generally believed that artificial intelligence can be realized as long as the machine is endowed with logical reasoning ability.Since then, however, it has been found that machines are far from intelligent, only equipped with logical reasoning.1970-1990, the era of "knowledge engineering" of artificial intelligence.During this period, people thought that to make machines intelligent, they should try to make machines learn knowledge, so expert systems were developed a lot.From 2000 to present, the "data mining" era of artificial intelligence.During this period, with the improvement of computer hardware and the development of big data analysis technology, the level of machine collection, storage and processing of data has been greatly improved.

The Development Stage of Artificial Intelligence

First stage

In the 1950s, after the concept of artificial intelligence was first proposed, a number of remarkable achievements emerged, such as machine theorem proving.

Sencond stage

From the late 1960s to the 1970s, the emergence of expert systems led to a new climax in the study of artificial intelligence.

Third stage

In the 1980s, artificial intelligence developed greatly.In 1982, Japan launched the "fifth generation computer development program", which aims to make logical reasoning.

Fourth stage

In the late 1980s, neural networks developed rapidly.In 1987, the first international conference on neural networks was held in the United States.

Fifth stage

In the 1990s, a new research climax of artificial intelligence emerged. Due to the development of network technology.

Sixth stage

With the maturity of Internet technology, the improvement of artificial intelligence support technologies such as big data and cloud computing.

The Development Dtatus of Artificial Intelligence

Research status of intelligent interface technology

Artificial intelligence interface research is to realize human-computer communication. Therefore, scholars must make efforts from both theoretical and practical aspects to solve such functional problems as computer's understanding and translation of words and languages and self-expression.As a result of the research and application of intelligent interface technology, the development of computer technology has obtained a great impetus.

Research status of data mining technology

Data mining technology mainly collects and mines all kinds of fuzzy, large amount of applied data, unknown and potential existing data, and conducts detailed research to find out useful data for research.At present, database, artificial intelligence and mathematical statistics have become the three major technical supports of data mining technology, taking basic theory, discovery algorithm, visualization technology and knowledge representation method as research contents.

Status of subject system research

The principal system can realize the generation of machine intention and idea, which is an intelligent entity system closer to human.The autonomous system can accomplish some relatively independent and autonomous tasks. It can even adjust its own state to cope with changes in the environment and special circumstances so as to ensure the completion of its own planning tasks.In the study of multi-agent system, the intelligent behavior of the subject is mainly analyzed from the aspects of physical and logical thinking.

Industry market development status

According to data from the market outlook and investment strategic planning analysis report of China's ai industry released by the forward-looking industrial research institute, the scale of China's ai industry will be further expanded and more industrial and consumer application products will emerge.In the future, "" ai +" "is expected to become a new business mode, and talent reserve will become an important factor that restricts the development of ai in China.
